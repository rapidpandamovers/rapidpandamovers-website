## Key Tips for Safe Moving in April

### Plan Ahead for Peak Times

April can be a busy time for moving companies. With Easter activities and seasonal transitions, scheduling your safe moving early ensures you get your preferred date and time. Many Miami residents also take advantage of spring break to relocate, making weekdays in mid-April particularly popular. If you're moving in neighborhoods like Coral Gables, Brickell, or Coconut Grove, consider booking at least two weeks in advance.

### Know Your Safe's Weight and Dimensions

Before the crew arrives, know exactly what they're working with. Check your safe's manual or manufacturer website for the weight. Gun safes typically range from 200 to over 1,000 pounds. Measure doorways, stairs, and hallways to ensure the safe can pass through. This information helps our team bring the right equipment, whether that's a heavy-duty appliance dolly, stair climber, or rigging gear for larger units.

### Consider the Weather

South Florida's spring weather is predictable in some ways and surprising in others. April in Miami typically sees temperatures in the mid-80s with humidity levels climbing as we approach summer. Afternoon thunderstorms can roll in quickly from the Everglades, especially between 2-5 PM. Professional movers know how to protect your belongings from humidity, rain, and heat exposure during transport. They also plan routes that avoid flood-prone areas like parts of Little Havana and low-lying sections near Biscayne Bay.

### Prepare Your Belongings

Take time to inventory your items before the move. This is especially important for safe moving, as proper documentation helps ensure everything arrives safely at your new location.
