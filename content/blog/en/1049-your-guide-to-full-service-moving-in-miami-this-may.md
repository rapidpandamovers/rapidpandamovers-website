## Why Full-Service Moving Matters in May

Late spring presents specific considerations for anyone planning a move. Miami's wet season begins in May, bringing afternoon thunderstorms that typically roll in between 3pm and 6pm. Humidity starts climbing, and scheduling around these weather patterns can make the difference between an easy transition and a stressful experience.

## Key Tips for Full-Service Moving in May

### Plan Ahead for Peak Times

May starts the busy moving season in South Florida. With end-of-school-year relocations and lease turnovers, scheduling your full-service move early ensures you get your preferred date and time.

### Create a priority list for which rooms to unpack first

This single step can save hours of stress and potential problems on moving day. Our experienced crews have seen what happens when this step is skipped.

### Consider the Weather

South Florida's wet season starts in May, bringing daily afternoon thunderstorms and rising humidity. Professional movers know how to protect your belongings from moisture and heat exposure during transport. Schedule loading for early morning to beat the weather.

### Prepare Your Belongings

Take time to inventory your items before the move. This is especially important for full-service moving, as proper documentation helps ensure everything arrives safely at your new location.
