# 9 Things Cluttering Your Home That Have Resale Value

Before you throw everything into a junk pile, take a closer look. Some of the items collecting dust in your Miami home are worth real money on the resale market. Facebook Marketplace, OfferUp, and Craigslist move items fast in South Florida, and you might be sitting on a few hundred dollars (or more) without realizing it.

## 1. Power Tools

That drill, circular saw, or pressure washer in the garage holds its value well. Brand-name power tools from DeWalt, Makita, and Milwaukee sell for 40% to 60% of retail price even when used. If they're sitting in a toolbox untouched, list them. Power tools are consistently among the fastest-selling items on Facebook Marketplace in Miami.

## 2. Mid-Century and Vintage Furniture

Miami has a strong market for vintage and mid-century modern furniture. That dresser, side table, or dining set from the 1960s or 1970s that you inherited or picked up years ago could be worth significantly more than you think. Buyers in Wynwood, the Design District, and Coconut Grove actively hunt for these pieces. Even pieces that need refinishing attract interest.

## 3. Exercise Equipment

Treadmills, stationary bikes, weight sets, and rowing machines are expensive to buy new. Used fitness equipment in working condition sells quickly in the Miami market, where people want home gym setups year-round. A Peloton bike, even an older model, can still fetch several hundred dollars.

## 4. Brand-Name Kitchen Appliances

KitchenAid stand mixers, Vitamix blenders, Breville espresso machines, and Le Creuset cookware retain strong resale value. If you have high-end kitchen items you don't use, someone in Miami will buy them. List them with clear photos and mention the model number for faster sales.

## 5. Outdoor Furniture and Grills

Miami's outdoor lifestyle means patio furniture and grills are always in demand. Teak, wrought iron, and aluminum outdoor sets sell well, even with some weathering. Weber, Traeger, and Big Green Egg grills hold their value particularly well. Clean them up and take photos in good light.

![A Large Pile Of Scrap Metal And Waste At 2](/images/blog/2028/12/9-things-cluttering-your-home-that-have-resale-value/a-large-pile-of-scrap-metal-and-waste-at-2.webp)


## 6. Musical Instruments

Guitars, keyboards, drums, and brass instruments rarely lose significant value if they're in playable condition. Even student-level instruments sell for decent prices because parents in Miami are always looking for affordable options for kids starting music lessons. Higher-end instruments can be worth hundreds or thousands.

## 7. Bicycles

Road bikes, mountain bikes, and even casual cruisers sell quickly in Miami. The cycling culture here supports a strong secondhand market, and name brands like Trek, Specialized, and Cannondale command good prices even after years of use. Beach cruisers and kids' bikes move fast too, especially in neighborhoods near the Rickenbacker Causeway and Key Biscayne.

## 8. Collectibles and Hobby Items

Sports memorabilia, vinyl records, vintage video games, trading cards, and coin collections can be worth far more than their owners realize. Before tossing boxes of "old stuff," spend a few minutes checking prices on eBay's sold listings. That box of baseball cards or stack of first-edition vinyl could fund a nice dinner out.

## 9. Baby and Kids' Gear

High-end strollers (UPPAbaby, Bugaboo, Nuna), car seats that haven't expired, cribs, and children's furniture sell fast in Miami's family-heavy neighborhoods. Parents in Coral Gables, Pinecrest, and Doral are always looking for quality used baby gear. Just verify that car seats haven't been recalled and aren't past their expiration date before listing.

## How to Sell Fast Before a Move

Price items to sell, not to maximize profit. You're on a timeline. Check what comparable items are going for on Facebook Marketplace, price yours 10% to 15% below, and be responsive to messages. Meet buyers at your home for pickup to avoid delivery hassles.

Set a hard deadline: anything that doesn't sell two weeks before your move gets donated or scheduled for [**Junk Removal**](/junk-removal). Don't let unsold items delay your moving plans.
