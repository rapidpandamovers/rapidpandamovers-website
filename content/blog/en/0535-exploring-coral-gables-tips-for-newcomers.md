Welcome to Coral Gables! Known as "The City Beautiful," Coral Gables offers a unique blend of Mediterranean architecture, tree-lined streets, and world-class amenities. Here's what you need to know before making this upscale Miami suburb your new home.

## About Coral Gables

Founded in the 1920s by visionary developer George Merrick, Coral Gables is a meticulously planned city located just southwest of Downtown Miami. With a population of approximately 50,400 residents, this prestigious community features stunning Mediterranean Revival architecture, lush parks, and a thriving business district.

![A Picturesque View Of Palm Trees Along A 3](/images/blog/2026/06/exploring-coral-gables-tips-for-newcomers/a-picturesque-view-of-palm-trees-along-a-3.webp)


### Demographics and Community

- **Population**: Approximately 50,400 (2026 estimate)
- **Median Age**: 39 years
- **Diversity**: Hispanic (56.4%), White (33.2%), Black (3.5%), Asian (3.5%)
- **Median Household Income**: $130,803
- **Education**: 71% of adults hold a bachelor's degree or higher
- **Foreign-Born Residents**: 38.5%

## Neighborhoods to Consider

### Miracle Mile District
The heart of Coral Gables with upscale shopping, dining, and entertainment. Walkable and vibrant.

### Gables Estates
One of the most exclusive waterfront communities in the country, with estates averaging over $20 million.

### Riviera Section
Family-friendly with excellent schools, tree-lined streets, and Mediterranean-style homes.

### Cocoplum
A gated waterfront community with 24-hour security, boat docks, and luxurious single-family homes.

### Crafts Section
Historic neighborhood with bungalow-style homes from the 1920s at more accessible price points.

## Schools and Education

### Top-Rated Public Schools
- **Coral Gables Preparatory Academy**: 76% reading proficiency, 72% math proficiency
- **George Washington Carver Middle School**: 96-100% proficiency in Algebra, Geometry, Biology, and Civics
- **Coral Gables Senior High School**: Ranked #1,931 nationally

### Higher Education
The University of Miami, one of the nation's top research universities, is located in Coral Gables.

## Housing Market

### Home Prices (2025)
- **Median Home Price**: $1.4 million
- **Price per Square Foot**: $682
- **Average Days on Market**: 123 days
- **Entry-Level Condos**: Starting around $200,000
- **Mid-Range Homes**: $1M-$3M (high demand)
- **Ultra-Luxury (Gables Estates)**: Average $21.9 million

## Major Employers

- **University of Miami**: Education, research, and healthcare
- **Baptist Health South Florida**: Leading healthcare provider
- **Lennar Corporation**: Major homebuilding company headquarters
- Various Financial Institutions and Tech Startups

## Transportation

- **Metrorail**: Douglas Road and University stations
- **Coral Gables Trolley**: Free trolley service throughout the city
- **Commute Times**: Downtown Miami (15-20 min), MIA (15 min), Brickell (10-15 min)

## Parks and Recreation

- **Venetian Pool**: Historic spring-fed pool carved from coral rock in 1923
- **Fairchild Tropical Botanic Garden**: 83 acres of tropical gardens
- **Matheson Hammock Park**: Beachside park with atoll pool and marina
- **Granada Golf Course**: Public championship golf

## Arts and Culture

- **Biltmore Hotel**: Iconic 1926 luxury resort
- **Coral Gables Art Cinema**: Premier nonprofit independent cinema
- **Actors' Playhouse**: Award-winning theater on Miracle Mile
- **Books & Books**: Iconic independent bookstore since 1982
