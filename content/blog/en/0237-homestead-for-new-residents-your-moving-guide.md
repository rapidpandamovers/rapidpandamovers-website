Welcome to your June guide for moving to Homestead! Whether you're relocating from within South Florida or making a bigger move this summer, understanding your new community is essential for a successful transition.

## Why Choose Homestead?

Homestead stands out as one of Miami-Dade County's most affordable and spacious communities. Located at the southern tip of the county, it offers a rural feel with urban conveniences. The area is known for its agricultural heritage, proximity to Everglades National Park, and attractions like the Homestead-Miami Speedway and historic Coral Castle.

![American Alligator Basking In Bonita Spr 3](/images/blog/2025/05/homestead-for-new-residents-your-moving-guide/american-alligator-basking-in-bonita-spr-3.webp)


The area attracts families, professionals, and retirees alike, thanks to its quality of life, convenient access to major employment centers, and excellent amenities.

### Location and Accessibility

Homestead sits at the intersection of US-1 and the Florida Turnpike, making it easy to reach Downtown Miami (about 35 miles north) or the Florida Keys. The Homestead Extension of Florida's Turnpike connects directly to I-95. While public transit is limited compared to urban Miami, the Miami-Dade Transit Busway provides express service to the Dadeland area.

### Community and Lifestyle

Homestead residents enjoy a tight-knit community feel. Harris Field Park hosts youth sports leagues, while Losner Park in downtown Homestead is home to the weekly farmers market. The area is famous for Knaus Berry Farm (open seasonally), Robert Is Here fruit stand, and the many u-pick farms in the Redland agricultural district.

## Neighborhoods to Consider

Homestead offers several distinct neighborhoods. The Redland area northwest of downtown features large lots and agricultural zoning, perfect for those wanting space and privacy. Downtown Homestead has been revitalized with local restaurants like Casita Tejas and Royal Palm Grill. The Keys Gate community offers golf course living, while Waterstone is a newer master-planned development with modern amenities.

### Choosing Your Perfect Spot

Consider these factors:

- **Proximity to work and schools**: Factor in your daily commute
- **Local amenities**: Parks, shopping, dining, and entertainment options
- **Property types**: Single-family homes, condos, townhouses, or apartments
- **Community vibe**: Family-oriented, young professional, or mixed demographics

## Moving to Homestead in June

June is an excellent time to consider your move. The summer weather in South Florida provides hot and humid conditions with daily afternoon thunderstorms for the moving process.

### Timing Your Move

When planning your relocation, consider:

- **Best moving days**: Weekdays often offer better availability and rates
- **Weather considerations**: Summer in Miami means hot and humid conditions with daily afternoon thunderstorms
- **Local events**: Check for any street closures or community events that might affect your move

## Essential Services to Locate

As a new Homestead resident, you'll want to find:

- **Healthcare**: Homestead Hospital (Baptist Health) and Urgent Care centers along US-1
- **Schools**: Miami-Dade Public Schools, including South Dade Senior High, plus private options like Sacred Heart
- **Shopping**: Southland Mall, Homestead Pavilion, and Publix and Sedano's grocery stores
- **Recreation**: Homestead Bayfront Park for waterfront access, Everglades Alligator Farm, and Schnebly Redland's Winery
