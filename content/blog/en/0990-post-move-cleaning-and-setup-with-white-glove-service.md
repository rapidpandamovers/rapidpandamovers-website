# Post-Move Cleaning and Setup With White Glove Service

The moving truck has left, the furniture is placed, and the boxes are unpacked. But the house still doesn't feel like home. Packing dust lingers on surfaces, furniture impressions mark the old floors, and the new kitchen needs organizing. White glove post-move cleaning and setup bridges the gap between "moved in" and "settled in," and for Miami homeowners juggling busy schedules, it's one of the most valuable parts of the service.

## What Post-Move Cleaning Covers

White glove cleaning goes beyond running a vacuum. The crew wipes down all surfaces that collected dust during unpacking, including countertops, shelves, windowsills, and cabinet interiors. Kitchen cabinets and drawers get cleaned before dishes and utensils go in. Bathroom fixtures are polished. Light switches and door handles, touched hundreds of times during the move, get sanitized. Glass surfaces and mirrors are streak-free when the team finishes.

## Debris Removal

One of the most immediate benefits is the removal of all packing materials. Flattened boxes, packing paper, bubble wrap, foam inserts, plastic wrap, and crate materials all leave with the crew. For a four-bedroom Coral Gables home, this can easily fill a truck bed. You won't spend the next two weekends breaking down cardboard and hauling it to the recycling center. The team handles disposal responsibly, recycling materials wherever possible.

## Furniture Arrangement and Fine-Tuning

During the initial placement, furniture goes where the floor plan indicates. But living in the space reveals adjustments that need to happen. The sofa should shift six inches toward the window. The dining table works better centered under the chandelier. The nightstands look wrong on those sides of the bed. White glove crews make these adjustments during the setup phase, often rearranging multiple rooms based on your real-time feedback.

## Bed Making and Linen Setup

White glove setup includes making every bed in the home. Mattress pads, fitted sheets, flat sheets, pillows, and decorative pillows all get placed. Duvet covers go on. For the master bedroom in a Brickell penthouse or a guest suite in a Pinecrest estate, the bed is move-in ready by the time you see it. Bathroom towels get hung or folded on racks and shelves.

## Kitchen Organization

The kitchen takes the most effort to organize properly. Beyond unpacking, the setup crew arranges your kitchen according to how you actually use it. Everyday dishes near the dishwasher. Coffee mugs near the coffee station. Pots and pans in the cabinets closest to the stove. Spices organized on racks. Pantry items grouped by category. This kind of thoughtful placement means you're not hunting through unfamiliar cabinets for a week after the move.

## Technology Reconnection

Televisions get mounted or placed on stands and connected to power and cable. Wi-Fi routers and mesh network nodes are positioned and powered on. Basic audio systems get reconnected. For complex home automation setups, the white glove team coordinates with your technology provider to schedule a follow-up visit for full system calibration, but the everyday devices work from day one.

## The Origin Home

Post-move service also extends to the home you left. The crew does a walk-through to remove any remaining protection materials, check for damage, and leave the property in showing condition. For homeowners who are selling their previous home in Coconut Grove or South Miami, this step saves you a return trip to clean up.
