Just moved to Kendall or planning your fall relocation? Food is one of the best ways to experience your new community. From authentic Cuban spots along Kendall Drive to upscale dining near Dadeland, this suburban Miami neighborhood delivers serious flavor. Here are 10 restaurants that locals swear by.

## 1. Versailles Restaurant

The iconic Cuban restaurant at 3555 SW 8th Street (just north of Kendall in Little Havana) is worth the 15-minute drive. Open 24 hours, Versailles serves classic Cuban dishes like ropa vieja, lechon asado, and their famous Cuban sandwiches. The ventanita window for quick cafecito stops is a Miami institution.

![A Gourmet Dish Elegantly Presented On A  3](/images/blog/2029/10/10-best-restaurants-to-try-in-kendall-after-your-move/a-gourmet-dish-elegantly-presented-on-a--3.webp)


## 2. Shorty's Bar-B-Q

A South Florida legend since 1951, the Kendall location at 9200 S Dixie Highway serves hickory-smoked ribs, chicken, and their famous corn on the cob. The no-frills atmosphere and consistent quality have made this a multi-generational family favorite.

## 3. Sergio's Restaurant

With several Kendall locations including 3252 SW 22nd Street, this Cuban comfort food spot delivers massive portions at reasonable prices. The pan con bistec (steak sandwich) and croquetas are local favorites. Open late for post-movie dinners.

## 4. Red Lobster Kendall

Located at 8705 Mills Drive near The Falls, this seafood chain delivers reliable quality for family outings. The Cheddar Bay Biscuits are legendary, and the Ultimate Feast lets you sample lobster, crab, and shrimp in one sitting.

![Assorted Gourmet Dishes Elegantly Plated 4](/images/blog/2029/10/10-best-restaurants-to-try-in-kendall-after-your-move/assorted-gourmet-dishes-elegantly-plated-4.webp)


## 5. Seasons 52

Fresh, seasonal American cuisine at 321 San Lorenzo Avenue near Dadeland Mall. Everything on the menu is under 475 calories, and the wine flights let you explore their impressive selection. The flatbreads and mini indulgence desserts are standouts.

## 6. Two Chefs

This upscale American bistro at 8287 S Dixie Highway has been a Kendall favorite for over 25 years. Known for creative seasonal menus and an excellent wine list. The Sunday brunch draws regulars from across Miami-Dade.

## 7. Tropical Chinese

At 7991 SW 40th Street, this sprawling dim sum palace is a Sunday tradition for many Kendall families. The cart service brings endless small plates from har gow dumplings to barbecue pork buns. Come early to avoid the wait.

## 8. La Carreta

Another Cuban staple with a location at 8650 Bird Road. Open 24 hours, La Carreta serves hearty Cuban meals and strong cafe cubano around the clock. The vaca frita (crispy shredded beef) and platanos maduros are excellent.

![Elegant Evening Dining At A Tokyo Restau 5](/images/blog/2029/10/10-best-restaurants-to-try-in-kendall-after-your-move/elegant-evening-dining-at-a-tokyo-restau-5.webp)


## 9. Shula's Steak House

Located at 7601 NW 154th Street (technically in Miami Lakes but a quick drive from Kendall), this steakhouse founded by legendary Dolphins coach Don Shula serves prime cuts in an upscale sports-themed setting. Perfect for celebrating your new Kendall address.

## 10. Matsuri

Japanese cuisine at 5959 Bird Road featuring sushi, hibachi, and traditional Japanese dishes. The omakase menu lets the chef showcase the freshest fish, while families love the theatrical hibachi tables.
