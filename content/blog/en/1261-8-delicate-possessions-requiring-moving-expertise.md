Whether you're a long-time Miami resident or just making your September move to South Florida, there's always something new to discover. Here's our curated list to help you make the most of life in the Sunshine State this fall.

## 1. Grand Pianos

These massive instruments require specialized equipment, multiple people, and expertise to avoid damage to the piano and property. Many Coral Gables and Pinecrest homes feature these beautiful instruments that need careful handling.

![Close Up View Of A Black Grand Piano In  1](/images/blog/2029/02/8-delicate-possessions-requiring-moving-expertise/close-up-view-of-a-black-grand-piano-in--1.webp)


## 2. Wine Collections

Temperature sensitivity, fragile bottles, and proper positioning require climate-controlled handling and custom crating. Miami's humidity and heat make climate control especially important for wine collectors in Brickell and Coconut Grove.

## 3. Hot Tubs and Spas

Draining, disconnecting, and moving these heavy items requires professionals familiar with the process.

## 4. Large Aquariums

Fish need separate transport; tanks must be drained, carefully padded, and moved level to prevent cracking. Saltwater aquarium enthusiasts in Aventura and Key Biscayne know how delicate these setups can be.

![High Resolution Close Up Of Piano Keys S 4](/images/blog/2029/02/8-delicate-possessions-requiring-moving-expertise/high-resolution-close-up-of-piano-keys-s-4.webp)


## 5. Pool Tables

Slate tops require disassembly by specialists. Improperly moved pool tables never play the same.

## 6. Fine Art and Sculptures

Custom crating, climate control, and white-glove handling protect valuable artwork during transit. Wynwood art collectors and Design District residents often have pieces that require specialized care.

## 7. Full Gym Equipment

Treadmills, weight machines, and home gyms are heavy and awkward. Professional movers have the equipment to handle them.

![Woman Playing A Grand Piano On Stage Exu 3](/images/blog/2029/02/8-delicate-possessions-requiring-moving-expertise/woman-playing-a-grand-piano-on-stage-exu-3.webp)


## 8. Classic Cars

Valuable vehicles need enclosed transport and special handling. Regular moving trucks aren't appropriate. Doral and Kendall have thriving classic car communities with vehicles that deserve proper care.
