Just moved to Cutler Bay or planning your August relocation? Here's your essential guide to the best restaurants in your new neighborhood. We've compiled this list to help you settle in and discover what makes Cutler Bay such a delicious place to call home.

## 1. Black Point Ocean Grill

**Address:** Black Point Marina, 24775 SW 87th Ave, Cutler Bay, FL

![Luxury Dining Setting In Dubai Featuring 1](/images/blog/2028/11/9-must-try-dining-spots-in-cutler-bay-for-new-residents/luxury-dining-setting-in-dubai-featuring-1.webp)


Waterfront dining with beautiful views and a boardwalk. Live music featuring rock, reggae, and country. Serving fresh local seafood since 2005 in a relaxed atmosphere.

## 2. El Churrascaso Brazilian Grill

**Address:** 20505 S Dixie Hwy #1245, Cutler Bay, FL

Authentic Brazilian steakhouse with juicy meats and generous sides. Don't miss the Brazilian cheese bread with guava sauce. Friendly, attentive staff.

## 3. Golden Rule Seafood

**Address:** South Dixie Hwy, Cutler Bay, FL

An iconic South Florida landmark since 1943. Family-owned seafood market and full-service restaurant specializing in locally inspired comfort foods fresh from the sea.

## 4. Moon Thai & Japanese

**Address:** Southland Mall, S Dixie Hwy, Cutler Bay, FL

Delicious sushi and traditional Thai and Japanese dishes in a chill, modern atmosphere. Known for the best hibachi in town.

## 5. Urban Rrasoi - Cutler Bay

**Address:** Cutler Bay, FL

Famous Indian restaurant following centuries-old recipes with a modern twist. Every dish, from dal to tandoori, is made with patience as the secret ingredient.

## 6. Tasty Ramen

**Address:** Cutler Bay, FL

![Stacked Bowls At A Street Food Stall In  2](/images/blog/2028/11/9-must-try-dining-spots-in-cutler-bay-for-new-residents/stacked-bowls-at-a-street-food-stall-in--2.webp)


House-made broth served with ramen, poke, rice bowls, and classic appetizers. Also features boba tea for a refreshing drink.

## 7. Rico's Steak & Grill

**Address:** Cutler Bay, FL

A fusion of American and Venezuelan cuisine. Choose from classic burgers or Venezuelan specialties like pabellon and cachapa. Dine-in, takeout, and delivery available.

## 8. Ackees Jamaican Cuisine

**Address:** S Dixie Hwy, Cutler Bay, FL

Classic Jamaican dishes including curry chicken, jerk chicken, oxtail, and brown stew chicken. Pair with BIGGA, the famous Jamaican soft drink.

## 9. Flanigan's Seafood Bar & Grill (Coming Soon)

**Address:** 20971 Old Cutler Rd, Cutler Bay, FL

One of South Florida's most iconic restaurant chains is joining Cutler Bay, bringing signature ribs and legendary flavor to the Shoppes of Cutler Bay.

## Getting Settled in Cutler Bay

Cutler Bay offers a welcoming mix of suburban comfort and South Florida flavor. Located between Homestead and Pinecrest along US-1, you'll find easy access to both the Florida Keys and downtown Miami. The community centers around Southland Mall and the Black Point Marina area, where many of these restaurants are located.

For new residents, these dining spots provide more than just great food. They're where you'll meet neighbors, celebrate milestones, and discover what makes Cutler Bay feel like home. From the longtime locals at Golden Rule Seafood to the diverse international options along South Dixie Highway, this neighborhood serves up something for every craving.
