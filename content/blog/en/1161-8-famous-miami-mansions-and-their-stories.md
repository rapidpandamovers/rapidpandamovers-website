Whether you're a long-time Miami resident or just making your June move to South Florida, there's always something new to discover. Here's our curated list to help you make the most of life in the Sunshine State this summer.

## 1. Star Island Estates

This exclusive island has housed Madonna, Diddy, and Gloria Estefan. These properties require discrete, high-security moving services with restricted access through the guarded bridge off MacArthur Causeway.

![A Grand Red Mansion Showcasing Classic C 2](/images/blog/2028/10/8-famous-miami-mansions-and-their-stories/a-grand-red-mansion-showcasing-classic-c-2.webp)


## 2. Versace Mansion (Casa Casuarina)

Gianni Versace's famous South Beach residence on Ocean Drive has seen multiple transformations and celebrity occupants. Now a boutique hotel, the 1930 Mediterranean Revival building remains one of Miami Beach's most photographed landmarks.

## 3. Fisher Island Properties

Accessible only by ferry from the terminal near South Pointe, this ultra-exclusive community requires specialized logistics for any move. Moving trucks must be scheduled in advance and transported via the cargo ferry.

## 4. Coral Gables Mansions

The historic Mediterranean-style estates along Granada Boulevard and Biltmore Way have housed numerous celebrities over the decades. Many feature mature banyan trees and circular driveways that require careful navigation for moving trucks.

![Charming Victorian Style Mansion Surroun 1](/images/blog/2028/10/8-famous-miami-mansions-and-their-stories/charming-victorian-style-mansion-surroun-1.webp)


## 5. Miami Beach Penthouses

Luxury high-rise penthouses require coordinated elevator access and building management approval for moves.

## 6. Brickell Key Condominiums

These waterfront luxury towers on the private island off Brickell Avenue have attracted international celebrities and require careful coordination for high-rise moves. Building management typically requires advance booking of service elevators and loading docks.

## 7. Coconut Grove Waterfront Estates

Historic estates along South Bayshore Drive and Main Highway have been home to various entertainment industry professionals over the years. The Grove's leafy streets and older construction often mean narrow access points and fragile antique fixtures to protect.

![Stunning Aerial Photo Of A Wedding Event 3](/images/blog/2028/10/8-famous-miami-mansions-and-their-stories/stunning-aerial-photo-of-a-wedding-event-3.webp)


## 8. Golden Beach Mansions

This ultra-exclusive town north of Sunny Isles Beach features beachfront estates owned by business moguls and celebrities. The small police force and private security mean all moving crews must be pre-approved and scheduled with the town.
