## Furniture Moving Tips That Actually Work in Miami

### Book Two Weeks Out in March

Spring break drives demand for movers across Miami-Dade. Families relocating to Kendall school districts, snowbirds heading north, and lease turnovers in Miami Beach all compete for truck space. Schedule early or pay premium rates.

### Measure Everything Twice

Your measurements should include:
- Doorway widths at both locations (remove door if under 32 inches)
- Hallway turning radius (especially in older Coral Gables homes)
- Elevator dimensions in high-rises (many Aventura condos have 6-foot depth limits)
- Stairwell clearance for walkups

Skip this step and you'll watch movers spend an hour removing door frames that a tape measure would have flagged.

### Schedule Morning Moves

Miami's spring weather pattern runs like clockwork: pleasant mornings, humidity building by noon, afternoon thunderstorms rolling in by 3pm. Load furniture between 7am and noon. After that, you're racing the weather and risking water damage on pieces sitting in the driveway.

### Protect Against Humidity

South Florida humidity warps wood furniture, rusts metal frames, and promotes mold growth in upholstery. We wrap furniture in breathable moving blankets, not plastic that traps moisture. For antique pieces moving to Pinecrest or Homestead homes with longer transit times, climate-controlled trucks prevent damage.

Get ready for moving day with these steps:
