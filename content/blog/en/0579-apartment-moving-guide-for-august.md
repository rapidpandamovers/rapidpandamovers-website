## Key Tips for Apartment Moving in August

### Plan Ahead for Peak Times

August can be a busy time for moving companies, especially in popular neighborhoods like Kendall, Doral, and Aventura. With summer moving season activities and seasonal transitions, scheduling your apartment moving early ensures you get your preferred date and time.

### Reserve the Freight Elevator Early

Reserve the freight elevator well in advance and confirm with building management. Most Miami high-rises in Brickell and Downtown require 48-72 hours notice, and some Wynwood loft buildings have limited elevator access windows. This single step can save hours of stress and potential problems on moving day. Our experienced crews have seen what happens when this step is skipped.

### Consider the Weather

South Florida's August weather brings daily afternoon thunderstorms that typically hit between 3pm and 6pm. Professional movers know how to protect your belongings from humidity, rain, and heat exposure during transport. Morning moves are ideal this time of year to avoid the worst of the afternoon storms.

### Prepare Your Belongings

Take time to inventory your items before the move. This is especially important for apartment moving, as proper documentation helps ensure everything arrives safely at your new location.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does apartment moving cost in August?

![Upward View Of A Tall Modern Building Wi 2](/images/blog/2026/08/apartment-moving-guide-for-august/upward-view-of-a-tall-modern-building-wi-2.webp)


August is peak season in Miami, so rates may be higher. A one-bedroom apartment move typically costs $500-$1,200, while a two-bedroom ranges from $900-$2,000. High-rise fees for COI and elevator access add $200-$500.

### How do I protect electronics during a hot August move?

Keep electronics in the air conditioning until the last minute. Transport them in the cab with AC, not the truck bed. Wrap monitors and TVs in blankets, and use silica gel packets to absorb moisture.

### Should I move in the morning or afternoon during August?

Always schedule morning moves in August. Afternoon thunderstorms hit between 3-6 PM almost daily, and temperatures peak in the early afternoon. An 8 AM start gives you the best conditions.
