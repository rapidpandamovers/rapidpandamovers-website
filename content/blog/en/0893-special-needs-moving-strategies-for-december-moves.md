Moving someone with mobility challenges or medical equipment isn't like a standard relocation. You're coordinating wheelchair ramps, power availability for medical devices, and making sure the new home in Coral Gables or Kendall is set up before arrival. December adds another layer: snowbirds flooding into South Florida, holiday parties blocking building elevators, and medical supply companies operating on limited holiday schedules.

## Key Tips for Special Needs Moving in December

### Plan Ahead for Peak Season

December ranks among the busiest months for Miami movers. Snowbirds arriving from the Northeast, families relocating before the new school year, and holiday vacation schedules create a perfect storm. For special needs moves, book at least 3-4 weeks ahead. If you're moving to a high-rise in Aventura or Doral, reserve the freight elevator the moment you have a confirmed date.

### Communicate Medical Equipment Requirements Early

This single step can save hours of stress on moving day. Tell us exactly what equipment you have: hospital beds, oxygen concentrators, motorized wheelchairs, lift systems. Our crews have handled moves from Baptist Hospital rehab facilities to private homes in Coconut Grove, and we know that a CPAP machine needs to be accessible on night one, not buried in a box.

### Account for Miami's December Weather

December averages 75 degrees in Miami, but afternoon rain showers still happen. Medical equipment and mobility aids need protection from both humidity and sudden downpours. We use climate-controlled trucks and waterproof wrapping for sensitive equipment. If you're moving to a ground-floor unit in Palmetto Bay or a waterfront condo in Key Biscayne, we'll also check for flooding history and plan the unload sequence accordingly.

### Create a Medical Equipment Inventory

Document every piece of medical equipment with photos, serial numbers, and any special handling requirements. Include battery backup needs, temperature sensitivity, and which items need to stay with the patient during transport. This inventory becomes your checklist for the new home setup in Wynwood, Pinecrest, or wherever you're headed.
