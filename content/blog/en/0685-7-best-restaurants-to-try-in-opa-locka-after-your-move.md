Just moved to Opa-locka or planning your spring relocation? Food is one of the best ways to experience your new community. We've compiled this March guide to help you discover the culinary gems that make Opa-locka such a delicious place to call home.

## 1. Romo's Place Restaurant

**Address:** 13300 NW 42nd Ave, Opa-locka, FL 33054

![Assorted Gourmet Dishes Elegantly Plated 3](/images/blog/2027/03/7-best-restaurants-to-try-in-opa-locka-after-your-move/assorted-gourmet-dishes-elegantly-plated-3.webp)


A beloved Cuban restaurant known for its incredible Alfredo and the best churrasco in town. The entire staff is friendly and helpful, making every visit feel like coming home.

## 2. El Novillo Restaurant

**Address:** NW 27th Ave, Opa-locka, FL

A family-owned Latin American steakhouse operating in South Florida since the 1980s. They specialize in churrascos cut from lean tenderloins, grilled and marinated in their homemade fresh chimichurri sauce.

## 3. World's Famous Souseman Soulfood

**Address:** NW 27th Ave, Opa-locka, FL

![Sophisticated Dining Setup With Gourmet  2](/images/blog/2027/03/7-best-restaurants-to-try-in-opa-locka-after-your-move/sophisticated-dining-setup-with-gourmet--2.webp)


A local favorite serving authentic Southern soul food. Known for generous portions and recipes passed down through generations. Perfect for comfort food cravings.

## 4. The Licking Food Court

**Address:** 4895 NW 183rd St, Opa-locka, FL 33055

One of the top-rated spots in the area, The Licking serves up soul food classics with a modern twist. Famous for their wings and sides that keep locals coming back.

## 5. La Romanita Restaurant & Lounge

**Address:** Opa-locka, FL

A neighborhood favorite offering Latin American cuisine with a relaxed atmosphere. Great for dinner with friends or a casual family meal.

## 6. Jackson's Soul Food 2

**Address:** NW 27th Ave area, Opa-locka, FL

![Wooden Table With Assorted Pizzas And Se 1](/images/blog/2027/03/7-best-restaurants-to-try-in-opa-locka-after-your-move/wooden-table-with-assorted-pizzas-and-se-1.webp)


Serving up traditional soul food favorites including fried chicken, collard greens, mac and cheese, and cornbread. A must-visit for Southern cuisine lovers.

## 7. Snapper's Fish and Chicken

**Address:** NW 125th St, Opa-locka, FL 33054

A local favorite for fresh fried fish and crispy chicken. Known for quick service and generous portions at affordable prices.
