PCS orders do not care about the calendar. If your Permanent Change of Station lands during the Thanksgiving period, you are dealing with holiday schedules, reduced office hours at base transportation, and moving companies juggling their busiest residential season. South Florida adds its own layer: snowbird traffic picks up in November, and the population in Miami-Dade swells as seasonal residents return. Here is how to handle a military move in Miami during the Thanksgiving window.

## Why Thanksgiving Complicates Military Moves

The period from mid-November through early December creates a scheduling crunch for several reasons:

![A Sophisticated Thanksgiving Table Setup 2](/images/blog/2029/11/thanksgiving-guide-to-military-moving-in-miami/a-sophisticated-thanksgiving-table-setup-2.webp)


- **Moving companies book up**: Thanksgiving week is one of the busiest periods for residential movers. Families want to be settled before the holidays, which means fewer available crews.
- **Base offices close for the holiday**: Transportation Management Offices (TMOs) at Homestead Air Reserve Base and other South Florida installations close for Thanksgiving and may have reduced hours the surrounding days.
- **Traffic increases**: I-95, the Turnpike, and US-1 see heavier traffic starting in November as seasonal residents return to South Florida.
- **Utility and service delays**: Setting up utilities, internet, and cable at your new address may take longer with holiday staffing reductions.

## PCS Move Types and How Thanksgiving Affects Each

### Government-Arranged Move (GHC)

If the military arranges your move through the Global Household Goods Contract, you have less control over the exact dates. The carrier assigned to your shipment operates on their own schedule, and Thanksgiving week may cause delays. Build a buffer of several days between your pack-out date and your report date.

### Personally Procured Move (PPM/DITY)

A PPM gives you more scheduling flexibility but puts the logistics on your shoulders. The advantage during Thanksgiving is that you choose when to load and drive. The disadvantage is that rental trucks and portable containers book up fast during the holiday period.

Key PPM documentation to keep organized:

- **Weight tickets**: Get certified weight tickets before and after loading. Weigh stations may have reduced hours during the holiday.
- **All receipts**: Fuel, tolls, packing materials, truck rental, and meals. The reimbursement rate for PPMs has increased to 100% of what the government would have paid, so thorough documentation directly affects your reimbursement.
- **Orders and amendments**: Keep copies accessible, not packed in a box.

### Temporary Storage

If there is a gap between your move-out and move-in dates, you may need temporary storage. The military covers storage in transit (SIT) for a set number of days. In Miami, [**storage solutions**](/storage-solutions) should be climate-controlled because humidity can damage belongings even in November when conditions are milder.

## Scheduling Strategy for Thanksgiving Week

### Book 6 to 8 Weeks Out

Do not wait until early November to schedule your move. Moving companies in South Florida start filling their Thanksgiving-week slots by mid-September. Contact your TMO and get your paperwork started as soon as you receive orders.

### Avoid Wednesday Through Sunday

Thanksgiving Wednesday through the following Sunday is the worst window for scheduling anything. If you have any flexibility in your report date, aim to complete your move by the Monday or Tuesday before Thanksgiving, or wait until the following Monday.

### Have a Backup Plan

Moves get rescheduled. Carriers cancel. Weather events, while rare in November, can disrupt plans. Have a plan for where your family will stay if there is a gap, whether that is a hotel, temporary lodging on base, or staying with friends.

## Miami-Specific Considerations for Military Families

### Homestead Air Reserve Base

Homestead ARB is the primary military installation in Miami-Dade County. The base is located in southern Miami-Dade, which means commutes to most of the county run 30 to 60 minutes depending on where you settle. The TMO at Homestead can help coordinate your move, but schedule appointments early since the office is smaller than those at larger installations.

### Housing Options by Budget

Military families moving to Miami spread across several areas depending on BAH and lifestyle preferences:

- **Homestead and Florida City**: Closest to the base, most affordable housing, longer commutes to the rest of the county
- **Cutler Bay and Palmetto Bay**: Mid-range pricing, good schools, reasonable commute to Homestead
- **Kendall**: Large inventory of homes and apartments, suburban feel, central location
- **Doral**: Popular with military families who want access to the airport and western Miami-Dade amenities

### BAH and Miami's Housing Market

Miami's housing costs consistently exceed national averages. Review your Basic Allowance for Housing rate for the Miami zip code you are targeting. BAH varies by rank and dependency status, and in a high-cost market like Miami, the gap between your allowance and actual rent or mortgage payment can be significant. Factor in utilities, renter's insurance, and Florida's property insurance costs when budgeting.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I use a private moving company for a military PCS?

Yes, through the PPM (Personally Procured Move) option. You arrange and pay for the move yourself, then submit documentation for reimbursement. Using a professional mover like Rapid Panda Movers means you get experienced crews while still qualifying for your PPM allowance.

### Does the military cover storage during a PCS?

The military covers Storage in Transit (SIT) for up to 90 days under most circumstances. Extensions are possible with approval. Make sure your storage facility provides the documentation required by your branch for reimbursement.

### How far in advance should I contact the TMO?

As soon as you receive orders. For a Thanksgiving-period move, aim for at least 6 to 8 weeks of lead time. The TMO can begin processing your move and connecting you with approved carriers or authorizing your PPM.
