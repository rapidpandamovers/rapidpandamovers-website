Standard moving practices don't work for high-profile relocations. ## Key Tips for Celebrity Moving in Winter

### Request the Same Trusted Crew for Both Properties

Consistency matters for high-profile moves. When the same vetted team handles both your origin and destination, you reduce the number of people who know your address, your schedule, and the contents of your home. Our celebrity crews sign NDAs and undergo background checks before every job.

### Schedule During Off-Peak Hours

Winter's shorter days and cooler mornings create natural windows for discreet loading. Early morning moves (6-8am) or late evening relocations avoid peak foot traffic in neighborhoods like Star Island and Fisher Island. Our crews bring portable lighting and work efficiently in low-visibility conditions.

### Coordinate Building Access in Advance

Luxury condos in Miami Beach and Aventura have strict protocols. Service elevators, loading docks, and security checkpoints all need advance coordination. We handle building management communication so your name stays out of the move-in logs where possible.

### Create a Detailed Inventory with Photography

High-value items like artwork, wine collections, and designer furniture need thorough documentation before the move. Our team photographs and catalogs every piece, providing insurance-grade records that protect your investment.
