### Have a Plan Before the Crew Arrives

Even though you are moving on short notice, take 30 minutes to organize your priorities. Decide what goes first, what can wait, and what you can leave behind. This helps the crew work faster and keeps costs down.

### Watch the Afternoon Forecast

October in Miami still brings afternoon storms, typically between 2 PM and 6 PM. If possible, aim for a morning start time to get the bulk of the move done before any rain rolls in. Professional movers know how to protect your belongings from humidity and sudden downpours. Our crews are familiar with loading dock schedules at high-rises in Downtown Miami and Edgewater, where timing around weather is key.

### Prepare Your Belongings

Take time to inventory your items before the move. This is especially important for same day moving, as proper documentation helps ensure everything arrives safely at your new location.
