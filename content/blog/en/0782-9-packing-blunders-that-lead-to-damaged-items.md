Moving in Miami comes with unique challenges, from high humidity that weakens cardboard to the long distances between neighborhoods like Kendall and Aventura. Packing mistakes can turn a smooth relocation into a costly disaster. Here are nine common packing blunders that lead to damaged items and how to avoid them.

## 1. Overpacking Large Boxes

A huge box of books is impossible to lift safely. Use small boxes for heavy items, always. Your back and your movers will thank you. This is especially important in Miami's heat, where carrying heavy loads up condo elevators in Brickell or through tight apartment hallways in Coral Gables makes every pound count.

![A Collection Of Labeled Cardboard Boxes  4](/images/blog/2027/08/9-packing-blunders-that-lead-to-damaged-items/a-collection-of-labeled-cardboard-boxes--4.webp)


## 2. Insufficient Cushioning

One layer of newspaper isn't enough. Fragile items need individual wrapping, padding on all sides, and zero room to shift.

## 3. Leaving Hollow Items Empty

Hollow items like vases and cups are fragile. Fill them with packing paper or soft materials for structural support.

## 4. Using Old, Weak Boxes

Old boxes lose structural integrity. In Miami's humidity, boxes absorb moisture even in air-conditioned spaces. Wet boxes are disasters waiting to happen. Use fresh, sturdy boxes from a moving supply store, and if you're storing boxes in a garage or storage unit in places like Doral or Homestead, check them before moving day.

## 5. Improper Dish Packing

Never stack plates horizontally. Pack them on edge, like records, with padding between each one. This prevents cracking from weight.

## 6. Taping Box Bottoms Only Once

Double-tape the bottom of every box. Single strips fail under weight, sending contents crashing to the ground.

![Unrecognizable Man Sealing Box With Duck 1](/images/blog/2027/08/9-packing-blunders-that-lead-to-damaged-items/unrecognizable-man-sealing-box-with-duck-1.webp)


## 7. Forgetting to Label Fragile Items

Mark fragile boxes on multiple sides. Movers need to see the warning from any angle during loading and unloading.

## 8. Packing Liquids Unsealed

Always seal liquid containers with tape and place them in plastic bags. Leaks ruin entire boxes of belongings.

## 9. Rushing the Packing Process

Hasty packing leads to poor protection. Start early, take your time, and pack methodically room by room. Miami traffic and building elevator reservations can add hours to your moving day, so don't let poor packing add even more stress.
