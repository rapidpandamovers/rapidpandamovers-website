Just moved to Florida City or planning your February relocation? This gateway to the Everglades and Florida Keys offers a unique small-town atmosphere. While Florida City itself is compact, the adjacent Homestead area provides excellent spots for remote workers and coffee lovers. Here are nine local favorites.

## 1. Genesis Bakery and Cafe

**60 N Homestead Blvd, Homestead** | Opens 5:30 AM

![A Young Woman Uses A Laptop Behind A Caf 1](/images/blog/2026/02/9-top-cafes-for-remote-workers-in-florida-city/a-young-woman-uses-a-laptop-behind-a-caf-1.webp)


This Cuban bakery serves what locals call "the best cafe con leche around." Open early for the pre-work crowd, Genesis offers high-quality coffee and freshly baked pastries that reflect their passion for Cuban traditions. They also have a food truck next to Robert Is Here for your Everglades adventures.

## 2. Passion 4 Coffee

**250 E Palm Dr, Florida City** | Florida Keys Outlet Marketplace

Located in the outlet mall, this internationally-influenced coffee shop offers lattes, Cafe Con Leche, and frappuccinos, both hot and iced. The selection of sweets pairs perfectly with your morning work session, and the mall location provides ample parking.

## 3. Robert Is Here Fruit Stand

**19200 SW 344th St, Homestead** | Opens 9 AM daily

Since 1959, Robert Is Here has been more than a fruit stand. It's a community institution. While not a traditional cafe, their legendary fruit milkshakes are perfect for a refreshing work break. Free WiFi, outdoor seating, live music on weekends, and an exotic fruit selection make this a must-visit destination.

## 4. Knaus Berry Farm

**16790 SW 177th Ave, Redland** | Opens 8 AM (closed Mondays)

The legendary cinnamon rolls at $1.45 each have been a South Florida institution since 1956. Recently reopened at a new Redland location under new ownership, they now accept credit cards and are open Sundays for the first time. The strawberry milkshakes are equally famous.

## 5. La Patrona Bakery

**110 W Mowry Dr, Homestead** | Opens 6 AM

This authentic Mexican bakery is an essential stop for remote workers heading to or from the Keys. Grab a metal tray and tongs to select from fresh conchas, maranitos, cuernitos, and pan dulce. Everything is baked fresh daily. Pair with their coffee for a perfect morning.

## 6. White Lion Cafe

**146 NW 7th St, Homestead** | (305) 248-1076

![Relaxing Caf Scene In Istanbul Featuring 2](/images/blog/2026/02/9-top-cafes-for-remote-workers-in-florida-city/relaxing-caf-scene-in-istanbul-featuring-2.webp)


A Homestead institution since 1989, White Lion feels like an antique shop that happens to serve excellent food. While primarily a lunch spot (they close at 3 PM most days with Friday dinner service), their globally-inspired American cuisine and charming atmosphere make it worth the visit.

## 7. Taqueria Morelia - Florida City

**961 W Palm Dr, Florida City** | Opens 9 AM

Attached to a gas station in true hidden-gem fashion, this is where construction workers and locals get their morning fuel. The cafe con leche is strong and cheap, and you can pair it with authentic breakfast tacos. Their salsa bar adds extra kick to your morning.

## 8. Yardie Spice

**225 S Krome Ave, Homestead** | Opens 11:30 AM

Owned by a Haitian-Jamaican couple since 2013, Yardie Spice brings incredible Caribbean flavors to Homestead. While they open later (making this an afternoon work session spot), their no-microwave kitchen serves authentic jerk chicken and curry dishes. BYOB and vegan options available.

## 9. Starbucks Florida City

**33501 S Dixie Hwy, Florida City**

For reliable WiFi and familiar surroundings, the Florida City Starbucks provides a consistent remote work option. Air conditioning, predictable hours, and plenty of outlets make it a safe fallback when you need guaranteed connectivity near the Everglades.
