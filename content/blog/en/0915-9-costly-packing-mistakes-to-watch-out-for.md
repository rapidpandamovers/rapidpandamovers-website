Packing seems straightforward until something breaks. These common errors are responsible for most moving damage we see at Rapid Panda Movers. After handling thousands of Miami relocations, our crews know exactly which packing mistakes lead to broken items and higher costs.

## 1. Overpacking Large Boxes

A huge box of books is impossible to lift safely. Use small boxes for heavy items, always. Large boxes should be reserved for lightweight items like pillows, linens, and lampshades. Your back and your movers will thank you.

![A Collection Of Labeled Cardboard Boxes  2](/images/blog/2027/11/9-costly-packing-mistakes-to-watch-out-for/a-collection-of-labeled-cardboard-boxes--2.webp)


## 2. Insufficient Cushioning

One layer of newspaper isn't enough. Fragile items need individual wrapping, padding on all sides, and zero room to shift.

## 3. Leaving Hollow Items Empty

Hollow items like vases and cups are fragile. Fill them with packing paper or soft materials for structural support.

## 4. Using Old, Weak Boxes

Old boxes lose structural integrity. In Miami's humidity, cardboard weakens even faster, especially boxes stored in garages or outdoor storage. Wet boxes are disasters waiting to happen. Use fresh, sturdy boxes from a moving supply store or ask your moving company about box rentals.

![Crop Faceless Male Sealing Packed Carton 4](/images/blog/2027/11/9-costly-packing-mistakes-to-watch-out-for/crop-faceless-male-sealing-packed-carton-4.webp)


## 5. Improper Dish Packing

Never stack plates horizontally. Pack them on edge, like records, with padding between each one. This prevents cracking from weight.

## 6. Forgetting to Seal Box Bottoms

The bottom of your box needs just as much tape as the top. Use the H-taping method: one strip down the center seam, then one perpendicular strip on each side.

## 7. Mixing Items from Different Rooms

Keep each room's items in separate boxes. This makes unpacking much easier and prevents confusion during the move. When boxes arrive at your new Brickell condo or Coral Gables home, movers can place them directly in the correct rooms without guessing.

## 8. Not Labeling Boxes Clearly

"Kitchen" isn't enough. Write "Kitchen - Everyday Dishes" or "Kitchen - Pots & Pans" on at least two sides. Include "FRAGILE" in large letters when needed.

![Hands Carefully Packing Glassware Into A 1](/images/blog/2027/11/9-costly-packing-mistakes-to-watch-out-for/hands-carefully-packing-glassware-into-a-1.webp)


## 9. Packing Everything Without Purging

Moving is the perfect time to declutter. Don't waste time and money moving items you'll just donate later. Sort before you pack.
