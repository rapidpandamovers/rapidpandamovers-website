Living in Opa-locka puts you within easy driving distance of some of South Florida's most beautiful beaches. Here are six coastal destinations worth your time, each offering something different for your next beach day.

## 1. Haulover Beach Park

**Distance from Opa-locka**: 20 minutes (10 miles)
**Address**: 10800 Collins Avenue, Miami Beach

![A Woman Taking A Photo On A Sunny Day By 2](/images/blog/2029/03/6-stunning-beaches-within-reach-of-opa-locka/a-woman-taking-a-photo-on-a-sunny-day-by-2.webp)


Haulover Beach Park stretches for nearly a mile along the Atlantic. The northern section is clothing-optional, but the main beach area is family-friendly with lifeguards, picnic areas, and clean facilities. The park also offers excellent fishing from the pier and a popular food truck area on weekends.

## 2. Crandon Park Beach

**Distance from Opa-locka**: 35 minutes (18 miles)
**Address**: 6747 Crandon Boulevard, Key Biscayne

Crandon Park consistently ranks among Florida's best beaches. The shallow, calm waters make it ideal for families with young children. The beach curves around a protected bay, creating a natural wading pool. Parking costs around $8, but the facilities, shaded picnic areas, and soft sand are worth it.

## 3. Oleta River State Park Beach

**Distance from Opa-locka**: 15 minutes (7 miles)
**Address**: 3400 NE 163rd Street, North Miami Beach

Florida's largest urban park includes a secluded beach on Biscayne Bay. The water is calm and shallow, perfect for kayaking and paddleboarding. The beach itself is smaller than ocean beaches but far less crowded. Rent kayaks on-site and explore the mangrove trails.

## 4. Bill Baggs Cape Florida State Park

**Distance from Opa-locka**: 40 minutes (20 miles)
**Address**: 1200 South Crandon Boulevard, Key Biscayne

![Young Woman Photographing The Ocean On A 1](/images/blog/2029/03/6-stunning-beaches-within-reach-of-opa-locka/young-woman-photographing-the-ocean-on-a-1.webp)


The beach at Bill Baggs wraps around the southern tip of Key Biscayne near the historic Cape Florida Lighthouse. The waters are crystal clear, and the beach offers excellent snorkeling near the seawall. Two on-site restaurants serve Cuban food with waterfront views.

## 5. Sunny Isles Beach

**Distance from Opa-locka**: 20 minutes (9 miles)
**Address**: Collins Avenue at 163rd Street, Sunny Isles Beach

Sunny Isles offers a wide stretch of public beach backed by high-rise condos. The sand is well-maintained, lifeguards are on duty, and numerous restaurants and cafes line the Collins Avenue strip. Metered street parking is available, and the beach is less crowded than South Beach on most days.

## 6. North Beach Oceanside Park

**Distance from Opa-locka**: 25 minutes (12 miles)
**Address**: 8328 Collins Avenue, Miami Beach

This quieter section of Miami Beach sits north of the busy South Beach scene. The park offers a playground, basketball courts, and clean restroom facilities. The beach is wide and well-maintained, with fewer tourists and more space to spread out.
