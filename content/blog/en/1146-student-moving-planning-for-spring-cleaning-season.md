Spring break ends, finals loom, and suddenly you realize your lease is up in six weeks. ## Why Spring Is Strategic for Student Moves

The semester crunch actually works in your favor. Most students wait until May to think about moving, which means March availability is better and prices are lower. Landlords near campus want to fill units before summer, giving you negotiating leverage. Plus, the weather is perfect for hauling boxes without sweating through your last clean shirt.
