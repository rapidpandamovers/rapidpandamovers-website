## Key Tips for Hourly Moving in October

### Have Everything Packed Before the Crew Arrives

This is the single biggest money-saver with hourly billing. Our crews charge from arrival to departure. Every minute spent waiting for boxes to be taped or furniture to be disassembled adds to your bill. Stack boxes near your front door, break down furniture the night before, and clear walkways so movers can work without obstacles.

Well-prepared moves often come in 30% under the estimated time.

### Schedule a Morning Start

October mornings in Miami are warm but manageable, with temperatures in the low 80s. By afternoon, the heat index climbs and pop-up thunderstorms can hit, especially in the first half of the month. Book your crew for 7 or 8 AM. By noon or early afternoon, your move is done and you have avoided both the worst heat and the storm window.

### Know What Affects Your Bill

Unlike flat-rate moves, hourly billing means you control the total cost. Here are the main factors:

- **Distance between stops**: Moving from Coconut Grove to Pinecrest takes less time than Homestead to Aventura
- **Floor level and elevator access**: High-rise condos in Brickell may require elevator reservations that add wait time
- **Item size and weight**: A piano or safe adds time. Let us know beforehand so we bring the right equipment
- **Preparation level**: Packed and ready? You save money. Still packing? The clock keeps running

Most Miami moves under 3 bedrooms take 2-4 hours with a two-person crew.

### Document Your Items

Create a simple inventory before the crew arrives. Photograph valuables and note their condition. Hourly movers work fast, so having this documentation ready protects both you and the moving team if questions come up later.

## Weather Considerations for October Moves

South Florida's October weather is transitional. The first two weeks still feel like summer, with daily storms and high humidity. The second half of the month starts drying out, with fewer storms and slightly lower humidity. If you have flexibility on your move date, aim for the last two weeks of October.
