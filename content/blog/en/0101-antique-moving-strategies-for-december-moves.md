## Key Tips for Antique Moving in December

### Plan Ahead for Peak Times

December can be a busy time for moving companies in Miami. With Art Basel visitors in Miami Beach, holiday season activities, and snowbirds arriving from up north, scheduling your antique moving early ensures you get your preferred date and time. Many families in neighborhoods like Pinecrest and Coral Gables also use the school break to relocate.

### Get appraisals and photograph items before the move for insurance purposes

This single step can save hours of stress and potential problems on moving day. Our experienced crews have seen what happens when this step is skipped.

### Consider Miami's December Weather

South Florida's December weather brings lower humidity than summer months, which is actually ideal for moving delicate antiques. However, temperatures can still reach the 80s, and sudden rain showers are common. Professional movers know how to protect your belongings from humidity, rain, and heat exposure during transport. If you're moving antique wooden furniture, the cooler December temperatures help prevent warping and cracking that can occur during our hot summer months.

### Prepare Your Belongings

Take time to inventory your items before the move. This is especially important for antique moving, as proper documentation helps ensure everything arrives safely at your new location.
