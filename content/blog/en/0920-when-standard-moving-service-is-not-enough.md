# When Standard Moving Service Is Not Enough

Standard moving service handles most household relocations just fine. A crew loads your boxes and furniture, drives across town, and unloads at the new place. But there are situations where that basic package falls short, and trying to force a standard crew to handle specialty items leads to damage, delays, and higher costs than if you'd planned for the right service from the start.

## Your Items Weigh More Than 300 Pounds Each

Standard moving crews work with hand trucks and two-person carries. When individual items exceed 300 pounds, you need heavy-duty equipment: safe dollies, stair-climbing machines, hydraulic lift gates, and sometimes a crane. Gun safes, marble slabs, commercial appliances, and cast-iron stoves all fall into this category. A standard crew trying to wrestle a 1,200-pound safe down a staircase in a Doral home is a recipe for injury and property damage.

## You Own Items with Mechanical Components

Grandfather clocks, arcade machines, player pianos, vintage jukeboxes. These items have internal mechanisms that can't handle vibration, tilting, or impact. Moving a grandfather clock without securing the pendulum, weights, and chains first will destroy the movement. A standard crew might not know to take those steps. Specialty movers inventory the internal components, secure or remove them, and reassemble at the destination.

## Building Access Is Complicated

High-rise condos in Brickell and Downtown Miami have strict moving rules: designated elevators, mandatory floor protection, time windows, insurance certificates, and loading dock reservations. Some buildings require a Certificate of Insurance naming the condo association as additionally insured. A standard moving company that shows up without the right paperwork gets turned away at the lobby. We handle building coordination as part of the service.

## The Value Justifies Extra Protection

Original artwork, wine collections, antique furniture, and designer pieces deserve more than a moving blanket and a prayer. When a single dining table costs $15,000 or a painting is worth six figures, the investment in custom crating, climate-controlled transport, and full replacement value insurance makes financial sense. Standard service typically includes basic coverage at $0.60 per pound, which means a destroyed $10,000 antique desk weighing 100 pounds gets you a check for $60.

## The Move Involves Disassembly and Reassembly

Pool tables, home gyms, modular closet systems, and custom built-ins need to be taken apart with precision and put back together correctly. A slate pool table that gets reassembled with an unleveled frame plays differently forever. Specialty movers photograph, label, and catalog every component during disassembly. They bring the tools and knowledge to get it right on reassembly.

## You Need Coordination Between Trades

Some moves require an electrician to disconnect a hot tub, a plumber to handle a wine cellar cooling system, or a crane operator to remove a safe through a second-story window. Specialty moving services coordinate these third parties as part of the project management, so you're not juggling six different vendors and hoping they all show up on time.
