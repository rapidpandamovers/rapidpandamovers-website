Just moved to Miami Gardens or planning your spring relocation? Food is one of the best ways to experience your new community. Miami Gardens is home to Hard Rock Stadium, but locals know the real attraction is the incredible mix of Caribbean, African, and American soul food restaurants scattered throughout the city. Here are ten spots that make Miami Gardens such a delicious place to call home.

## 1. Lorna's Caribbean & American Grill

**Address:** NW 27th Ave (across from Walmart Supercenter), Miami Gardens, FL

![Served Table With Various Delicious Dish 3](/images/blog/2028/06/10-best-restaurants-to-try-in-miami-gardens-after-your-move/served-table-with-various-delicious-dish-3.webp)


A cozy eatery near Hard Rock Stadium featuring a diverse menu of Jamaican, Bahamian, and American dishes with a focus on fresh seafood. A neighborhood gem.

## 2. Sunday's Eatery

**Address:** Miami Gardens, FL

Owned by Miami's own Trick Daddy, this soul food restaurant serves up comfort classics with celebrity flair. The menu features Southern favorites that have earned a loyal following among Miami Gardens residents.

## 3. Awash Ethiopian Restaurant

**Address:** Miami Gardens, FL

A casual dining spot serving authentic Ethiopian cuisine with an abundance of rare spices. Best paired with their imported Ethiopian beer and wine in a welcoming atmosphere.

## 4. Sheri Nigerian Restaurant

**Address:** Miami Gardens, FL

One of the very few places to find authentic Nigerian food in Miami. A unique culinary experience for those looking to explore West African cuisine.

## 5. Sweet Hand Kathy's

**Address:** Miami Gardens, FL

A Trinidadian restaurant known for amazing doubles and perfectly stewed chicken. Authentic Caribbean flavors that transport you to the islands.

## 6. L.C.'s Roti Shop

**Address:** Miami Gardens, FL

A classic Trinidadian roti shop where you can get your roti filled with just about anything. Perfect for a filling, flavorful lunch.

## 7. Yarumba Restaurant and Lounge

**Address:** Miami Gardens, FL

![Sophisticated Dining Setup With Gourmet  2](/images/blog/2028/06/10-best-restaurants-to-try-in-miami-gardens-after-your-move/sophisticated-dining-setup-with-gourmet--2.webp)


A Dominican restaurant featuring all the noteworthy traditional dishes. From mofongo to sancocho, experience the best of Dominican cuisine.

## 8. Arline's Caribbean Soul Food

**Address:** Miami Gardens, FL

A long-time local favorite serving seriously delicious down-home cooking and seafood. Their chicken and waffles menu section is legendary.

## 9. Stadium Diner

**Address:** Near Hard Rock Stadium, Miami Gardens, FL

A classic American diner with really good pancakes and hearty breakfast options. Located near Hard Rock Stadium, it's a popular spot before Dolphins games or weekend brunch with the family.

## 10. Tony's Pizza

**Address:** Miami Gardens, FL

A local pizzeria serving pizzas with heavy toppings and crispy crusts. A neighborhood staple for Italian-American comfort food.

## Settling Into Miami Gardens

Miami Gardens sits in the heart of North Miami-Dade, just north of Opa-locka and west of North Miami Beach. The city's diverse population has created one of the most eclectic food scenes in the county. Unlike the trendy spots in Wynwood or upscale dining in Coral Gables, Miami Gardens restaurants focus on authentic, family-style cooking that reflects the Caribbean and African heritage of the community.

When you're ready to explore beyond your new neighborhood, you'll find easy access to Aventura Mall, the beaches of Sunny Isles, and downtown Miami via I-95. But with food this good right in your backyard, you might not want to leave.
