Whether you're moving to Brickell, settling into a Coral Gables bungalow, or starting fresh in Wynwood, your first day in a new Miami home sets the tone for everything that follows. Here are six essential tasks to complete before you unpack that first box.

## 1. Change All the Locks

You never know who has copies of the previous owner's keys. In busy Miami neighborhoods like Coconut Grove and Little Havana, this is especially important. Contact a local locksmith or visit Home Depot on Coral Way for quick rekeying services.

![Cheerful Young Man And Woman Smiling Whi 2](/images/blog/2025/12/6-day-one-tasks-for-your-new-residence/cheerful-young-man-and-woman-smiling-whi-2.webp)


## 2. Test All Appliances

Run the dishwasher, check the stove, and test the washer/dryer. Miami's humidity can take a toll on appliances, so check the AC unit carefully. Identify any problems while warranty claims are still fresh.

## 3. Locate Emergency Shutoffs

Find and label the water main, electrical panel, and gas shutoff. During hurricane season, you will need to know these locations quickly. Better to find them now than scramble during a storm warning.

## 4. Set Up Mail Forwarding

Visit USPS.com to ensure nothing gets lost during the transition period. This should happen before you move.

## 5. Transfer Utilities to Your Name

Contact FPL for electricity, Miami-Dade Water and Sewer for water, and your preferred internet provider. Confirm all accounts are active and in your name before move-in day to avoid service interruptions.

![Young Adults Signing Documents On Moving 4](/images/blog/2025/12/6-day-one-tasks-for-your-new-residence/young-adults-signing-documents-on-moving-4.webp)


## 6. Schedule Critical Installations

Book your internet, cable, and security system installations early. In fast-growing areas like Doral and Aventura, installation windows fill up quickly. AT&T, Xfinity, and ADT all serve the Miami metro area.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Should I change the locks even if I have all the keys?**
Yes. Previous owners may have given copies to neighbors, service providers, or family members. A fresh start with new locks gives you peace of mind.

**What if an appliance fails after move-in?**
Document the issue immediately with photos and contact the seller or your home warranty provider. Most warranties require notification within 30 days of closing.

**How long does mail forwarding take to activate?**
USPS mail forwarding typically takes 7-10 business days to process, so set it up at least a week before your move.
