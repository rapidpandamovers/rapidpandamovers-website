## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does a PCS move cost in October?
Local moves in Miami-Dade typically cost $500-$1,500. Interstate moves range from $3,000-$8,000 depending on distance and weight. PPM reimbursement rates apply if you choose a personally procured move.

### Is October a good time for a military move in Miami?
October marks the end of hurricane season and the start of cooler weather. Demand is lower than peak PCS season (May-September), which can mean better availability and scheduling flexibility.
