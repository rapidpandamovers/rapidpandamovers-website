## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does a same building move cost in Miami?

![Young Woman Moving Boxes Out Of An Apart 3](/images/blog/2026/08/fall-same-building-moving-insights-for-miami/young-woman-moving-boxes-out-of-an-apart-3.webp)


Same building moves typically cost $200-$800 depending on unit size and floor changes. Since no truck is needed, costs are lower than standard moves. Most same-building moves are billed hourly at $100-$200 per hour for a two-person crew.

### Do I need to coordinate with building management?

Yes. Most Miami high-rises require elevator reservations, COI documentation, and advance notice of 48-72 hours. Some buildings charge move-in/move-out fees. Contact your building management early to avoid scheduling conflicts.

### How long does a same building move take?

A one-bedroom move within the same building typically takes 1-3 hours. Larger units may take 3-5 hours. Elevator wait times and building policies can affect duration.
