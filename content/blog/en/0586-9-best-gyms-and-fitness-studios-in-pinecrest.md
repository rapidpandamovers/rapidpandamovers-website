Just moved to Pinecrest or planning your relocation to this family-friendly South Miami-Dade neighborhood? Whether you're a CrossFit enthusiast, yoga devotee, or just looking to stay active, Pinecrest offers excellent fitness options. Located between Coral Gables and Kendall along the US-1 corridor, this leafy suburb makes staying fit convenient. Here are nine gyms and studios worth checking out.

## 1. Paradise Gym

**12900 SW 89th Court, Miami, FL 33176**

![Close Up Of Pilates Ultra Fit Circles Ne 1](/images/blog/2026/11/9-best-gyms-and-fitness-studios-in-pinecrest/close-up-of-pilates-ultra-fit-circles-ne-1.webp)


This massive 9,000 square foot facility is a haven for serious athletes. The space features turf, CrossFit rigs, powerlifting equipment, and Olympic lifting stations. Round-the-clock classes accommodate any schedule, with both monthly and annual membership options.

## 2. SWEAT440 Pinecrest

**Colonial Palms Plaza, US Route 1**

With classes starting every 10 minutes, you're never late. The 40-minute workout flows through four 10-minute stations designed for efficiency and results. Located near Barnes and Noble with convenient parking, it's perfect for fitting fitness into a busy schedule.

## 3. Life Time Miami at the Falls

**8778 SW 136th Street, Miami, FL 33176**

This comprehensive facility offers personal training, group fitness classes, and state-of-the-art equipment. The unique outdoor poolside workout area hosts aquatics classes, a refreshing option given Miami's year-round warm weather.

## 4. INFINITY Beyond Fitness - Pinecrest

**7890 SW 104th Street, Suite B101, Miami, FL 33156**

![Detailed View Of An Exercise Bike Pedal  2](/images/blog/2026/11/9-best-gyms-and-fitness-studios-in-pinecrest/detailed-view-of-an-exercise-bike-pedal--2.webp)


Dubbed "Miami's leading transformation studio," INFINITY has developed a loyal following. The facility combines training, nutrition, and recovery for complete wellness. Members rave about the results-focused approach and supportive community atmosphere that keeps them accountable.

## 5. Peak 360 Fitness

**6802 SW 81st Street, Miami, FL 33143**

This highly-rated gym focuses on personalized training approaches with CrossFit programming. The attentive staff and smaller class sizes mean more individualized attention, perfect for newcomers wanting proper form guidance.

## 6. Miami Strength and Fitness Club

**8855 SW 131st Street, Miami, FL 33176**

A local favorite for strength training enthusiasts, this gym offers a no-frills approach focused on results. The knowledgeable staff and serious training environment attract dedicated fitness enthusiasts.

## 7. Pure Barre Pinecrest

**11349 S Dixie Hwy, Pinecrest, FL 33156**

![Modern Gym With Multiple Exercise Bikes  4](/images/blog/2026/11/9-best-gyms-and-fitness-studios-in-pinecrest/modern-gym-with-multiple-exercise-bikes--4.webp)


This boutique studio offers low-impact, high-intensity workouts focused on sculpting. The barre-based classes are particularly popular with residents looking for effective workouts that are gentle on joints.

## 8. Hotworx Pinecrest

**Colonial Palms Plaza**

Using infrared heat technology, Hotworx enhances traditional workouts including hot yoga, Pilates, and HIIT sessions. The 24-hour access accommodates any schedule, and the unique approach yields impressive results.

## 9. Body and Brain Yoga Tai Chi

**9727 S Dixie Hwy, Pinecrest, FL 33156**

For a holistic approach to wellness, this studio combines yoga, tai chi, and meditation practices. The emphasis on mind-body connection provides balance for busy professionals adjusting to a new community.
