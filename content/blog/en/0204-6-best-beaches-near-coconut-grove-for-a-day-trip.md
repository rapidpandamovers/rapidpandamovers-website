Living near Coconut Grove means easy access to some of Florida's best beaches. Whether you're looking for family-friendly shores, water sports, or quiet relaxation, here are six beaches worth the drive.

## 1. Matheson Hammock Park Beach

Just 10 minutes from Coconut Grove, this is the closest beach option for Grove residents. The man-made atoll pool creates calm, shallow water that's perfect for families with young children. Full amenities include showers, restrooms, and picnic pavilions. The Red Fish Grill on-site serves lunch with waterfront views.

![A Tranquil Boardwalk At Sunset Framed By 1](/images/blog/2025/04/6-best-beaches-near-coconut-grove-for-a-day-trip/a-tranquil-boardwalk-at-sunset-framed-by-1.webp)


## 2. Crandon Park Beach

Head 25 minutes east via the Rickenbacker Causeway to Key Biscayne for one of South Florida's best beaches. Two miles of golden sand, calm waters protected by a sandbar, and excellent facilities make this worth the drive. Parking fills early on weekends, so arrive before 9am. Lifeguards are on duty year-round.

## 3. Bill Baggs Cape Florida State Park

At the southern tip of Key Biscayne, about 30 minutes from the Grove, this state park offers a quieter alternative to Crandon. The historic lighthouse provides a scenic backdrop, and the beach has a more natural feel. Rent kayaks or paddleboards to explore the mangrove shoreline. The park has full facilities and a casual restaurant.

## 4. Virginia Key Beach

The closest beach to Coconut Grove that feels like an escape. Just 15 minutes away, Virginia Key attracts a mellower crowd than South Beach. The water can be choppier here, but the trade-off is fewer crowds and easier parking. Basic amenities available.

## 5. Hollywood Beach

Head 40 minutes north on I-95 to find the Broadwalk, a 2.5-mile promenade along the ocean. The family-friendly vibe, variety of restaurants, and wide beach make this a great full-day destination. Less trendy than South Beach but more space to spread out.

![Scenic View Of Miami Beach With Palm Tre 4](/images/blog/2025/04/6-best-beaches-near-coconut-grove-for-a-day-trip/scenic-view-of-miami-beach-with-palm-tre-4.webp)


## 6. Bahia Honda State Park

For the ultimate beach day, drive 2.5 hours south on US-1 to the Florida Keys. Bahia Honda consistently ranks among Florida's best beaches, with crystal-clear water and pristine sand. Pack a cooler since amenities are limited. The drive through the Keys is half the adventure.
