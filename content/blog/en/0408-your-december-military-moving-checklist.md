## Key Tips for Military Moving in December

### Plan Ahead for Peak Times

December can be a busy time for moving companies. With winter activities and seasonal transitions, scheduling your military moving early ensures you get your preferred date and time.

### Keep all documentation organized for reimbursement purposes

This single step can save hours of stress and potential problems on moving day. Our experienced crews have seen what happens when this step is skipped.

### Consider the Weather

South Florida's winter weather offers ideal moving conditions. December humidity is lower than summer months, typically around 60-65%, and rain is infrequent. Occasional cold fronts can bring brief temperature drops, but overall conditions are comfortable for moving. Professional movers still take precautions, wrapping furniture in moving blankets and using climate-appropriate packing materials to protect against any moisture.

### Prepare Your Belongings

Take time to inventory your items before the move. This is especially important for military moving, as proper documentation helps ensure everything arrives safely at your new location.
