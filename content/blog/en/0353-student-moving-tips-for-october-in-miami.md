## Key Tips for Student Moving in October

### Book Early for Weekend Availability

Weekends fill up fast for student moves, especially around midterms and housing changeover dates. Scheduling your student move at least two weeks ahead gives you the best selection of dates and times.

### Book Early During Peak Move-In Weekends at Universities

This single step can save hours of stress and potential problems on moving day. Our experienced crews have seen what happens when this step is skipped.

### Watch the Afternoon Forecast

South Florida's October weather still brings daily afternoon storms. Plan your move for the morning hours to avoid rain delays. Professional movers know how to protect your belongings from humidity and sudden downpours during transport.

### Prepare Your Belongings

Take time to inventory your items before the move. This is especially important for student moving, as proper documentation helps ensure everything arrives safely at your new location.
