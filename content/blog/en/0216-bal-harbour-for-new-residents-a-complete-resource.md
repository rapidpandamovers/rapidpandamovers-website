Moving to Bal Harbour? This tiny village of just 0.27 square miles packs serious luxury into a small footprint. Whether you're relocating from Aventura down the road or flying in from out of state, here's what to know about your new home.

## Why Choose Bal Harbour?

Bal Harbour is one of the smallest municipalities in Florida, but it's also one of the wealthiest. The village sits at the northern tip of Miami Beach, bordered by Surfside to the south and Haulover Park to the north.

![Elegant Bottle Of Byredo Bal D Afrique P 3](/images/blog/2025/05/bal-harbour-for-new-residents-a-complete-resource/elegant-bottle-of-byredo-bal-d-afrique-p-3.webp)


The Bal Harbour Shops draw visitors from around the world. This open-air mall features Chanel, Gucci, Prada, and dozens of other high-end retailers. Carpaccio and Makoto are popular spots for lunch between shopping trips.

Beyond the shops, Bal Harbour offers pristine beach access, low crime rates, and a quiet residential feel that's hard to find elsewhere in Miami-Dade.

### Location and Getting Around

Collins Avenue (A1A) runs through the center of Bal Harbour. You're about 20 minutes from Downtown Miami via I-95 and 15 minutes from Aventura Mall. Miami International Airport is roughly 30 minutes away, depending on traffic.

The village runs a free trolley service along Collins Avenue. Many residents also use the Miami-Dade Transit buses that connect to Aventura and points south.

### Community and Lifestyle

Bal Harbour hosts regular events at the shops and beach areas. The annual holiday lighting ceremony at Bal Harbour Shops is a local tradition. The village also maintains Bal Harbour Beach Park, where residents can enjoy beach access with lifeguards and restroom facilities.

## Where to Live in Bal Harbour

Most Bal Harbour housing consists of luxury high-rise condos along the oceanfront. Buildings like The St. Regis, Oceana, and One Bal Harbour offer ocean views, concierge services, and resort-style amenities.

### What to Expect

- **Condo living**: Most residents live in high-rises with HOAs
- **Price points**: Entry-level condos start around $500K, with penthouses in the tens of millions
- **Building rules**: Many buildings have strict move-in requirements, including reserved elevator times and insurance certificates
- **Parking**: Most buildings include garage parking, but visitor spots can be limited

## Moving to Bal Harbour: Timing Tips

May through September brings afternoon thunderstorms to South Florida. Plan morning moves when possible to beat the rain and the heat.

![Elegant Byredo Bal D Afrique Perfume Env 1](/images/blog/2025/05/bal-harbour-for-new-residents-a-complete-resource/elegant-byredo-bal-d-afrique-perfume-env-1.webp)


### What to Know Before Moving Day

- **Building coordination**: Contact your condo management at least two weeks ahead to reserve the service elevator
- **Certificate of Insurance**: Your building will likely require a COI from your moving company
- **Collins Avenue traffic**: Avoid moving during rush hours (8-9 AM and 5-7 PM) when Collins gets backed up
- **Loading zones**: Some buildings have designated loading areas; others require street parking

## Services Near Bal Harbour

New residents should know these nearby spots:

- **Healthcare**: Aventura Hospital and Medical Center is 10 minutes north. Mount Sinai Medical Center is 15 minutes south in Miami Beach
- **Groceries**: Publix in Surfside or Aventura. Whole Foods in North Miami Beach
- **Schools**: Ruth K. Broad Bay Harbor Elementary (public) and private options like Cushman School are nearby
- **Fitness**: Equinox at Bal Harbour Shops, plus building gyms in most condos
