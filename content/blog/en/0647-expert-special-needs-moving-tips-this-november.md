## Key Tips for Special Needs Moving in November

### Plan Ahead for Peak Times

November can be a busy time for moving companies. With snowbirds arriving and seasonal transitions, scheduling your special needs moving early ensures you get your preferred date and time.

### Communicate specific needs and any medical equipment requirements in advance

This single step can save hours of stress and potential problems on moving day. Our experienced crews have seen what happens when this step is skipped.

### Consider the Weather

South Florida's November weather is dry and comfortable, with minimal rain and low humidity compared to summer months. Professional movers know how to protect your belongings and medical equipment during transport, and the mild conditions make it easier to keep medical devices at stable temperatures.

### Prepare Your Belongings

Take time to inventory your items before the move. This is especially important for special needs moving, as proper documentation helps ensure everything arrives safely at your new location.

### How much does special needs moving cost in Miami?

Special needs moves typically cost 20-30% more than standard moves due to additional time, equipment, and care required. A local move within Miami-Dade with medical equipment usually ranges from $800-$1,500 depending on home size and equipment complexity.

### How do you handle medical equipment during a move?

We coordinate with your medical team and equipment suppliers. Life-support equipment gets set up first at the new location. We use climate-controlled transport for temperature-sensitive devices and keep essential medications accessible throughout the move.

### Can you accommodate wheelchair-accessible needs during the move?

Yes. Our crews assess both locations for accessibility before moving day, including ramp availability, doorway widths, and elevator access. We prioritize setting up wheelchair paths and grab bars at the new home before other items.
