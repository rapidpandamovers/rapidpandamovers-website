## Why Antique Moving Requires Special Care in August

August is the heart of Miami's wet season and one of the busiest moving months in South Florida. Moving companies book out weeks ahead, and afternoon thunderstorms roll in like clockwork between 3pm and 6pm. For antiques, this timing creates real challenges: humidity spikes can damage wood finishes in hours, and rain delays mean valuable pieces sit longer in un-climate-controlled trucks.

## Key Tips for Antique Moving in August

### Book Your Move Two to Three Weeks Out

Late August is packed with back-to-school moves and lease turnovers across Miami-Dade. If you are moving antiques from Brickell to Kendall or relocating a family collection from Miami Beach to Doral, reserve your crew early. Last-minute bookings often mean inexperienced teams or compromised scheduling.

### Document Everything Before the Truck Arrives

Photograph each antique from multiple angles, including existing scratches, wear marks, and hardware details. Get appraisals for valuable pieces. This documentation protects you if insurance claims become necessary. Our crews have seen families lose thousands in claims simply because they lacked proof of pre-move condition.

### Time Your Move for Miami Weather

Schedule loading for early morning, ideally before 9am. By afternoon, Miami humidity hits 80% or higher, and those 3pm thunderstorms can delay everything. If you are moving from an Aventura high-rise to a Homestead estate, factor in the drive time and plan to have everything unloaded before the weather turns.

### Prepare Your Antiques for Miami's Climate

Wooden antiques need climate control during transport. In neighborhoods like Wynwood or Little Havana where buildings may lack central AC, get pieces wrapped and loaded quickly. Consider plastic bins with silica gel packets for smaller valuables that might sit in the truck.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does antique moving cost in Miami?

Antique moving typically costs 20-40% more than standard moving due to specialized packing materials, custom crating, and climate-controlled transport. For a typical Coral Gables home with 10-15 antique pieces, expect $500-$1,500 above standard moving rates.

### Can antiques be moved during Miami's rainy season?

Yes, but timing matters. We schedule loading and unloading around the predictable afternoon storms. Our climate-controlled trucks protect pieces during transport regardless of outside conditions.

### Do I need separate insurance for antique moving?

We recommend full valuation coverage for irreplaceable pieces. Standard moving insurance covers items by weight, not value. An antique writing desk worth $5,000 might only get $300 at standard rates.
