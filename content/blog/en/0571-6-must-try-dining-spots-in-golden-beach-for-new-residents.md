Just moved to Golden Beach or planning your February relocation? As one of Miami-Dade's most exclusive residential communities, Golden Beach offers privacy and oceanfront luxury. Dining options require a short drive to neighboring areas, but that's part of the charm. Here are the must-try spots nearby that locals love.

## 1. Il Mulino New York at Acqualina Resort

**17875 Collins Avenue, Sunny Isles Beach** | (305) 466-9191

![Close Up Of A Seafood Platter With Fresh 2](/images/blog/2026/08/6-must-try-dining-spots-in-golden-beach-for-new-residents/close-up-of-a-seafood-platter-with-fresh-2.webp)


This acclaimed Italian restaurant has been rated the #1 Italian restaurant on the New York City Zagat Survey for 20 years and holds a Forbes Travel Guide Recommended Award. The oceanfront terrace offers stunning views, and their three-course lunch special at $35 per person is a local favorite. Don't miss the Piccata di Vitello al Limone.

## 2. Artisan Beach House at The Ritz-Carlton Bal Harbour

**10295 Collins Avenue, Bal Harbour** | (305) 455-5460

At the intersection of the Atlantic Ocean and the Intracoastal Waterway, Artisan Beach House celebrates centuries of fishing tradition with the freshest seafood. The stunning water views, craft cocktails, and locally-sourced ingredients make this perfect for a romantic evening or special occasion just minutes from Golden Beach.

## 3. BALEENkitchen at Sole Miami

**17315 Collins Avenue, Sunny Isles Beach** | (786) 923-9305

![Exquisite Seafood Presentation With Crab 1](/images/blog/2026/08/6-must-try-dining-spots-in-golden-beach-for-new-residents/exquisite-seafood-presentation-with-crab-1.webp)


Inspired by the nomadic Baleen whale, this restaurant brings global flavors to the table. The eclectic menu features small plates, chilled shellfish, grilled items, and pizzas. Arrive early for their unique cocktails and Happy Hour specials. Complimentary valet parking makes it an easy choice.

## 4. JOEY Aventura

**19505 Biscayne Blvd, Aventura** | (786) 763-2300

The first JOEY Restaurant in Florida spans two stunning levels with a large patio. The menu takes inspiration from various international cuisines, including fire-torched sushi, premium steaks, and comforting bowls. Try the Szechuan lettuce wraps or the Sake-Glazed Chilean Sea Bass. Open until midnight most nights.

## 5. Juniper On The Water

**1975 S Ocean Drive, Hallandale Beach** | (954) 544-3370

![Luxurious Seafood Platter Featuring Cavi 4](/images/blog/2026/08/6-must-try-dining-spots-in-golden-beach-for-new-residents/luxurious-seafood-platter-featuring-cavi-4.webp)


This coastal cuisine gem on the Intracoastal waterway is hailed as South Florida's hidden gem. The covered outdoor terrace offers gorgeous sunset views, and the Seafood Paella with toasted Israeli couscous is a signature dish. Perfect for a relaxed, upscale dinner with waterfront ambiance.

## 6. El Tropico Cuban Cuisine

**17020 Collins Avenue, Sunny Isles Beach**

For authentic Cuban comfort food, El Tropico is the local go-to. Open from 8 AM to 10 PM most days (11 PM Saturdays), they serve classics like Ropa Vieja, Cubano sandwiches, Pollo a la Plancha, and the best Arroz Con Leche in the area. Perfect for a casual meal that won't break the bank.
