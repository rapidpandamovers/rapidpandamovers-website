## Popular Miami Neighborhoods for Celebrity Relocations

Miami attracts celebrities and high-net-worth individuals to several exclusive areas. Star Island and Fisher Island offer waterfront estates with maximum privacy and security. Indian Creek Village, known as "Billionaire Bunker," features 24/7 police patrols and limited access. Coral Gables provides historic Mediterranean-style mansions near the Biltmore Hotel, while Coconut Grove offers a more bohemian luxury feel with tree-lined streets. Miami Beach's North Beach and Surfside areas have seen increased celebrity interest due to their quieter atmosphere compared to South Beach.
