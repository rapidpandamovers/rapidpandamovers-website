Welcome to your guide for moving to South Miami! This charming city offers suburban tranquility, urban convenience, and strong community character just minutes from Downtown Miami.

## About South Miami

South Miami is an incorporated city within Miami-Dade County, located about 8 miles southwest of Downtown Miami. With approximately 13,200 residents, this growing community offers tree-lined streets, local shops, excellent schools, and easy access to major employment centers.

![Colorful Miami Mural Featuring E11Even V 2](/images/blog/2026/06/why-south-miami-is-perfect-for-your-next-move/colorful-miami-mural-featuring-e11even-v-2.webp)


### Demographics and Community

- **Population**: Approximately 13,207 (2026 estimate)
- **Growth Rate**: 5.94% annually - one of the fastest-growing in the area
- **Median Age**: 39.9 years
- **Diversity**: Hispanic (52.4%), White (27.5%), Black (9.5%)
- **Median Household Income**: $88,313

## Neighborhoods to Consider

### City Center
The heart of South Miami with walkable streets, shops, restaurants, and professional offices.

### South Miami West
Residential area with mature trees and established single-family homes. Popular with families.

### Ludlam
Mixed residential neighborhood with convenient access to US-1 and major thoroughfares.

### Glenvar Heights East
Adjacent community with larger lots and more space for families wanting room to grow.

## Schools and Education

### Notable Public Schools
- **Southside Elementary School**: Well-regarded elementary option
- **Shenandoah Middle School**: Strong middle school serving the area
- **Miami Senior High School**: Major high school with diverse programs

### Higher Education
- **University of Miami**: Just minutes away in Coral Gables
- **Miami-Dade College**: Multiple campuses in the region

## Housing Market

### Home Prices (2025)
- **Median Home Price**: $1.1 million (up 9.8% year-over-year)
- **Price per Square Foot**: $637 (up 15.9%)
- **Average Days on Market**: 176 days
- **Market Type**: Currently favors buyers

### Market Considerations
- South Miami's median price is 153% higher than the national average
- 63% of properties have some flood risk - consider insurance needs

## Major Employers

### Healthcare Sector
- **Jackson Memorial Hospital**: Major medical center employer
- **Miami VA Healthcare System**: Significant employer
- **University of Miami Health System**: Research and medical facilities

### Other Industries
Education, Professional Services, Retail and Hospitality, Construction and Real Estate

## Transportation

- **US-1 (South Dixie Highway)**: Primary north-south corridor
- **Metrorail**: South Miami station provides direct access to Downtown, Brickell, and the airport
- **Commute Times**: Downtown (20-25 min), Brickell (15-20 min), MIA (15-20 min), Coral Gables (5-10 min)

## Shopping and Dining

- Local boutiques and shops along Sunset Drive
- **Dadeland Mall**: Major shopping destination minutes away
- **Merrick Park**: Upscale shopping in Coral Gables
- Diverse dining from casual to upscale
