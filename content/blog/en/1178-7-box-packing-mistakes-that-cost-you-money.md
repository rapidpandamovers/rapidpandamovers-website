Packing seems straightforward until something breaks. These common summer errors are responsible for the majority of moving damage we see across Miami-Dade County. Avoid them and your belongings will arrive safely, whether you're relocating from Brickell to Aventura or moving within Doral.

## 1. Overpacking Large Boxes

A huge box of books is impossible to lift safely. Use small boxes for heavy items. Always. Your back and your movers will thank you. We see this constantly when helping families move from Coral Gables condos to Kendall townhomes.

![Crop Unrecognizable Man In Casual Clothe 4](/images/blog/2028/10/7-box-packing-mistakes-that-cost-you-money/crop-unrecognizable-man-in-casual-clothe-4.webp)


## 2. Insufficient Cushioning

One layer of newspaper isn't enough. Fragile items need individual wrapping, padding on all sides, and zero room to shift.

## 3. Leaving Hollow Items Empty

Hollow items like vases and cups are fragile. Fill them with packing paper or soft materials for structural support.

![Labeled Cardboard Boxes For Summer Cloth 3](/images/blog/2028/10/7-box-packing-mistakes-that-cost-you-money/labeled-cardboard-boxes-for-summer-cloth-3.webp)


## 4. Using Old, Weak Boxes

Old boxes lose structural integrity. Wet boxes are disasters waiting to happen, especially in Miami's humid climate. Use fresh, sturdy boxes from a moving supply store. This is particularly important during our rainy summer months when humidity can weaken cardboard overnight.

## 5. Improper Dish Packing

Never stack plates horizontally. Pack them on edge, like records, with padding between each one. This prevents cracking from weight.

## 6. Not Labeling Boxes Clearly

Generic labels waste time. Write the room destination and a brief contents list on multiple sides of each box for easy identification.

![Side View Of Crop Anonymous Man Packing  1](/images/blog/2028/10/7-box-packing-mistakes-that-cost-you-money/side-view-of-crop-anonymous-man-packing--1.webp)


## 7. Packing Liquids Without Sealing

Shampoo, cleaning supplies, and other liquids need their lids taped and then placed in sealed plastic bags. Miami's summer heat can cause pressure changes in bottles, leading to unexpected leaks. We've seen toiletries ruin entire boxes of clothes during moves from Pinecrest to Coconut Grove.

## Benefits of Proper Packing

When you pack correctly, you'll experience:

- **Faster unpacking**: Labeled, organized boxes mean you can set up your new place in hours instead of days
- **Lower moving costs**: Properly sized boxes mean more efficient truck loading and fewer trips
- **Insurance compliance**: Items packed to professional standards are fully covered if anything goes wrong
- **Less stress**: Knowing your belongings are secure lets you focus on the excitement of your new home

## What to Expect on Moving Day

With properly packed boxes, your moving day will go smoothly:

1. **Quick loading**: Uniform boxes stack efficiently, speeding up the process
2. **Safe transport**: Secure items don't shift in the truck
3. **Easy unloading**: Clear labels mean boxes go directly to the right rooms
4. **Simple unpacking**: Organized packing makes finding essentials immediate
