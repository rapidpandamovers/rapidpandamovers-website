## Key Tips for Packing Services in May

### Plan Ahead for Peak Times

May can be a busy time for moving companies. With the start of summer moving season and lease turnovers, scheduling your packing services early ensures you get your preferred date and time.

### Start packing non-essentials 4-6 weeks before moving day

This single step can save hours of stress and potential problems on moving day. Our experienced crews have seen what happens when this step is skipped.

### Consider the Weather

South Florida's summer weather is predictable in some ways and surprising in others. Those daily 3 PM thunderstorms rolling in from the Everglades are almost guaranteed in May. Professional movers know how to protect your belongings from humidity, rain, and heat exposure during transport. We often schedule morning moves to beat the afternoon storms, especially for moves in exposed areas like Miami Beach or Key Biscayne.

### Prepare Your Belongings

Take time to inventory your items before the move. This is especially important for packing services, as proper documentation helps ensure everything arrives safely at your new location.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much do packing services cost in Miami?

Professional packing services in Miami typically cost $300-$800 for a one-bedroom apartment and $800-$2,000 for a three-bedroom house. This includes labor and all packing materials (boxes, tape, bubble wrap, paper).

### How long does professional packing take?

A one-bedroom apartment usually takes 2-3 hours to pack. A three-bedroom house takes 4-8 hours depending on the amount of belongings. Kitchens and offices with fragile items take the most time.

### Should I pack anything myself before the crew arrives?

Pack personal items, medications, and valuables you want to transport yourself. Leave everything else for the professional crew. They pack faster, use proper techniques for fragile items, and their work is covered by the moving company's insurance.
