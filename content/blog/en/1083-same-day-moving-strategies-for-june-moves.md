## Key Strategies for Same Day Moving in June

### Call as Early as Possible

Call by 7 AM for the best chance of securing a crew. Morning slots disappear fast during June, especially for moves in Brickell, Coral Gables, Kendall, and other high-demand areas.

### Simplify Your Packing

With same-day notice, skip the color-coded labeling system. Use garbage bags for clothes on hangers. Leave items in drawers for short-distance moves. Pack breakables in towels and linens rather than waiting for bubble wrap.

### Plan Around Afternoon Storms

Loading should be complete by early afternoon whenever possible. June thunderstorms typically hit between 2 PM and 6 PM. If storms are unavoidable, our crews have waterproof covers and will pause loading during heavy rain rather than risk damage.

### Confirm Building Access Quickly

For Brickell condos, Miami Beach high-rises, and Aventura towers, call building management immediately. Some buildings require 24-hour notice for elevator reservations, though many will accommodate same-day moves during regular business hours.
