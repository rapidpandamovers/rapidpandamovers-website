## Key Tips for Last Minute Moving in December

### Plan Ahead for Peak Times

December can be a busy time for moving companies. With holiday schedules and year-end lease changes, scheduling your last minute moving early ensures you get your preferred date and time. Miami's popular areas like Coconut Grove, South Beach, and Doral see heavy moving traffic this time of year.

### Prioritize Must-Move Items

When time is short, focus on what matters most. Identify essentials you need immediately at your new place: medications, important documents, chargers, toiletries, and a change of clothes. Pack these in a clearly labeled "Open First" box. Let the movers handle the rest. Accept help with packing if offered. We've seen clients save three or four hours by letting our crews handle boxes while they focus on coordinating logistics.

### Consider the Weather

South Florida's December weather is mild and dry, with temperatures averaging 68-78°F and minimal rain. Professional movers know how to protect your belongings from humidity, rain, and heat exposure during transport. They also know which buildings have covered loading docks, like many in Downtown Miami and Edgewater.

### Prepare Your Belongings

Take time to inventory your items before the move. This is especially important for last minute moving, as proper documentation helps ensure everything arrives safely at your new location.
