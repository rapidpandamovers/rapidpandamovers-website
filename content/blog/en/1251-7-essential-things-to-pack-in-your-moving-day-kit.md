Your moving day kit stays with you throughout the move, not on the truck. Whether you're moving from Brickell to Coral Gables or heading to a new Kendall home, these essentials keep you prepared for anything Miami throws at you.

## 1. Important Documents

Passports, IDs, birth certificates, and lease or closing papers should stay on your person or in your personal vehicle. Never pack these in a moving box.

![A Man Labels Cardboard Boxes For Moving  1](/images/blog/2029/01/7-essential-things-to-pack-in-your-moving-day-kit/a-man-labels-cardboard-boxes-for-moving--1.webp)


## 2. Medications and First Aid

Current prescriptions, over-the-counter essentials, and any medical devices you might need in the next 24 hours stay with you. In Miami's heat, also pack sunscreen and any allergy medications if you're sensitive to tropical pollen.

## 3. Phone Chargers and Power Banks

Dead phones on moving day mean missed calls from movers, utility companies, and anyone trying to help coordinate.

![Crop Faceless Male Sealing Packed Carton 3](/images/blog/2029/01/7-essential-things-to-pack-in-your-moving-day-kit/crop-faceless-male-sealing-packed-carton-3.webp)


## 4. Basic Toolkit

A screwdriver set, pliers, box cutter, and hammer cover most last-minute needs at both locations. You'll use these more than you expect.

## 5. Cleaning Supplies

Paper towels, all-purpose cleaner, and trash bags handle quick clean-ups during the transition at both old and new locations.

![Hands Carefully Packing Glassware Into A 5](/images/blog/2029/01/7-essential-things-to-pack-in-your-moving-day-kit/hands-carefully-packing-glassware-into-a-5.webp)


## 6. First-Night Box

Toiletries, towels, bedding, change of clothes, and anything you'll need to sleep comfortably night one without unpacking everything.

## 7. Snacks and Water

Moving is exhausting work, especially during Miami summers when temperatures hit the 90s. Keep energy bars, plenty of water bottles, and quick snacks accessible for yourself and anyone helping. Staying hydrated is non-negotiable here.
