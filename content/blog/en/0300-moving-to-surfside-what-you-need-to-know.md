Surfside continues to attract new residents from across the country during November, and it's easy to see why. As a premier Miami-Dade location, Surfside offers a unique blend of community, convenience, and lifestyle options.

## Why Choose Surfside?

Surfside stands out as one of Miami-Dade County's most appealing locations. The community offers a quiet beach town atmosphere and is particularly known for family beaches and small-town atmosphere.

![A Young Woman In A Bikini Smiles While T 2](/images/blog/2025/08/moving-to-surfside-what-you-need-to-know/a-young-woman-in-a-bikini-smiles-while-t-2.webp)


The area attracts families, professionals, and retirees alike, thanks to its quality of life, convenient access to major employment centers, and excellent amenities.

### Location and Accessibility

One of Surfside's biggest advantages is its strategic location within Miami-Dade County. The town sits between Bal Harbour to the north and Miami Beach to the south along Collins Avenue. Residents enjoy easy access to I-95 via 96th Street and the Haulover Beach area. The Miami Beach S Route bus runs along Collins Avenue, connecting Surfside to Aventura and South Beach. Downtown Miami is about 25 minutes away, and Miami International Airport is a 30-minute drive.

### Community and Lifestyle

Surfside residents enjoy a strong sense of community. The Surfside Community Center on Collins Avenue hosts fitness classes, youth programs, and community gatherings. 93rd Street Beach is a family favorite with its calm waters and lifeguard stations. Local favorites like Josh's Deli on Harding Avenue and Café Ragazzi bring neighbors together. The town hosts seasonal events at Surfside Park, including outdoor movie nights and holiday celebrations.

## Neighborhoods to Consider

When planning your move to Surfside, the Harding Avenue area is particularly popular with newcomers. Each neighborhood has its own character. Some offer walkable dining and shopping along 95th Street, while others provide quiet, tree-lined residential blocks near Surfside Community Center.

### Choosing Your Perfect Spot

Consider these factors:

- **Proximity to work and schools**: Factor in your daily commute
- **Local amenities**: Parks, shopping, dining, and entertainment options
- **Property types**: Single-family homes, condos, townhouses, or apartments
- **Community vibe**: Family-oriented, young professional, or mixed demographics
