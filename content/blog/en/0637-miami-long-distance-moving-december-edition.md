## Key Tips for Long Distance Moving in December

### Plan Ahead for Peak Times

December can be a busy time for moving companies. With Art Basel in Miami Beach, holiday parties in Wynwood, and seasonal snowbirds arriving in Aventura, scheduling your long distance moving early ensures you get your preferred date and time. Book at least three weeks ahead during the holiday season.

### Get a Binding Estimate to Lock in Your Moving Costs

This single step can save hours of stress and potential problems on moving day. Our experienced crews have seen what happens when this step is skipped. A binding estimate protects you from surprise charges, especially important when moving from Miami to cities like Atlanta, Charlotte, or New York.

### Consider the Weather

South Florida's winter weather is predictable in some ways and surprising in others. While December temperatures in Kendall and Doral hover around 75 degrees, cold fronts can bring sudden drops. Professional movers know how to protect your belongings from humidity changes during the drive north where temperatures can swing 40 degrees.

### Prepare Your Belongings

Take time to inventory your items before the move. This is especially important for long distance moving, as proper documentation helps ensure everything arrives safely at your new location. Create a detailed list with photos, particularly for valuable items like electronics, artwork, and furniture.
