## Key Tips for Last Minute Moving in May

### Plan Ahead for Peak Times

May can be a busy time for moving companies across Miami-Dade County. With Memorial Day activities, parades in Coral Gables, and beach events in South Beach, scheduling your last minute moving early ensures you get your preferred date and time. Many families in Brickell, Kendall, and Doral are also timing their moves around the end of the school year.

### Prioritize must-move items and accept help with packing to save time

This single step can save hours of stress and potential problems on moving day. Our experienced crews have seen what happens when this step is skipped.

### Consider the Weather

South Florida's spring weather is predictable in some ways and surprising in others. Late May brings the start of hurricane season and regular afternoon thunderstorms that can roll in from the Everglades. Professional movers know how to protect your belongings from Miami's humidity, sudden rain showers, and intense heat exposure during transport. Planning your move for the morning hours helps avoid the typical 3-5 PM storms.

### Prepare Your Belongings

Take time to inventory your items before the move. This is especially important for last minute moving, as proper documentation helps ensure everything arrives safely at your new location.
