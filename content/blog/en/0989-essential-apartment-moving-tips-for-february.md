February ranks among the busiest moving months in Miami-Dade County. Lease cycles end, families relocate before school starts, and everyone competes for the same elevator reservations. If you are planning an apartment move this month, start the logistics now.

## Why February Apartment Moves Get Complicated

Building management offices field dozens of move requests in February. Elevator reservations book up 2-3 weeks out. Some buildings restrict moves to weekday mornings only during peak winter months. And the high demand from snowbird season means every available slot fills quickly.

![Tall Modern Building With A Unique Red R 1](/images/blog/2028/02/essential-apartment-moving-tips-for-february/tall-modern-building-with-a-unique-red-r-1.webp)


Whether you are leaving a Brickell tower, a Coral Gables mid-rise, or an Aventura complex, these steps will help your February move go smoothly.

## Reserve the Elevator First

Before you book movers or pack a single box, call your building's management office and reserve the freight elevator. Many Miami high-rises require:

- Elevator reservation 2-4 weeks in advance
- Certificate of Insurance (COI) from your moving company
- Damage deposit ($250-$1,000 depending on the building)
- Specific move-in and move-out time windows

Get your new building's requirements too. Some buildings on Brickell only allow moves before noon on weekdays during winter.

## Take Advantage of Dry Season Weather

February is part of Miami's dry season, with comfortable temperatures in the mid-70s and minimal rain. This is great news for apartment moves since you won't face weather delays. Morning moves are still ideal because loading docks and hallways stay cooler before the afternoon sun heats up the building.

## Book Movers Early

Moving companies fill their schedules weeks in advance during February. If you want a Saturday or first-of-month move, book at least 3 weeks ahead. Weekday moves offer better availability and sometimes lower rates.

## February Moving Checklist

Two weeks before:
- Reserve freight elevator at both buildings
- Book movers and confirm date
- Request COI from your moving company

One week before:
- Confirm elevator reservations
- Pack non-essential items
- Schedule utility transfers

Day before:
- Confirm move time with building management
- Pack remaining items except essentials
- Charge devices and set alarms
