Bal Harbour continues to attract new residents from across the country during May, and it's easy to see why. As a premier Miami-Dade location, Bal Harbour offers a unique blend of community, convenience, and lifestyle options.

## Why Choose Bal Harbour?

Bal Harbour stands out as one of Miami-Dade County's most exclusive villages. The community centers around the world-famous Bal Harbour Shops on Collins Avenue, featuring designer boutiques like Chanel, Gucci, and Louis Vuitton. Residents enjoy pristine oceanfront condominiums and private beach access along one of South Florida's most beautiful stretches of sand.

![Loc Aerial View Of Bal Harbour Beachfront An 1](/images/blog/2025/04/your-ultimate-guide-to-moving-to-bal-harbour/loc-aerial-view-of-bal-harbour-beachfront-an-1.webp)


The area attracts families, professionals, and retirees alike, thanks to its quality of life, convenient access to major employment centers, and excellent amenities.

### Location and Accessibility

Bal Harbour sits at the northern tip of Miami Beach, bordered by Surfside to the south and Haulover Beach Park to the north. Collins Avenue (A1A) runs through the village, connecting residents to I-95 via the 826 and providing quick access to Downtown Miami (about 25 minutes), Aventura Mall (10 minutes), and Miami International Airport (30 minutes). The free Bal Harbour trolley service helps residents get around without a car.

### Community and Lifestyle

Bal Harbour offers an intimate village feel with only about 3,000 residents. The Bal Harbour Village Hall hosts community events year-round, from holiday celebrations to art walks. Residents frequent Haulover Beach Park for morning walks and picnics, while the oceanfront beach clubs provide a more exclusive atmosphere. Local favorites include Makoto for Japanese cuisine and Le Zoo for French brasserie dining at Bal Harbour Shops.

## Neighborhoods to Consider

Bal Harbour is a compact village, so location choices focus on building selection rather than distinct neighborhoods. The most sought-after addresses include the St. Regis Bal Harbour, Oceana Bal Harbour, and the Ritz-Carlton Residences along Collins Avenue. Some residents prefer the quieter western side near Bay Harbor Islands, while others want to be steps from Bal Harbour Shops.

### Choosing Your Perfect Spot

Consider these factors:

- **Proximity to work and schools**: Factor in your daily commute
- **Local amenities**: Parks, shopping, dining, and entertainment options
- **Property types**: Single-family homes, condos, townhouses, or apartments
- **Community vibe**: Family-oriented, young professional, or mixed demographics

## Essential Services to Locate

As a new Bal Harbour resident, you'll want to find:

- **Healthcare facilities**: Mount Sinai Medical Center in Miami Beach is 15 minutes away, and Aventura Hospital is nearby
- **Schools**: Ruth K. Broad Bay Harbor K-8 Center is highly rated, with private options like Lehrman Community Day School in Miami Beach
- **Shopping**: Publix at Bal Harbour Shops for groceries, plus Whole Foods in Surfside
- **Recreation**: Haulover Beach Park, Bal Harbour Beach, and the Indian Creek Golf Club nearby
