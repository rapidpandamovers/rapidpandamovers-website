Weekends in Cutler Bay offer endless possibilities, especially in November. Whether you prefer relaxing brunches, outdoor adventures, or cultural experiences, here's how to make the most of your time off in your new community.

## 1. Explore Black Point Marina

**Address:** 24775 SW 87th Ave, Homestead, FL 33032

![A Happy Family Having Fun And Posing Tog 1](/images/blog/2028/12/10-things-to-do-in-cutler-bay-this-weekend/a-happy-family-having-fun-and-posing-tog-1.webp)


Rent a boat, go fishing, or enjoy waterfront dining at Black Point Ocean Grill. Live music on weekends makes this a perfect destination for a relaxing day on the water.

## 2. Play at Cutler Ridge Park

**Address:** SW 200th St, Cutler Bay, FL

A 10-acre park with a 25-meter swimming pool, athletic fields, and covered playground. Home to the Riptides Swim and Water Polo teams. Great for active families.

## 3. Walk the Blue Heron Park Trail

**Address:** SW 216th St and SW 97th Ave, Cutler Bay, FL

A shaded half-mile paved path perfect for walking, jogging, or biking. Outdoor fitness equipment and pavilions overlook a scenic lake.

## 4. Play Pickleball at Saga Bay Park

**Address:** SW 205th St and SW 79th Ave, Cutler Bay, FL

![Diverse Group Of Young Adults Enjoying A 2](/images/blog/2028/12/10-things-to-do-in-cutler-bay-this-weekend/diverse-group-of-young-adults-enjoying-a-2.webp)


Six lighted pickleball courts plus two tennis courts. Also features outdoor fitness equipment, a covered playground, and paved walking paths.

## 5. Visit the Deering Estate

**Address:** 16701 SW 72nd Ave, Palmetto Bay, FL 33157

A short drive from Cutler Bay, this historic estate offers nature trails, kayak tours, and stunning views of Biscayne Bay. A top destination for nature lovers.

## 6. Browse Cauley Square

**Address:** 22400 Old Dixie Hwy, Miami, FL 33170

A historic village with antique shops, galleries, and restaurants. The Renaissance Festival season adds special events and entertainment.

## 7. Catch a Show at Black Box Theater

**Address:** Cutler Bay area

Local theater productions offer entertainment close to home. Check their schedule for comedy nights, plays, and community events.

## 8. Fish at Homestead Bayfront Park

**Address:** 9698 SW 328th St, Homestead, FL 33033

![Little Girl Captivated By Giant Bubble A 3](/images/blog/2028/12/10-things-to-do-in-cutler-bay-this-weekend/little-girl-captivated-by-giant-bubble-a-3.webp)


A calm beach area on Biscayne Bay with boat ramps and fishing spots. Pack a picnic and make a day of it.

## 9. Play Mini Golf at Palmetto Golf

**Address:** Near Cutler Bay, FL

Family-friendly mini golf and activities perfect for weekend fun with kids. Air-conditioned arcade inside for hot summer days.

## 10. Enjoy Splash in the Bay

**Address:** Cutler Bay Parks

The Town of Cutler Bay hosts community splash events and activities throughout the year. Check their Parks & Recreation calendar for upcoming family-friendly events.
