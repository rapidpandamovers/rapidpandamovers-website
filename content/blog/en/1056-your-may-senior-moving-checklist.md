May is a smart time for seniors to relocate before Miami's peak summer heat arrives. Whether downsizing to a retirement community, moving closer to family, or transitioning to a more manageable home, this checklist will help you plan a smooth move as the wet season begins.

## Why May Works for Senior Moves

May sits at the transition between Miami's dry and wet seasons. Temperatures are warm but not yet at their summer peak, and afternoon thunderstorms are just starting to become regular. Moving now means you're settled before the hottest months and the heart of hurricane season.

## Key Tips for Senior Moving in May

### Plan Ahead for Peak Times

May kicks off the busy summer moving season. Book at least 2-3 weeks ahead to secure your preferred date, especially for weekend moves when family members can help with the transition.

### Set Up the Bedroom First

Creating a comfortable, familiar space immediately helps seniors adjust to their new home. This single step can save hours of stress and potential problems on moving day. Place familiar items like family photos, a favorite lamp, and comfortable bedding to make the space feel like home right away.

### Consider the Weather

May brings the start of Miami's wet season with afternoon thunderstorms, temperatures in the upper 80s, and rising humidity. Morning moves (starting by 8 AM) help seniors avoid the hottest part of the day and the typical afternoon storm window. Our crews bring tarps and waterproof materials to protect your belongings.

### Prepare Your Belongings

Take time to inventory your items before the move. This is especially important for senior moving, as proper documentation helps ensure everything arrives safely at your new location.
