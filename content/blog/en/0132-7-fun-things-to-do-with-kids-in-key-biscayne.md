Moving is tough on kids, especially during the winter season. Their whole world is changing, and they don't have the context adults do to understand why. With the right approach, you can help them adjust and maybe even get excited about the new adventure.

## 1. Get the Library Card First Week

The Key Biscayne Branch Library on Crandon Boulevard offers free library cards that open doors to story times, summer reading programs, movie screenings, and craft sessions. This should be one of your first stops after moving.

![A Joyful Child With Socks On Feet Playin 3](/images/blog/2025/01/7-fun-things-to-do-with-kids-in-key-biscayne/a-joyful-child-with-socks-on-feet-playin-3.webp)


## 2. Search Miami-Dade Parks for 'Splash Pad'

Florida heat demands water activities. Bill Baggs Cape Florida State Park on the south end of the island has beaches perfect for wading, while the county parks department lists splash pads throughout Miami-Dade. Crandon Park's beach and playground combo is a local favorite for families.

## 3. Join the Nextdoor Parents Group

Search Nextdoor for 'Key Biscayne parents' or 'kids.' Local parents share the real scoop on which playgrounds are best, which activities are worth it, and where to find deals.

![A Young Girl With Curly Hair Peeking Ove 1](/images/blog/2025/01/7-fun-things-to-do-with-kids-in-key-biscayne/a-young-girl-with-curly-hair-peeking-ove-1.webp)


## 4. Time Outdoor Activities Around Heat

In Key Biscayne, plan outdoor family activities for before 10am or after 4pm. Midday Florida sun is brutal on kids (and parents). Indoor activities or pool time fill the hot hours.

## 5. Sign Up for Museum Memberships Early

Miami Children's Museum on Watson Island, Zoo Miami in South Dade, and the Phillip and Patricia Frost Museum of Science in downtown Miami all offer annual memberships. If you'll visit more than twice, they pay for themselves and often include guest passes. Being on Key Biscayne puts you just minutes from downtown attractions via the Rickenbacker Causeway.

## 6. Find the Secret Playground Gems

Key Biscayne Village Green Park has a great playground that locals love, and the Key Biscayne Community Center offers programs year-round. Ask other parents at Crandon Park's beach playground for their actual favorites around the island.

![Young Girl Engrossed In Drawing On A Car 2](/images/blog/2025/01/7-fun-things-to-do-with-kids-in-key-biscayne/young-girl-engrossed-in-drawing-on-a-car-2.webp)


## 7. Download the Miami-Dade Parks App

The county app shows all parks, activities, and programs near Key Biscayne. Youth sports leagues, swimming lessons, and camps all register through it.
