## Key Tips for Student Moving in December

### Book Early for End-of-Semester Rush

December is one of the busiest months for student moves. With finals ending and leases turning over, scheduling your student move early ensures you get your preferred date and time.

### Book early during peak move-out weekends at universities

This single step can save hours of stress and potential problems on moving day. Whether you are headed to student housing near Brickell, apartments in Kendall close to FIU, or dorms at UM, our experienced crews have seen what happens when this step is skipped.

### Take Advantage of the Dry Season Weather

December in Miami brings mild temperatures in the mid-70s with low humidity and almost no rain. This is ideal moving weather, and you can schedule moves at any time of day without worrying about afternoon storms. Professional movers can work efficiently in these comfortable conditions.

### Prepare Your Belongings

Take time to inventory your items before the move. This is especially important for student moving, as proper documentation helps ensure everything arrives safely at your new location.
