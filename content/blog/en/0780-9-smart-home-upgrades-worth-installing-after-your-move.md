A new home means a fresh start with your tech setup. These nine smart home devices pay for themselves through energy savings, convenience, and protection against South Florida's humidity, flooding, and summer storms.

## 1. Smart Thermostat

Nest, Ecobee, or similar devices learn your schedule and optimize energy use. In Miami's hot and humid climate, where AC runs nearly year-round, a smart thermostat can cut your FPL bill by 15-20%. These devices pay for themselves within the first year.

![Bluetooth Speaker And Smartphone Playing 4](/images/blog/2027/08/9-smart-home-upgrades-worth-installing-after-your-move/bluetooth-speaker-and-smartphone-playing-4.webp)


## 2. Video Doorbell

Ring, Nest, or other video doorbells let you see visitors and delivery people from anywhere via smartphone. Whether you live in a Brickell high-rise or a Coral Gables single-family home, video doorbells help monitor package deliveries and unexpected visitors.

## 3. Smart Locks

Keyless entry, remote locking, and temporary codes for visitors eliminate key management hassles.

## 4. Smart Lighting

Automated lighting controlled by voice, app, or schedule adds convenience and security. Schedule lights to turn on at sunset to make your Kendall or Doral home look occupied while you're out, or set up motion-activated porch lights for those late evenings.

## 5. Mesh WiFi System

Large homes benefit from mesh networks that eliminate dead zones and provide consistent coverage everywhere.

## 6. Smart Smoke Detectors

Nest Protect and similar devices send phone alerts and communicate with each other during emergencies.

![Close Up Of Three Smart Home Security Ca 1](/images/blog/2027/08/9-smart-home-upgrades-worth-installing-after-your-move/close-up-of-three-smart-home-security-ca-1.webp)


## 7. Water Leak Sensors

Small sensors near water heaters, under sinks, and by washing machines alert you to leaks before disasters. In Miami, where summer storms and occasional flooding are common, early leak detection can save thousands in water damage repairs. Place sensors near AC drain pans too, since constant AC use can lead to condensation problems.

## 8. Smart Plugs

Transform any device into a smart device. Schedule appliances, monitor energy usage, and control devices remotely with affordable smart plugs.

## 9. Robot Vacuum

Automated vacuums like Roomba keep floors clean between deeper cleanings, especially helpful during the chaos of moving and settling in.
