January marks the beginning of PCS season for military families across South Florida. Whether you're arriving at Homestead Air Reserve Base, USSOUTHCOM in Doral, or one of the Coast Guard stations, getting settled before your report date takes smart planning. Here's what service members and their families need to know about moving in January.

## Why January PCS Moves Need Early Planning

PCS season peaks between January and August, when military moving companies handle their highest volume. If you're using a military-approved moving company, expect limited availability during these months. Booking 4-6 weeks ahead gives you better choices for dates and prevents scrambling at the last minute.

## Key Tips for Military Moving in January

### Plan Ahead for Peak Times

January kicks off PCS season, and military-approved moving companies start filling their schedules quickly. Book 4-6 weeks ahead to secure your preferred date, especially if you need weekend availability.

### Keep all documentation organized for reimbursement purposes

This single step can save hours of stress and potential problems on moving day. Our experienced crews have seen what happens when this step is skipped.

### Consider the Weather

January brings dry, comfortable weather to South Florida. The mild conditions are ideal for moving, with no summer storms or extreme heat to worry about. Our crews still protect your belongings from direct sun during loading.

### Prepare Your Belongings

Take time to inventory your items before the move. This is especially important for military moving, as proper documentation helps ensure everything arrives safely at your new location.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How far in advance should I book a January PCS move?

Book at least 4-6 weeks ahead. January marks the start of PCS season, and military-approved movers fill their schedules quickly. If you're doing a PPM/DITY move, reserve trucks and labor even earlier.

### Can I claim moving expenses on my taxes?

Active-duty military members can still deduct unreimbursed PCS moving expenses, even though this deduction was suspended for civilians. Keep all receipts and consult your base legal office or a military tax specialist before filing.

### What if my base housing is not ready when I arrive?

Plan for 1-4 weeks of temporary lodging. Extended-stay options near Homestead ARB include Florida City and Cutler Bay. Near USSOUTHCOM, Doral apartments often offer military short-term leases. We provide temporary storage while you wait for housing.
