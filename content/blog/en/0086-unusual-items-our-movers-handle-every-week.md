# Unusual Items Our Movers Handle Every Week

Most people assume movers deal with boxes, sofas, and mattresses all day. That's part of it. But our crews in Miami handle items every single week that would make a standard two-man-and-a-truck outfit walk away from the job.

## Gun Safes and Fireproof Cabinets

A residential gun safe weighs anywhere from 300 to 1,500 pounds. We move several per week across Miami-Dade. The challenge isn't just the weight. It's the narrow hallways in older Coral Gables homes, the tight elevator clearances in Brickell condos, and the fact that these safes can't tip without risking internal lock damage. We use heavy-duty appliance dollies with stair-climbing wheels and 4-point strapping systems to keep things controlled.

## Hot Tubs and Jacuzzis

South Florida backyards are practically designed for hot tubs, which means we relocate them constantly. An average hot tub weighs 600-900 pounds empty. We disconnect plumbing and electrical, move the unit with a crane or rigging setup depending on yard access, and coordinate with licensed electricians at the destination. Getting one through a side gate in Kendall requires precise measurements before we even send a truck.

## Wine Collections and Refrigerated Units

Miami's wine collectors aren't messing around. We've moved collections worth six figures from Pinecrest estates to new homes in Coral Gables. Temperature control is everything. We use insulated wine shipping boxes, transport during early morning hours to avoid peak heat, and coordinate delivery timing so bottles go straight into climate-controlled storage. Wine fridges themselves need to stay upright during transport to protect the compressor.

## Marble and Stone Furniture

Italian marble dining tables, onyx console pieces, stone bathroom vanities. These show up regularly in luxury Miami homes. Marble cracks from shock, not just impact, so we use custom padded crates and vibration-dampening materials. We never lay marble flat in a truck bed without proper support underneath.

## Large Aquariums

Saltwater reef tanks in particular require serious planning. We've moved 200-gallon setups from Aventura to Doral, transporting livestock in heated bags, keeping live rock submerged, and reassembling filtration systems on the other end. The glass tanks travel empty on edge, secured to prevent any flex or vibration damage.

## Gym Equipment and Commercial Machines

Pelotons, Rogue squat racks, commercial-grade treadmills. Home gyms have exploded in popularity, and the equipment is heavy, awkward, and often partially disassembled for transport. A loaded squat rack with plates can exceed 1,000 pounds total. We inventory every pin, bolt, and attachment before disassembly.
