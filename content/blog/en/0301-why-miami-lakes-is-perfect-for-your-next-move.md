Miami Lakes continues to attract new residents from across the country during November, and it's easy to see why. As a premier Miami-Dade location, Miami Lakes offers a unique blend of community, convenience, and lifestyle options.

## Why Choose Miami Lakes?

Miami Lakes stands out as one of Miami-Dade County's most appealing locations. The community offers a planned community feel atmosphere and is particularly known for lakes, trails, and Main Street shopping.

![Scenic Aerial Shot Of A Sprawling Reside 5](/images/blog/2025/08/why-miami-lakes-is-perfect-for-your-next-move/scenic-aerial-shot-of-a-sprawling-reside-5.webp)


The area attracts families, professionals, and retirees alike, thanks to its quality of life, convenient access to major employment centers, and excellent amenities.

### Location and Accessibility

One of Miami Lakes's biggest advantages is its strategic location within Miami-Dade County. The Palmetto Expressway (SR 826) and I-75 provide quick access to Downtown Miami, Miami International Airport, and Fort Lauderdale. The town is just 16 miles northwest of downtown, making it ideal for professionals who work in the city but want a quieter suburban lifestyle.

### Community and Lifestyle

Miami Lakes residents enjoy a strong sense of community centered around Main Street, the town's walkable shopping and dining district. Royal Oaks Park and Miami Lakes Optimist Park host youth sports leagues, while the Miami Lakes Athletic Club draws fitness enthusiasts. The fall season brings the popular Miami Lakes Art Festival and holiday events at Town Center.

## Neighborhoods to Consider

When planning your move to Miami Lakes, the Main Street area is particularly popular with newcomers. Each neighborhood has its own character. Some offer walkable dining and shopping at Main Street, while others provide quiet, tree-lined streets near the lakes.

### Popular Miami Lakes Neighborhoods

**Royal Oaks** offers larger single-family homes with spacious lots near Royal Oaks Park. **Miami Lakes West** provides newer construction and townhome options. **The Shores** features waterfront properties along the lakes that give the town its name. **Main Street area** appeals to those who want walkable access to restaurants like Shula's Steak House, boutiques, and the AMC theater.

## Moving to Miami Lakes in November

November is an excellent time to consider your move. The fall weather in South Florida provides gradually cooling temperatures and decreasing humidity for the moving process.

### Timing Your Move

- **Best moving days**: Weekdays often offer better availability and rates

## Essential Services to Locate

As a new Miami Lakes resident, you'll want to find:

- **Healthcare**: Palmetto General Hospital is nearby in Hialeah, and the town has several urgent care centers along Miami Lakes Drive
- **Schools**: Miami Lakes K-8 Center, Miami Lakes Middle School, and Barbara Goleman Senior High serve the area, plus several private options
- **Shopping**: Publix and Whole Foods for groceries, Miami Lakes Town Center for retail, and Main Street for boutique shopping
