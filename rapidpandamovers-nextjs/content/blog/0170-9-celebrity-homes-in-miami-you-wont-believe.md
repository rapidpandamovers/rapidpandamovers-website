Miami attracts the rich and famous, and their homes are as impressive as you'd expect. This May, take a look at some of the most notable celebrity properties in the area—from Star Island mansions to South Beach penthouses.

## 1. Star Island Estates

This exclusive island has housed Madonna, Diddy, and Gloria Estefan—requiring discrete, high-security moving services.

![Celebrity Mansion Luxury Overview](/images/blog/2025/05/9-celebrity-homes-in-miami-you-wont-believe/celebrity-mansion-luxury-overview.webp)


## 2. Versace Mansion (Casa Casuarina)

Gianni Versace's famous South Beach residence has seen multiple transformations and celebrity occupants.

## 3. Fisher Island Properties

Accessible only by boat, this ultra-exclusive community requires specialized logistics for any move.

## 4. Coral Gables Mansions

The historic Mediterranean-style estates in Coral Gables have housed numerous celebrities over the decades.

## 5. Miami Beach Penthouses

Luxury high-rise penthouses require coordinated elevator access and building management approval for moves.

## 6. Indian Creek Island

Known as "Billionaire Bunker," this ultra-private island has attracted residents like Ivanka Trump, Tom Brady, and Jeff Bezos.

## 7. Key Biscayne Estates

This barrier island paradise has been home to presidents and celebrities seeking privacy while staying close to Miami.

## 8. La Gorce Island

This exclusive gated community in Miami Beach has housed celebrities like Cher and remains one of the most prestigious addresses in Florida.

## 9. Hibiscus Island Properties

This man-made island in Biscayne Bay offers waterfront living and has attracted numerous entertainment industry figures.
