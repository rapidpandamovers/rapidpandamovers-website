Moving with kids this winter? North Miami Beach is a fantastic choice for families. From December adventures to everyday activities, here's your guide to keeping the whole family entertained and connected to your new community.

## 1. Get the Library Card First Week

Your North Miami Beach library card is free and unlocks story times, summer reading programs, movie screenings, and craft sessions. This should be one of your first stops after moving.

## 2. Search Miami-Dade Parks for 'Splash Pad'

Florida heat demands water activities. The county parks department lists all North Miami Beach area splash pads and spray parks—free and perfect for young kids.

## 3. Join the Nextdoor Parents Group

Search Nextdoor for 'North Miami Beach parents' or 'kids.' Local parents share the real scoop on which playgrounds are best, which activities are worth it, and where to find deals.

![Kids enjoying a playground in North Miami Beach](/images/blog/2024/12/8-family-friendly-activities-in-north-miami-beach/north-miami-beach-kids-playground.jpg)

## 4. Time Outdoor Activities Around Heat

In North Miami Beach, plan outdoor family activities for before 10am or after 4pm. Midday Florida sun is brutal on kids (and parents). Indoor activities or pool time fill the hot hours.

## 5. Sign Up for Museum Memberships Early

Miami Children's Museum, Zoo Miami, and science centers near North Miami Beach offer annual memberships. If you'll visit more than twice, they pay for themselves and often include guest passes.

## 6. Find the Secret Playground Gems

Every North Miami Beach neighborhood has parks that locals love but tourists miss. Ask other parents at the obvious playgrounds for their actual favorites.

![Family exploring parks in North Miami Beach](/images/blog/2024/12/8-family-friendly-activities-in-north-miami-beach/north-miami-beach-family-parks.jpg)

## 7. Download the Miami-Dade Parks App

The county app shows all parks, activities, and programs near North Miami Beach. Youth sports leagues, swimming lessons, and camps all register through it.

## 8. Join Sports Through Schools and Rec Centers

Registration for youth sports in North Miami Beach often opens months early. Get on mailing lists now so you don't miss soccer, baseball, or swim team signups.
