Moving doesn't have to break the bank, even in August. With smart planning and the right strategies, you can significantly reduce your summer moving costs without sacrificing quality or safety.

## 1. Declutter Before Getting Quotes

Fewer items equals lower costs. Aggressively declutter before movers assess your load—every box saved is money saved.

![Budget Saving Money Affordable Steps](/images/blog/2028/08/9-budget-friendly-moving-tips-that-actually-work/budget-saving-money-affordable-steps.webp)


## 2. Time Your Move Strategically

Mid-week, mid-month moves cost less than weekend or end-of-month moves. Avoid peak summer months when possible.

## 3. Get Multiple Quotes

Compare at least three estimates from reputable companies. Make sure they're all based on the same inventory for accurate comparison.

## 4. Do Your Own Packing

Professional packing adds cost. If you have time, pack everything yourself with proper materials from a discount store.

## 5. Source Free Boxes

Check with local stores, Facebook Marketplace, and Nextdoor for free moving boxes. Many people give them away after their moves.

## 6. Use What You Have for Packing

Towels, blankets, and clothing make excellent padding for fragile items. Use socks to wrap glasses and suitcases for heavy books.

## 7. Cancel Unnecessary Services Early

End subscriptions, gym memberships, and services you won't use at your new location. These small savings add up quickly.

## 8. Move Non-Essentials Yourself

Transport small items, plants, and valuables in your own vehicle. This reduces the overall load and cost of professional movers.

## 9. Ask About Discounts

Many moving companies offer discounts for military, seniors, students, or AAA members. Always ask about available promotions.
