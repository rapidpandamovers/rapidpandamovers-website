Welcome to your comprehensive guide for moving to Kendall! This large unincorporated community offers suburban living, excellent schools, and convenient access to Downtown Miami and South Florida attractions.

## About Kendall

Kendall is a large census-designated place (CDP) in Miami-Dade County, located about 10 miles southwest of Downtown Miami. Covering 16.1 square miles with approximately 80,000 residents, Kendall offers diverse neighborhoods, shopping centers, and suburban living within Miami's urban core.

![Kendall Florida Suburban Family Guide](/images/blog/2027/11/your-ultimate-guide-to-moving-to-kendall/kendall-florida-suburban-family-guide.webp)


### Demographics and Community

- **Population**: Approximately 78,500-80,000 (2023)
- **Median Age**: 41.9 years
- **Diversity**: Hispanic (65.5%), White (23.8%), Black (4.3%), Asian (3.1%)
- **Median Household Income**: $83,664
- **Per Capita Income**: $49,885
- **Poverty Rate**: 13.4%

### Home Values
- **Median Home/Condo Value**: $672,600

## Neighborhoods

Kendall comprises multiple distinct neighborhoods:

### West Kendall
Newer development with master-planned communities, shopping centers, and family-friendly amenities. Site of the proposed $2 billion City Park development.

### Kendall Proper
Established neighborhoods with mature landscaping and diverse housing options.

### The Falls Area
Near The Falls shopping center, offering retail access and established residential areas.

### Country Walk/Three Lakes
Master-planned communities with parks, lakes, and family amenities.

## Schools and Education

Kendall is served by Miami-Dade County Public Schools with many well-regarded options:

### School Quality
- Multiple A-rated schools
- Magnet program access
- Charter school options
- Active parent involvement

### Higher Education Nearby
- **Miami-Dade College (Kendall Campus)**: Two-year programs
- **Florida International University**: 15-minute drive
- **University of Miami**: 20-minute drive

## Housing Market

### Housing Characteristics
- Mix of single-family homes, townhouses, and condos
- Gated communities throughout
- Housing from starter homes to luxury estates
- New development ongoing

### 2025 Developments
- **City Park at West Kendall**: Proposed $2 billion, 990-acre development with 7,800 residential units
- **Coral Nest**: New 6.5-acre mixed-use development with 38 townhomes

- **West Kendall Baptist Hospital**: Healthcare

### Nearby Business Districts
- **Dadeland/Datran**: Corporate offices

## Location and Accessibility

### Transportation
- **US-1 (South Dixie Highway)**: Main corridor
- **Palmetto Expressway (SR 826)**: East-west access
- **Florida Turnpike**: North-south access
- **Kendall Drive (SW 88th Street)**: East-west corridor
- **Metrorail**: Dadeland stations provide rail access

### Commute Times
- **Downtown Miami**: 25-30 minutes
- **Miami Beach**: 35-40 minutes
- **Miami International Airport**: 20-25 minutes
- **Coral Gables**: 15-20 minutes
- **Homestead**: 25-30 minutes

Extensive restaurant options along Kendall Drive and throughout the community, from fast-casual to fine dining.

## Moving Tips for Kendall

### Best Time to Move
- **Fall/Winter (October-March)**: Best weather
- **Weekdays**: Avoid weekend traffic at shopping centers

### Local Considerations
- **Traffic**: Kendall Drive can be congested
- **Gated Communities**: Coordinate access with security
- **HOA Rules**: Many communities have specific guidelines
- **Flood Zones**: Check insurance requirements
