Living near Aventura means easy access to some of Florida's best beaches, perfect for fall outings. Whether you're looking for family-friendly shores, water sports, or quiet relaxation, here are the coastal destinations worth your time.

## 1. Sunny Isles Beach

**Address:** Collins Ave from 163rd to 195th St, Sunny Isles Beach, FL

![A Bustling Beach Scene With People Enjoy 1](/images/blog/2027/09/5-best-beaches-near-aventura-for-a-day-trip/a-bustling-beach-scene-with-people-enjoy-1.webp)


A two-mile stretch of pristine coastline just minutes from Aventura. Family-friendly with soft white sand and crystal-clear waters. Samson Oceanfront Park at 174th Street offers the best access with playgrounds and picnic areas.

## 2. Haulover Beach Park

**Address:** 10800 Collins Ave, Miami Beach, FL 33154

Just 10 minutes south of Aventura, featuring wide sandy shores perfect for swimming and sunbathing. The park includes picnic areas, a dog-friendly section, bike rentals, and Haulover Inlet for fishing enthusiasts.

## 3. Oleta River State Park

**Address:** 3400 NE 163rd St, North Miami Beach, FL 33160

![A Wetsuit Hanging From An Open Car Trunk 2](/images/blog/2027/09/5-best-beaches-near-aventura-for-a-day-trip/a-wetsuit-hanging-from-an-open-car-trunk-2.webp)


Florida's largest urban park, about 15 minutes from Aventura. Features a quiet, secluded beach on Biscayne Bay with calm waters. Combine beach time with mountain biking, kayaking, or paddleboarding.

## 4. Hollywood Beach

**Address:** Hollywood Beach Broadwalk, Hollywood, FL

A short drive north from Aventura to this classic Florida beach with its famous 2.5-mile Broadwalk. Restaurants, shops, and entertainment along the promenade. Great for biking and rollerblading.

## 5. Hallandale Beach

**Address:** S Ocean Dr, Hallandale Beach, FL

![Car Trunk Loaded With Surfboards And Wet 5](/images/blog/2027/09/5-best-beaches-near-aventura-for-a-day-trip/car-trunk-loaded-with-surfboards-and-wet-5.webp)


Just north of Aventura, this beach offers a more relaxed atmosphere than crowded Miami Beach. Easy parking and access make it perfect for quick day trips.

## Quick Tips for Beach Days

- **Parking:** Arrive before 10 AM on weekends for easier parking at Haulover and Sunny Isles
- **Weather:** September still brings afternoon thunderstorms, so plan your beach time for mornings
- **Traffic:** Take Biscayne Boulevard to avoid I-95 congestion during peak hours
