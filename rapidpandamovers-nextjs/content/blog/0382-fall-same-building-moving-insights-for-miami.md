## Same Building Moves in Miami's Popular High-Rises

Miami is home to hundreds of high-rise buildings where same building moves are common. In Brickell, buildings like Icon Brickell, Millecento, and SLS Lux see residents upgrading units or moving for better views. Coral Gables condos often require coordination with strict HOA rules. Downtown Miami's high-rises near Bayside Marketplace have specific loading dock schedules. Even Aventura's Turnberry complexes have peak times for elevator reservations.

Understanding your building's specific policies saves time and prevents moving day headaches.
