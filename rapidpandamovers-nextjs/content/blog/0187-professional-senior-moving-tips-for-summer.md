## Key Tips for Senior Moving in June

### Plan Ahead for Peak Times

June can be a busy time for moving companies. With peak moving season activities and seasonal transitions, scheduling your senior moving early ensures you get your preferred date and time.

### Set up the bedroom first so they have a comfortable, familiar space immediately

This single step can save hours of stress and potential problems on moving day. Our experienced crews have seen what happens when this step is skipped.

### Consider the Weather

South Florida's summer weather is predictable in some ways and surprising in others. Daily afternoon storms roll in from the Everglades around 3-4 PM, so scheduling your move for the morning hours is ideal. Professional movers know how to protect your belongings from Miami's 80-90% humidity levels, sudden rain, and heat that can reach 95 degrees or higher during transport.

### Prepare Your Belongings

Take time to inventory your items before the move. This is especially important for senior moving, as proper documentation helps ensure everything arrives safely at your new location.
