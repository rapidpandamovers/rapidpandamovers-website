Moving is tough on kids, especially during the winter season. Their whole world is changing, and they don't have the context adults do to understand why. With the right approach, you can help them transition smoothly—and maybe even get excited about the new adventure.

## 1. Get the Library Card First Week

Your Key Biscayne library card is free and unlocks story times, summer reading programs, movie screenings, and craft sessions. This should be one of your first stops after moving.

![Family Kids Activities Guide](/images/blog/2030/01/7-fun-things-to-do-with-kids-in-key-biscayne/family-kids-activities-guide.webp)


## 2. Search Miami-Dade Parks for 'Splash Pad'

Florida heat demands water activities. The county parks department lists all Key Biscayne area splash pads and spray parks—free and perfect for young kids.

## 3. Join the Nextdoor Parents Group

Search Nextdoor for 'Key Biscayne parents' or 'kids.' Local parents share the real scoop on which playgrounds are best, which activities are worth it, and where to find deals.

![Family Kids Activities Overview](/images/blog/2030/01/7-fun-things-to-do-with-kids-in-key-biscayne/family-kids-activities-overview.webp)


## 4. Time Outdoor Activities Around Heat

In Key Biscayne, plan outdoor family activities for before 10am or after 4pm. Midday Florida sun is brutal on kids (and parents). Indoor activities or pool time fill the hot hours.

## 5. Sign Up for Museum Memberships Early

Miami Children's Museum, Zoo Miami, and science centers near Key Biscayne offer annual memberships. If you'll visit more than twice, they pay for themselves and often include guest passes.

## 6. Find the Secret Playground Gems

Every Key Biscayne neighborhood has parks that locals love but tourists miss. Ask other parents at the obvious playgrounds for their actual favorites.

![Family Kids Activities Steps](/images/blog/2030/01/7-fun-things-to-do-with-kids-in-key-biscayne/family-kids-activities-steps.webp)


## 7. Download the Miami-Dade Parks App

The county app shows all parks, activities, and programs near Key Biscayne. Youth sports leagues, swimming lessons, and camps all register through it.
