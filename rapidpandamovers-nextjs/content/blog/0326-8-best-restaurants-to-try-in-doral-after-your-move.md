Just moved to Doral or planning your relocation? This fast-growing city west of Miami International Airport has become a culinary destination, with restaurants representing cuisines from around the world. Whether you're coming from Kendall, Hialeah, or out of state, here are eight must-try spots after your move.

## 1. Pisco y Nazca

**8551 NW 53rd Street**

![Elegant Table Setting With Glassware And 5](/images/blog/2026/05/8-best-restaurants-to-try-in-doral-after-your-move/elegant-table-setting-with-glassware-and-5.webp)


This bustling Peruvian spot delivers authentic ceviche and traditional dishes in a vibrant atmosphere. Order the Ceviche Mixto or Lomo Saltado, paired with a refreshing Pisco Sour. The buzzing energy makes it perfect for celebrations with new neighbors.

## 2. Il Lago Trattoria

**6747 NW 103rd Avenue**

For a luxurious Italian night out, this waterfront gem offers rustic elegance with candlelit tables and stunning views. The Osso Buco alla Milanese and Risotto al Tartufo are standouts, and the Panna Cotta al Limone is the perfect finish.

## 3. Marfil Bistro

**3470 NW 82nd Avenue**

This stylish European bistro combines inventive cuisine with elegant ambiance. The Duck Confit and Foie Gras Burger are house specialties, while the Lobster Ravioli delights seafood lovers. A sophisticated choice for date nights.

## 4. Doral Steakhouse

**9400 NW 41st Street**

![Sophisticated Restaurant Table Setting W 2](/images/blog/2026/05/8-best-restaurants-to-try-in-doral-after-your-move/sophisticated-restaurant-table-setting-w-2.webp)


The ultimate destination for steak enthusiasts, combining classic sophistication with a modern edge. Premium cuts like Bone-In Ribeye and Chateaubriand are perfectly prepared, with indulgent sides like Truffle Mac and Cheese.

## 5. Beirut Doral

**NW 12th Street**

Experience authentic Lebanese cuisine with warm spices and bold Mediterranean flavors. The mezze platters are perfect for sharing, and the mixed grill options satisfy meat lovers. A wonderful introduction to the area's diverse dining scene.

## 6. Bulla Gastrobar

**5335 NW 87th Avenue**

Blending urban sophistication with warm hospitality, Bulla's contemporary Latin fusion menu bursts with creativity. The tapas-style dining encourages sharing and exploring multiple dishes, perfect for getting to know new friends.

## 7. La Tremenda

**11402 NW 41st Street**

![Warm And Inviting Restaurant Interior Wi 3](/images/blog/2026/05/8-best-restaurants-to-try-in-doral-after-your-move/warm-and-inviting-restaurant-interior-wi-3.webp)


For affordable, delicious Mexican food, this colorful casual spot delivers. The Birria Tacos and Tlayudas are fantastic, and the freshly made guacamole is not to be missed. End with Churros con Cajeta for a sweet finish.

## 8. El Porton de La Flaca

**NW 79th Avenue**

Don't miss this Colombian gem for generous portions of classic dishes. The empanadas with beef and potatoes are legendary, and the bandeja paisa (rice, red beans, fried egg, pork skin, charbroiled steak, sweet plantains, and corn patty) is an unforgettable feast.
