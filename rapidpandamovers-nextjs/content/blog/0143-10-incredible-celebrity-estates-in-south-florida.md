Miami attracts the rich and famous, and their homes are as impressive as you'd expect. This March, take a look at some of the most notable celebrity properties in the area—from Star Island mansions to South Beach penthouses.

## 1. Star Island Estates

This exclusive island has housed Madonna, Diddy, and Gloria Estefan—requiring discrete, high-security moving services.

![Celebrity Mansion Luxury Guide](/images/blog/2025/03/10-incredible-celebrity-estates-in-south-florida/celebrity-mansion-luxury-guide.webp)


## 2. Versace Mansion (Casa Casuarina)

Gianni Versace's famous South Beach residence has seen multiple transformations and celebrity occupants.

## 3. Fisher Island Properties

Accessible only by boat, this ultra-exclusive community requires specialized logistics for any move.

## 4. Coral Gables Mansions

The historic Mediterranean-style estates in Coral Gables have housed numerous celebrities over the decades.

## 5. Miami Beach Penthouses

Luxury high-rise penthouses require coordinated elevator access and building management approval for moves.

## 6. Indian Creek Island

Known as "Billionaire Bunker," this private island has hosted celebrities like Tom Brady, Gisele Bundchen, and Jared Kushner.

![Celebrity Mansion Luxury Overview](/images/blog/2025/03/10-incredible-celebrity-estates-in-south-florida/celebrity-mansion-luxury-overview.webp)


## 7. Key Biscayne Estates

This exclusive barrier island offers privacy and prestige, with estates owned by numerous high-profile athletes and executives.

## 8. Hibiscus Island Mansions

This guard-gated island in Biscayne Bay has attracted celebrities seeking privacy while remaining close to South Beach nightlife.

## 9. La Gorce Island Properties

Another ultra-exclusive island community, La Gorce has been home to celebrities including Cher and Billy Joel.

## 10. Bal Harbour Oceanfront Estates

These beachfront mansions offer direct Atlantic Ocean access and have attracted international celebrities and business moguls.
