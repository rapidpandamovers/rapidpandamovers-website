Miami attracts the rich and famous, and their homes are as impressive as you'd expect. This January, take a look at some of the most notable celebrity properties in the area—from Star Island mansions to South Beach penthouses.

## 1. Star Island Estates

This exclusive island has housed Madonna, Diddy, and Gloria Estefan—requiring discrete, high-security moving services for anyone lucky enough to relocate here.

![Celebrity Mansion Luxury Guide](/images/blog/2027/01/10-legendary-celebrity-residences-in-miami/celebrity-mansion-luxury-guide.webp)


## 2. Versace Mansion (Casa Casuarina)

Gianni Versace's famous South Beach residence has seen multiple transformations and celebrity occupants since the designer's time there.

## 3. Fisher Island Properties

Accessible only by boat, this ultra-exclusive community requires specialized logistics for any move, making it one of Miami's most challenging relocation destinations.

## 4. Coral Gables Mansions

The historic Mediterranean-style estates in Coral Gables have housed numerous celebrities over the decades, from classic Hollywood stars to modern influencers.

## 5. Miami Beach Penthouses

Luxury high-rise penthouses require coordinated elevator access and building management approval for moves, especially in the most exclusive towers along Collins Avenue.

## 6. Brickell Avenue High-Rises

Miami's financial district attracts celebrities and executives alike to its stunning waterfront towers with views of Biscayne Bay.

![Celebrity Mansion Luxury Steps](/images/blog/2027/01/10-legendary-celebrity-residences-in-miami/celebrity-mansion-luxury-steps.webp)


## 7. Coconut Grove Waterfront Estates

This historic neighborhood's waterfront properties have been home to celebrities seeking privacy and natural beauty in the heart of Miami.

## 8. Aventura Luxury Condos

The ultra-modern towers in Aventura offer celebrities privacy, security, and world-class amenities just minutes from Bal Harbour.

## 9. Key Biscayne Private Homes

This barrier island community offers celebrities the perfect balance of Miami access and secluded island living.

## 10. Sunset Islands Waterfront Homes

Nestled in Biscayne Bay between Miami Beach and the mainland, these exclusive islands provide celebrities with waterfront luxury and privacy.
