## Key Tips for Packing Services in December

### Plan Ahead for Peak Times

December can be a busy time for moving companies. With new year planning activities and seasonal transitions, scheduling your packing services early ensures you get your preferred date and time.

### Start Packing Non-Essentials 4-6 Weeks Before Moving Day

This single step can save hours of stress and potential problems on moving day. Our experienced crews have seen what happens when this step is skipped.

### Consider Miami's December Weather

December in Miami brings cooler mornings (often in the 60s) but afternoons can still reach the 80s. While you might escape the summer thunderstorms, cold fronts can bring unexpected rain. Professional movers know how to protect your belongings from humidity changes and keep furniture covered during brief showers. Pro tip: Schedule your move for morning hours when temperatures are most comfortable and traffic is lighter on I-95 and the Palmetto.

### Prepare Your Belongings

Take time to inventory your items before the move. This is especially important for packing services, as proper documentation helps ensure everything arrives safely at your new location.
