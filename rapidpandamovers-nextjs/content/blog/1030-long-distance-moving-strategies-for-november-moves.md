## Key Strategies for November Long Distance Moves

### Time Your Move Around Thanksgiving Week

The week of Thanksgiving is typically one of the busiest travel times in the United States. Highway congestion peaks on Wednesday before Thanksgiving and Sunday after. Schedule your long distance move for the first two weeks of November or the final week after the holiday rush subsides. This timing provides better highway conditions and often more flexible scheduling with moving companies.

### Lock In Pricing with a Binding Estimate

Get a binding estimate to lock in your moving costs before the holiday season. This single step can save hours of stress and potential problems on moving day. Our experienced crews have seen what happens when this step is skipped, and last-minute pricing changes during peak season can be significant.

### Account for Shorter Daylight Hours

November brings shorter days, which affects loading and unloading schedules. Professional movers adjust their timelines to maximize productive daylight hours. An early morning start becomes even more important for long distance moves crossing multiple time zones.

### Prepare for Variable Weather Across Routes

South Florida's fall weather is predictable in some ways and surprising in others. However, your interstate route may pass through regions with very different November conditions. Professional movers know how to protect your belongings from humidity, rain, cold snaps, and even early snow in northern destinations.

### Inventory and Document Everything

Take time to inventory your items before the move. This is especially important for long distance moving, as proper documentation helps ensure everything arrives safely at your new location. Create a detailed list with photos for valuable items and keep important documents easily accessible during transit.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is November a good time for long distance moving?

![Traffic Flows On A Busy Los Angeles Free 1](/images/blog/2030/11/long-distance-moving-strategies-for-november-moves/traffic-flows-on-a-busy-los-angeles-free-1.webp)


November can be an excellent time for long distance moves. Moving companies often have more availability than summer months, and rates may be lower outside the peak season. The key is avoiding the Thanksgiving week itself when highways are congested.

### How far in advance should I book a November move?

Book your long distance move at least 4-6 weeks in advance, especially if you need specific dates around Thanksgiving. This ensures availability and allows time for proper planning and coordination.

### What happens if weather delays my long distance move?

Professional moving companies build weather contingencies into their schedules. If severe weather causes delays, your moving company should communicate proactively and adjust the timeline while keeping your belongings secure.

### Can I move during Thanksgiving week?

Yes, but expect challenges including heavy highway traffic, limited availability, and potentially higher rates. If possible, schedule your move for before November 20th or after November 28th for a smoother experience.

### How do binding estimates protect me?

A binding estimate guarantees your moving cost based on the inventory you provide. Unlike non-binding estimates that can change, a binding estimate protects you from unexpected charges on moving day.
