Moving with kids this summer? North Miami Beach is a fantastic choice for families. From July adventures to everyday activities, here's your guide to keeping the whole family entertained and connected in your new community.

## 1. Haulover Beach Park

**Address:** 10800 Collins Ave, Miami Beach, FL 33154

![Family Kids Activities Steps](/images/blog/2027/07/5-family-friendly-activities-in-north-miami-beach/family-kids-activities-steps.webp)


A favorite among locals featuring wide sandy shores perfect for swimming and sunbathing. The 7,500-square-foot skate park and pump track is great for kids, and there's a dog-friendly beach section between lifeguard houses 2 and 3.

## 2. Oleta River State Park

**Address:** 3400 NE 163rd St, North Miami Beach, FL 33160

Florida's largest urban park offers a quiet, secluded beach on Biscayne Bay with calm waters. Features biking trails, kayak rentals, and nature programs perfect for family adventures.

## 3. Frost Museum of Science

**Address:** 1101 Biscayne Blvd, Miami, FL 33132

An epic science museum and aquarium spread over 250,000 square feet. Four interlinked buildings house a planetarium, aquarium, and exhibition wings where kids can explore science, technology, and engineering.

## 4. Miami Children's Museum

**Address:** 980 MacArthur Causeway, Miami, FL 33132

![Family Kids Activities Tips](/images/blog/2027/07/5-family-friendly-activities-in-north-miami-beach/family-kids-activities-tips.webp)


Dedicated to enriching children's lives through hands-on exhibits. Baby STEAM programs for ages 1-3 blend science, technology, engineering, art, and math. Regular family fun days with special activities.

## 5. Surfside Beach

**Address:** Collins Ave at 93rd St, Surfside, FL

Just south of North Miami Beach, Surfside offers white-sand beaches with walking paths, playgrounds, and family amenities. The calm waters are perfect for young swimmers learning the waves.
