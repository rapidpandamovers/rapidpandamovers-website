Your safe weighs 800 pounds. The staircase in your Coral Gables home has a 90-degree turn. Moving day is next week. Without the right equipment and trained specialists, that safe stays put or something gets damaged.

## Why Safe Moving Requires Specialized Expertise

Home safes range from 200 to 2,500 pounds. Gun safes, fire safes, and commercial vaults all present unique challenges that standard moving equipment cannot handle. Our crews move 50+ safes monthly across Miami-Dade, from Brickell high-rises to Kendall single-family homes.

January offers ideal conditions for safe moving in South Florida. The cooler temperatures (highs around 75°F) mean less fatigue for crews handling heavy items. Plus, building elevators in Aventura and Sunny Isles condos are less congested than during peak season.

## Key Tips for Moving Your Safe in January

### Know Your Safe's Exact Weight

Before moving day, find the manufacturer's weight specification. A Liberty Fat Boy gun safe weighs 1,100 pounds empty, but add ammunition and it climbs to 1,400+. Our Doral warehouse crew once arrived at a home in Coconut Grove expecting a 500-pound safe only to find an 1,800-pound antique vault. The job required different equipment and a second trip.

### Book Two Weeks Ahead

January brings a wave of relocations as Miami residents act on New Year plans. Homeowners in Pinecrest and Palmetto Bay often schedule moves after the holidays. Our [**safe moving**](/safe-moving) crews book out 10-14 days in advance during this period.

### Clear the Path

Measure doorways, hallways, and any turns. Standard doors are 36 inches wide, but older homes in Coral Gables and Miami Shores sometimes have narrower openings. Remove doors from hinges if needed. Our crews bring door-hanging tools as standard equipment.

### Check Building Requirements

Moving a safe into a Downtown Miami or Brickell condo? Most buildings require 48-hour elevator reservations, a certificate of insurance (COI) naming the building, and freight elevator access. Miss this step and you reschedule.

## January Moving Checklist for Safe Owners

Before your move, complete these steps:

- Empty the safe completely (reduces weight and protects contents)
- Document the manufacturer, model, and weight specifications
- Photograph the safe from all angles for insurance purposes
- Measure every doorway and hallway between the safe and the truck
- Request building COI requirements if moving to a condo

### How much does it cost to move a safe in Miami?

Safe moving typically runs $150-$500 depending on weight, distance, and stairs. An 800-pound safe on a single-story Kendall home costs less than moving the same safe from a third-floor Wynwood walkup.

### Can regular movers handle safe moving?

Most cannot safely. Safes require specialized equipment and training. Attempting to move a heavy safe with furniture dollies risks injury and property damage.

### Do you need to empty the safe before moving?

Yes. Remove all contents, especially firearms and ammunition. This reduces weight and ensures nothing shifts during transport.
