Whether you're a long-time Miami resident or just making your June move to South Florida, there's always something new to discover. Here's our curated list to help you make the most of life in the Sunshine State this summer.

## 1. Star Island Estates

This exclusive island has housed Madonna, Diddy, and Gloria Estefan—requiring discrete, high-security moving services.

![Celebrity Mansion Luxury Checklist](/images/blog/2027/06/8-famous-miami-mansions-and-their-stories/celebrity-mansion-luxury-checklist.webp)


## 2. Versace Mansion (Casa Casuarina)

Gianni Versace's famous South Beach residence has seen multiple transformations and celebrity occupants.

## 3. Fisher Island Properties

Accessible only by boat, this ultra-exclusive community requires specialized logistics for any move.

## 4. Coral Gables Mansions

The historic Mediterranean-style estates in Coral Gables have housed numerous celebrities over the decades.

## 5. Miami Beach Penthouses

Luxury high-rise penthouses require coordinated elevator access and building management approval for moves.

![Celebrity Mansion Luxury Guide](/images/blog/2027/06/8-famous-miami-mansions-and-their-stories/celebrity-mansion-luxury-guide.webp)


## 6. Brickell Key Condominiums

These waterfront luxury towers have attracted international celebrities and require careful coordination for high-rise moves.

## 7. Coconut Grove Waterfront Estates

Historic estates along the bay have been home to various entertainment industry professionals over the years.

## 8. Golden Beach Mansions

This ultra-exclusive town north of Miami Beach features beachfront estates owned by business moguls and celebrities.
