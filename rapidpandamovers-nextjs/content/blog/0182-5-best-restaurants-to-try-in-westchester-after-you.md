Just moved to Westchester or planning your summer relocation? Food is one of the best ways to experience your new community. We've compiled this June guide to help you discover the culinary gems that make Westchester such a delicious place to call home.

## 1. Versailles Restaurant

No Westchester dining list is complete without Versailles, the "World's Most Famous Cuban Restaurant." Order the lechon asado (roasted pork) and finish with cafe con leche at the famous ventanita window.

![Restaurant Food Dining Checklist](/images/blog/2025/06/5-best-restaurants-to-try-in-westchester-after-your-move/restaurant-food-dining-checklist.webp)


## 2. El Pub Restaurant

A local favorite since 1974, El Pub serves authentic Cuban comfort food in generous portions. The vaca frita and ropa vieja keep regulars coming back for decades.

## 3. Pollo Tropical

Born in Westchester, this Caribbean-style chain started right here. Try the citrus-marinated grilled chicken with rice and beans—a Miami institution.

## 4. La Carreta

Open 24 hours, La Carreta is the go-to spot for late-night croquetas and Cuban sandwiches. Perfect for families craving authentic Cuban fare any time of day.

## 5. Islas Canarias

Known for their legendary churros and authentic Spanish-Cuban cuisine, Islas Canarias has been a Westchester staple since 1977. Don't skip dessert.
