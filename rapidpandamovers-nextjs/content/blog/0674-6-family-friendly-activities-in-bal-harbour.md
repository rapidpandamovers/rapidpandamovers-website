Moving with kids this summer? Bal Harbour offers fantastic activities for the whole family. Here are six spots where new residents can explore and settle into their oceanfront community.

## 1. Haulover Park Beach

Located at 10800 Collins Avenue, Haulover Park features a family-friendly beach section with shallow waters, perfect for kids and new swimmers. The southern end has a rocky jetty where children can spot fish, manatees, and pelicans. Picnic areas with grills, playgrounds, tennis courts, and a skate park round out the amenities. Every Tuesday night from 5 to 10pm, the Food Truck Round-Up at Bill Bird Marina brings local vendors and live entertainment.

## 2. Bal Harbour Shops Koi Ponds

This open-air shopping destination at 9700 Collins Avenue features beautiful gardens, koi ponds with goldfish and turtles, and fountains that captivate children. Visit during quieter weekday mornings when kids can explore freely. Grab lunch at one of the family-friendly restaurants after watching the fish. The serene grounds offer a pleasant break from the Florida heat.

## 3. Bal Harbour Waterfront Park

This village park on the Intracoastal offers a playground, walking paths, and waterfront views. During the holiday season, millions of twinkling lights transform Founders Circle and the waterfront into a magical display. The park hosts community events throughout the year, including stargazing nights with telescopes in partnership with Frost Museum of Science.

## 4. Bal Harbour Beach

One mile of white sand and gentle Atlantic waves stretches along the village's coastline. The beach is less crowded than South Beach, making it ideal for families. A seawall jetty extends into the ocean, perfect for fishing or spotting dolphins. Chair and umbrella rentals are available, and lifeguards patrol during peak hours.

## 5. Village Recreation Programs

The Bal Harbour Recreation Department at Village Hall runs programs for residents. Art & Sensory Enrichment classes for ages 2-5 and Super Soccer Stars sessions help toddlers develop skills through age-appropriate games. Programs typically begin in January, so register early after your move. Check balharbourfl.gov for the current schedule.

## 6. The Market at Bal Harbour

This seasonal farmers market features fresh produce, artisanal breads, local honey, fresh-squeezed juices, and coconut water. Kids enjoy sampling treats while parents stock up on farm-fresh ingredients. The market creates a community gathering spot where new residents can meet neighbors and discover local vendors.
