Moving isn't just about boxes and furniture. Some items in your home require specialized knowledge, equipment, and handling techniques. Here are six complex items that demand special attention during your move.

## 1. Grand and Upright Pianos

Pianos are among the most challenging items to move. They're extremely heavy (300-1,200 lbs), have delicate internal mechanisms, and can be damaged by temperature and humidity changes. Professional piano movers use specialized equipment and know how to protect both the instrument and your floors.

![Unusual Special Care Unique Guide](/images/blog/2028/04/6-complex-items-that-demand-special-attention/unusual-special-care-unique-guide.webp)


## 2. Antique Furniture

Antique pieces require custom crating and climate-controlled transport. Old wood is sensitive to humidity changes, and original finishes can be irreplaceable. Always document condition with photos before moving.

## 3. Large Artwork and Mirrors

Oversized paintings, sculptures, and mirrors need custom crating with proper cushioning. Glass should be taped in an X pattern to prevent shattering, and pieces should be transported vertically, never flat.

## 4. Hot Tubs and Pool Tables

Both require partial disassembly by specialists. Hot tubs need electrical disconnection and proper draining. Pool tables must be leveled precisely after reassembly—this is not a DIY job.

![Unusual Special Care Unique Steps](/images/blog/2028/04/6-complex-items-that-demand-special-attention/unusual-special-care-unique-steps.webp)


## 5. Wine Collections

Temperature-sensitive wine needs climate-controlled transport, especially in Miami's heat. Bottles should remain horizontal and avoid vibration. Serious collectors often use specialized wine moving services.

## 6. Gun Safes

Weighing 500-2,500 lbs, gun safes require specialized equipment like stair-climbing dollies and proper strapping. Contents should be removed before moving, and local regulations for transporting firearms must be followed.
