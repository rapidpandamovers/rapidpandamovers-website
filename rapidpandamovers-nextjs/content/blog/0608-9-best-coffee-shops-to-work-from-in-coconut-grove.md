Finding your perfect coffee spot is one of the first steps to feeling at home in a new neighborhood. Coconut Grove has a diverse café scene perfect for spring days—from traditional Cuban ventanitas to modern specialty roasters. Here's where to get your caffeine fix.

## 1. Start with the Ventanitas

Miami's walk-up Cuban coffee windows are the real deal. In Coconut Grove, find one near your house and make it your morning ritual. Café con leche runs about $2 and comes with free neighborhood gossip.

![Coffee Shop Cafe Remote Work Guide](/images/blog/2028/03/9-best-coffee-shops-to-work-from-in-coconut-grove/coffee-shop-cafe-remote-work-guide.webp)


## 2. Check Google Reviews for 'WiFi' and 'Outlets'

If you work remotely, filter Coconut Grove coffee shop reviews for these keywords. Not all cafes welcome laptop workers—find the ones that do before camping out.

## 3. Visit at Different Times

That perfect Coconut Grove coffee spot might be packed at 9am but empty at 2pm. Try potential favorites at various hours before committing to a routine.

## 4. Ask About Their Beans

Miami has a growing specialty coffee scene. In Coconut Grove, look for shops that can tell you where their beans come from. It's a sign they take coffee seriously.

## 5. Follow the Construction Workers

The best cheap, strong coffee in Coconut Grove is often wherever the work crews go. These spots prioritize speed, strength, and value.

## 6. Look for Pastelito Pairings

Cuban coffee and guava pastelitos are a Miami institution. Find a Coconut Grove spot that makes both fresh, and you've found your new happy place.

![Coffee Shop Cafe Remote Work Overview](/images/blog/2028/03/9-best-coffee-shops-to-work-from-in-coconut-grove/coffee-shop-cafe-remote-work-overview.webp)


## 7. Check Hours Before Making It Your Spot

Many Coconut Grove coffee shops close by 3pm. If you need afternoon caffeine, verify hours before getting too attached.

## 8. Tip Well and Become a Regular

Once you find your Coconut Grove coffee spot, tip consistently. Being recognized as a regular means better service and genuine local connections.

## 9. Explore Beyond Main Street

Some of the best Coconut Grove coffee shops are tucked away on side streets or in unexpected plazas. Take a walk around and you might discover a hidden gem that becomes your go-to spot.
