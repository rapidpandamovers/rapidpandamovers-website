Moving during July? You're not alone. The summer months bring a surge of relocations across Miami-Dade County, making professional military moving more important than ever.

## Understanding Your PCS Move Options

Military families have three primary options for PCS (Permanent Change of Station) moves. Understanding each helps you make the best choice for your situation.

![Military Veterans American Flag Checklist](/images/blog/2027/07/summer-military-moving-insights-for-miami/military-veterans-american-flag-checklist.webp)


### Household Goods (HHG) Move
The government contracts with moving companies to pack, ship, and deliver your belongings. This is the most common option for military families with significant household goods.

**Pros:**
- No out-of-pocket costs
- Professional packing and handling
- Insurance coverage included

**Cons:**
- Less control over timing
- Can't always choose your moving company
- May have delays during peak season

### Personally Procured Move (PPM/DITY)
You manage the move yourself or hire a commercial mover. The government reimburses you based on what it would have cost for an HHG move.

**2025 Update:** As of May 2025, the Department of Defense has temporarily increased PPM reimbursement to **130% of the government's estimated cost** (through September 30, 2025). This means more money in your pocket if you manage expenses well.

**Potential Savings:**
- Many military families earn $2,000-$5,000 or more through DITY moves
- You keep the difference between your costs and the government estimate

**Requirements:**
- DD Form 2278 (Application for DITY Move)
- Weight tickets (empty and loaded)
- All receipts for moving expenses
- Rental agreements for truck/trailer
- Copy of PCS orders
- Submit documentation within 45 days of completing your move

### Partial DITY Move
Combine both options: the government moves large items while you handle smaller belongings. This works well when you want to keep valuables with you or have items needing special care.

## Weight Allowance by Rank

Your moving weight allowance depends on your rank and dependency status. Exceeding your allowance means paying for the overage out of pocket, so tracking your weight matters.

### Tips to Stay Within Allowance
- Create a home inventory before packing
- Donate or sell items you no longer need
- Weigh your belongings before the move if possible
- A video walkthrough helps if you need to file a loss/damage claim

## 2025 PCS Entitlements

Current entitlements to budget for:

![Military Veterans American Flag Guide](/images/blog/2027/07/summer-military-moving-insights-for-miami/military-veterans-american-flag-guide.webp)


- **Mileage Rate**: 22 cents per mile for DITY moves
- **Per Diem**: Covers meals and lodging during travel
- **Dislocation Allowance (DLA)**: Helps offset move-in costs
- **Temporary Lodging Expense (TLE)**: For temporary housing

## CONUS vs. OCONUS Moves

**Important:** PPM/DITY moves are only authorized for CONUS (continental United States) moves. All OCONUS moves (including Hawaii and Alaska) require government-arranged shipping due to complex timelines and customs requirements.

## Miami-Specific Military Moving Tips

### South Florida Bases and Areas
Military families in South Florida often connect with:
- Homestead Air Reserve Base
- U.S. Southern Command (SOUTHCOM) at Doral
- Coast Guard installations
- Various National Guard facilities

### Summer Heat Considerations
July moves in Miami require heat awareness:
- Schedule loading for early morning hours
- Protect electronics and heat-sensitive items
- Stay hydrated during any DIY portions
- Consider climate-controlled transport for valuables

### Hurricane Season
Summer PCS moves coincide with hurricane season (June-November):
- Have backup dates ready
- Keep important documents accessible
- Know evacuation routes at both old and new locations
- Discuss weather policies with your transportation office

## Steps to Start Your PCS Move

1. **Visit your Transportation Office (TO)**
   - Get briefed on your options
   - Understand your entitlements
   - Receive required forms

![Military Veterans American Flag Overview](/images/blog/2027/07/summer-military-moving-insights-for-miami/military-veterans-american-flag-overview.webp)


2. **Set up your DPS Account**
   - Defense Personal Property System manages your move
   - Schedule pickup and delivery
   - Track your shipment

3. **Start Early**
   - Begin decluttering 8 weeks before
   - Get estimates if doing a PPM
   - Book services during peak summer

4. **Document Everything**
   - Photograph valuable items
   - Keep all receipts
   - Note pre-existing damage on furniture

## Filing Claims for Loss or Damage

If items are lost or damaged during an HHG move:
- Report damage within 75 days of delivery
- File a claim within 9 months
- Provide documentation (photos, receipts, repair estimates)
- Use DPS to file and track your claim
