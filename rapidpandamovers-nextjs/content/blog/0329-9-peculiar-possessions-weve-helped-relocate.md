Whether you're a long-time Miami resident or just making your June move to South Florida, there's always something new to discover. Here's our curated list to help you make the most of life in the Sunshine State this summer.

## 1. Grand Pianos

These massive instruments require specialized equipment, multiple people, and expertise to avoid damage to the piano and property.

![Funny Unusual Stories Checklist](/images/blog/2026/06/9-peculiar-possessions-weve-helped-relocate/funny-unusual-stories-checklist.webp)


## 2. Wine Collections

Temperature sensitivity, fragile bottles, and proper positioning require climate-controlled handling and custom crating.

## 3. Hot Tubs and Spas

Draining, disconnecting, and moving these heavy items requires professionals familiar with the process.

## 4. Large Aquariums

Fish need separate transport; tanks must be drained, carefully padded, and moved level to prevent cracking.

## 5. Pool Tables

Slate tops require disassembly by specialists. Improperly moved pool tables never play the same.

## 6. Fine Art and Sculptures

Custom crating, climate control, and white-glove handling protect valuable artwork during transit.

![Funny Unusual Stories Guide](/images/blog/2026/06/9-peculiar-possessions-weve-helped-relocate/funny-unusual-stories-guide.webp)


## 7. Full Gym Equipment

Treadmills, weight machines, and home gyms are heavy and awkward. Professional movers have the equipment to handle them.

## 8. Classic Cars

Valuable vehicles need enclosed transport and special handling. Regular moving trucks aren't appropriate.

## 9. Medical Equipment

Hospital beds, oxygen concentrators, and mobility devices require careful handling and often need to be set up immediately at the destination.
