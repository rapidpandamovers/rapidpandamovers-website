Living near Golden Beach means access to some of Miami's best coffee spots, perfect for getting work done. Whether you're looking for strong WiFi, great espresso, or a quiet atmosphere, here are the top cafes for remote workers in and around Golden Beach.

## 1. Wolsen Caffe

**Address:** 17100 Collins Ave, Ste 105, Sunny Isles Beach, FL 33160

![Coffee Shop Cafe Remote Work Checklist](/images/blog/2027/06/7-top-cafes-for-remote-workers-in-golden-beach/coffee-shop-cafe-remote-work-checklist.webp)


Open daily 9:30am-9pm, Wolsen Caffe offers fresh, healthy cuisine alongside excellent coffee and tea. A cozy atmosphere with high-speed WiFi makes it perfect for remote work.

## 2. The Coffee Store

**Address:** 18060 Collins Ave, Sunny Isles Beach, FL

An Argentinian bakery established in 1998 offering high-quality coffee, medialunas, empanadas, and pastries. European-style cafe perfect for breakfast, brunch, or a working lunch.

## 3. Filomena's Bean Coffee

**Address:** Sunny Isles Beach, FL

![Coffee Shop Cafe Remote Work Essentials](/images/blog/2027/06/7-top-cafes-for-remote-workers-in-golden-beach/coffee-shop-cafe-remote-work-essentials.webp)


A perfect day-to-day go-to spot with good music, excellent energy, and delicious drinks. Designed as a place to work, meet friends, and enjoy great coffee.

## 4. Naughty Coffee

**Address:** Collins Ave, Sunny Isles Beach, FL

One of the top-rated coffee spots in the area, known for creative drinks and a trendy atmosphere. Great for a change of scenery from home office.

## 5. Neverland Coffee Bar

**Address:** Sunny Isles Beach, FL

![Coffee Shop Cafe Remote Work Planning](/images/blog/2027/06/7-top-cafes-for-remote-workers-in-golden-beach/coffee-shop-cafe-remote-work-planning.webp)


A specialty coffee shop with a whimsical atmosphere. Features quality espresso drinks and light bites in a comfortable setting for working remotely.

## 6. Ralph's Coffee

**Address:** Sunny Isles Beach, FL

Elegant coffee shop offering premium brews in a sophisticated setting. Perfect for important video calls or focused work sessions.

## 7. Granier Bakery

**Address:** Sunny Isles Beach, FL

![Coffee Shop Cafe Remote Work Steps](/images/blog/2027/06/7-top-cafes-for-remote-workers-in-golden-beach/coffee-shop-cafe-remote-work-steps.webp)


A European-style bakery with excellent coffee and fresh pastries. Offers a quiet atmosphere ideal for morning work sessions.
