Not everything in your home can be tossed in a cardboard box and loaded onto a truck. Some possessions require specialized knowledge, equipment, and handling techniques that only professional movers can provide. Here are six specialty items that demand expert attention during your next relocation.

![Professional movers carefully handling a grand piano during relocation](/images/blog/2030/11/6-specialty-possessions-for-expert-relocation/specialty-items-grand-piano-moving.jpg)

## 1. Grand Pianos

Grand pianos are among the most challenging items to move. Weighing between 500 and 1,200 pounds, these instruments contain thousands of precisely calibrated parts including hammers, strings, and soundboards that can be damaged by improper handling. Professional piano movers use specialized equipment including piano boards, skids, and heavy-duty straps to safely transport these instruments. They understand how to navigate tight doorways and staircases while keeping the piano level and protected.

![Unusual Special Care Unique Essentials](/images/blog/2030/11/6-specialty-possessions-for-expert-relocation/unusual-special-care-unique-essentials.webp)


## 2. Wine Collections

Fine wine represents a significant investment that requires careful handling. Temperature fluctuations, vibration, and improper positioning can ruin even the most prized vintages. Expert movers use climate-controlled vehicles and custom crating systems designed to keep bottles horizontal and cushioned. For valuable collections, they coordinate move timing to avoid extreme temperatures and ensure wines arrive at their destination in perfect condition.

![Wine collection being packed in climate-controlled crating for moving](/images/blog/2030/11/6-specialty-possessions-for-expert-relocation/specialty-items-wine-collection-moving.jpg)

## 3. Hot Tubs and Spas

Moving a hot tub involves much more than physical strength. These units must be properly drained, disconnected from electrical systems, and prepared for transport. Professional movers understand the specific requirements for different hot tub models and have the equipment to move these bulky, awkward items without damaging your property or the unit itself. Improper moving can crack shells, damage pumps, or void warranties.

## 4. Large Aquariums

Aquariums present unique challenges that require both moving expertise and basic aquaculture knowledge. The tank itself must be completely drained and moved level to prevent stress cracks. Fish, corals, and plants need separate transport in temperature-controlled containers with proper aeration. Professional movers coordinate the timing to minimize stress on aquatic life and can help with setup at your new location.

![Unusual Special Care Unique Overview](/images/blog/2030/11/6-specialty-possessions-for-expert-relocation/unusual-special-care-unique-overview.webp)


## 5. Pool Tables

A pool table's slate top typically weighs 400 to 800 pounds and must be perfectly level for proper play. Professional pool table movers disassemble these pieces with specialized tools, carefully transport the slate sections with proper padding, and reassemble everything at your new home. Tables moved by inexperienced movers often develop warping, uneven surfaces, or damaged felt that affects gameplay permanently.

## 6. Fine Art and Sculptures

Valuable artwork requires white-glove handling from start to finish. Professional art movers assess each piece individually, creating custom crating solutions that account for size, fragility, and material composition. Climate-controlled transport protects against temperature and humidity damage, while specialized insurance coverage provides peace of mind for irreplaceable pieces.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How much does it cost to move specialty items?**
Costs vary based on the item type, size, distance, and required services. Piano moves typically start at $200-$500 for local transport, while complex items like large aquariums or extensive art collections may cost more. Request a detailed quote for accurate pricing.

**Should I move specialty items myself to save money?**
While DIY moving might seem cost-effective, the risk of damage to valuable items often outweighs potential savings. Repair or replacement costs for damaged pianos, artwork, or wine collections can easily exceed professional moving fees.

**How far in advance should I book specialty moving services?**
Book specialty moving services at least 2-4 weeks before your move date. During peak moving season (May-September), consider booking 4-6 weeks ahead to ensure availability.

**Can all moving companies handle specialty items?**
Not all movers have the equipment or expertise for specialty items. Look for companies with specific experience in your item type, appropriate insurance coverage, and positive reviews from customers with similar moving needs.
