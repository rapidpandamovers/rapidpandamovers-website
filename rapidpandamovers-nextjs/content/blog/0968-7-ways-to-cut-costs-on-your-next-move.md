Whether you're a long-time Miami resident or just making your July move to South Florida, there's always something new to discover. Here's our curated list to help you make the most of life in the Sunshine State this summer.

## 1. Declutter Before Getting Quotes

Fewer items equals lower costs. Aggressively declutter before movers assess your load—every box saved is money saved.

![Budget Saving Money Affordable Checklist](/images/blog/2030/07/7-ways-to-cut-costs-on-your-next-move/budget-saving-money-affordable-checklist.webp)


## 2. Time Your Move Strategically

Mid-week, mid-month moves cost less than weekend or end-of-month moves. Avoid peak summer months when possible.

## 3. Get Multiple Quotes

Compare at least three estimates from reputable companies. Make sure they're all based on the same inventory for accurate comparison.

![Budget Saving Money Affordable Essentials](/images/blog/2030/07/7-ways-to-cut-costs-on-your-next-move/budget-saving-money-affordable-essentials.webp)


## 4. Do Your Own Packing

Professional packing adds cost. If you have time, pack everything yourself with proper materials from a discount store.

## 5. Source Free Boxes

Check with local stores, Facebook Marketplace, and Nextdoor for free moving boxes. Many people give them away after their moves.

![Budget Saving Money Affordable Planning](/images/blog/2030/07/7-ways-to-cut-costs-on-your-next-move/budget-saving-money-affordable-planning.webp)


## 6. Ask About Discounts

Many movers offer military, senior, or AAA discounts. Ask about any available promotions before booking your move.

## 7. Move During Off-Peak Hours

Early morning or late afternoon moves may cost less. Some companies offer reduced rates for flexible scheduling.
