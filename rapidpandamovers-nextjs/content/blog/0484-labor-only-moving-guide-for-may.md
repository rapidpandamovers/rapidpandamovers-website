## Key Tips for Labor Only Moving in May

### Plan Ahead for Peak Times

May can be a busy time for moving companies. With graduation ceremonies at UM, FIU, and Miami Dade College, plus lease turnovers in Coral Gables and Coconut Grove, scheduling your labor only moving early ensures you get your preferred date and time.

### Have everything packed and the truck ready when movers arrive

This single step can save hours of stress and potential problems on moving day. Our experienced crews have seen what happens when this step is skipped.

### Consider the Weather

South Florida's spring weather brings afternoon thunderstorms nearly every day. These quick downpours can pop up within minutes, especially in areas like Kendall and Doral. Professional movers know how to protect your belongings from humidity, rain, and heat exposure during transport, and they time loading and unloading around weather patterns.

### Prepare Your Belongings

Take time to inventory your items before the move. This is especially important for labor only moving, as proper documentation helps ensure everything arrives safely at your new location.
