Indian Creek is one of South Florida's most exclusive communities, but the private island itself has no commercial gyms. Residents typically head to nearby Aventura, Surfside, or Bal Harbour for fitness. After helping families move to this area, we've learned which facilities Indian Creek residents prefer. Here are seven gyms and studios worth checking out.

## 1. Equinox Aventura

**19501 Biscayne Blvd, Aventura, FL 33180**

![From Above Composition Of Dumbbells And  2](/images/blog/2026/07/7-best-gyms-and-fitness-studios-in-indian-creek/from-above-composition-of-dumbbells-and--2.webp)


This upscale fitness club inside Aventura Mall offers everything you'd expect from a luxury gym: pristine locker rooms, eucalyptus towels, top-tier equipment, and group classes ranging from yoga to cycling. The spa services and personal training programs attract Indian Creek residents who want a full wellness experience. Monthly memberships start around $275. The location makes it easy to combine workouts with shopping or dining.

## 2. Barry's Aventura

**2920 NE 207th St, Suite 105, Aventura, FL 33180**

Barry's signature red room workouts combine treadmill intervals with strength training on the floor. Each 50-minute class burns serious calories while building lean muscle. The Aventura studio opened to serve the growing demand from residents in Indian Creek, Sunny Isles, and surrounding areas who want high-intensity workouts without driving to Miami Beach. Classes run from early morning through evening.

## 3. Orangetheory Fitness Aventura

**18839 Biscayne Blvd, Suite A, Aventura, FL 33180**

Heart rate-based interval training is what sets Orangetheory apart. You'll wear a monitor during class and try to stay in specific heart rate zones for optimal calorie burn. The Aventura location has a strong community feel, with members tracking progress and celebrating milestones together. Hours run Monday through Thursday 5am-7:30pm, with weekend morning classes available.

## 4. LA Fitness Aventura

**3455 NE 207th St, Miami, FL 33180**

![Spacious Fitness Center Featuring Treadm 4](/images/blog/2026/07/7-best-gyms-and-fitness-studios-in-indian-creek/spacious-fitness-center-featuring-treadm-4.webp)


For a more traditional gym experience at a lower price point, LA Fitness delivers solid equipment and amenities. The Aventura location has a pool, basketball courts, group fitness classes, and plenty of cardio and weight machines. Hours are generous (5am-10pm on weekdays), and the membership price makes it accessible for those who want gym access without premium pricing.

## 5. Olympia Gym and Fitness

**20335 Biscayne Blvd, Suite L-201, Aventura, FL 33180**

This bodybuilder-friendly gym focuses on serious weight training with a comprehensive selection of free weights and machines. The no-frills environment appeals to those who want to lift heavy without the spa amenities or boutique pricing. Personal trainers are available, and the community of dedicated lifters keeps the atmosphere focused and motivating.

## 6. Kodiak Fitness Center Gold

**18260 Collins Ave, Sunny Isles Beach, FL 33160**

Located directly on Collins Avenue in Sunny Isles, Kodiak offers a convenient option for Indian Creek residents heading north. This state-of-the-art facility has expert trainers and modern equipment. Hours run 7am-10pm on weekdays and 8am-8pm on weekends. The all-inclusive membership covers classes and amenities without nickel-and-diming for extras.

## 7. Fitness Time

**17050 Collins Ave, Sunny Isles Beach, FL 33160**

![Women Lifting Dumbbells In Front Of Mirr 1](/images/blog/2026/07/7-best-gyms-and-fitness-studios-in-indian-creek/women-lifting-dumbbells-in-front-of-mirr-1.webp)


A personal training-focused studio that provides customized workout programs. Fitness Time attracts clients who want individual attention rather than crowded group classes. The trainers work with all fitness levels, from post-rehab clients rebuilding strength to athletes training for specific goals. The smaller setting means more personalized service.

## Finding Your Fit

Each gym serves different needs. Equinox and Barry's cater to those who want luxury and intensity. LA Fitness and Olympia work well for traditional gym-goers. Kodiak and Fitness Time offer solid mid-range options in Sunny Isles. Most offer trial classes or guest passes, so visit a few before committing to a membership.
