Just moved to Aventura or planning your spring relocation? One of the best things about this vibrant city is its abundance of green spaces perfect for relaxing, exercising, or simply enjoying nature. Here are six green spaces in Aventura that locals love.

## 1. Founders Park

Aventura's premier park features walking trails, playgrounds, and beautiful lake views. The 12-acre park includes tennis courts, a fitness zone, and plenty of shaded picnic areas. It's the perfect spot for a morning jog or an afternoon with the family.

![Parks Nature Outdoor Checklist](/images/blog/2028/03/6-green-spaces-in-aventura-perfect-for-relaxing/parks-nature-outdoor-checklist.webp)


## 2. Waterways Dog Park

If you have a furry friend, this dedicated dog park is a must-visit. Located along the Aventura waterways, it offers separate areas for large and small dogs, with plenty of shade and water stations.

![Parks Nature Outdoor Overview](/images/blog/2028/03/6-green-spaces-in-aventura-perfect-for-relaxing/parks-nature-outdoor-overview.webp)


## 3. Country Club Golf Course Green Spaces

The areas surrounding Turnberry Isle's golf courses provide beautiful walking paths and scenic views. Non-golfers can enjoy the landscaped grounds and peaceful atmosphere.

## 4. Don Soffer Exercise Trail

This popular paved trail winds through Aventura's most scenic areas. Perfect for jogging, cycling, or a leisurely walk, the trail connects several neighborhoods and offers waterfront views.

![Parks Nature Outdoor Steps](/images/blog/2028/03/6-green-spaces-in-aventura-perfect-for-relaxing/parks-nature-outdoor-steps.webp)


## 5. Aventura Circle Park

A smaller but charming neighborhood park with mature trees providing excellent shade. It's ideal for a quiet afternoon reading session or a peaceful lunch break.

## 6. Intracoastal Waterway Walkways

The walking paths along Aventura's Intracoastal Waterway offer stunning water views and gentle breezes. Time your visit for early morning or sunset for the best experience.
