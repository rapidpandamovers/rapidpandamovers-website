## Key Tips for Special Needs Moving in March

### Book Two to Three Weeks Early

March brings spring break travelers and end-of-quarter corporate moves to Miami. Special needs moves require our most experienced crews, and availability fills quickly. Families moving to Homestead, Miami Beach, or Wynwood should schedule by mid-February for March dates.

### Share Medical Equipment Details Before Moving Day

Send us your equipment list at least one week ahead. Include model numbers for hospital beds, oxygen concentrators, and lift chairs. Our crews review this information to bring the right tools and plan proper disassembly sequences. Skipping this step once cost a family in Coral Gables three hours while we sourced the right hex keys for a specialized wheelchair lift.

### Plan Around Weather Patterns

March afternoons in Miami can shift from sunny to storming in 20 minutes. We schedule equipment loading for morning hours when weather is most stable. Temperature-sensitive medical supplies stay in climate-controlled portions of our trucks until the last possible moment.

### Create a Medical Supply Go-Bag

Keep three days of medications, medical supplies, and essential equipment with you during the move. This includes CPAP machines, nebulizers, and anything your loved one needs for daily care. These items never go on the truck.
