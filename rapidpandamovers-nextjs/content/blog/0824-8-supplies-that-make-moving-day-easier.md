Moving day chaos hits everyone. Boxes everywhere, movers asking questions, keys to hand off, and somehow you need to find the coffee maker before noon. The difference between a smooth move and a stressful one often comes down to having the right supplies within arm's reach. Here are eight items our Miami moving crews see make the biggest difference.

## 1. Important Documents

Passports, IDs, birth certificates, lease or closing papers, and vehicle registrations belong with you, not in a moving box. Keep these in your personal bag or vehicle throughout the day. If you're moving within Miami-Dade, you'll also want utility account numbers handy for address transfers with FPL and Miami-Dade Water.

![A Moving Truck Parked Outside A Modern A 4](/images/blog/2029/08/8-supplies-that-make-moving-day-easier/a-moving-truck-parked-outside-a-modern-a-4.webp)


## 2. Medications and First Aid

Current prescriptions, over-the-counter essentials, and any medical devices you might need in the next 24 hours stay with you. Miami's heat and humidity can make moving physically demanding, so include sunscreen, insect repellent, and pain relievers in your kit.

## 3. Phone Chargers and Power Banks

Dead phones on moving day mean missed calls from movers, utility companies, and anyone trying to help coordinate.

![A U Haul Moving Truck Parked On Gravel W 3](/images/blog/2029/08/8-supplies-that-make-moving-day-easier/a-u-haul-moving-truck-parked-on-gravel-w-3.webp)


## 4. Basic Toolkit

Screwdriver set, pliers, box cutter, and hammer are all useful for last-minute needs at both locations. You'll use these more than you expect.

## 5. Cleaning Supplies

Paper towels, all-purpose cleaner, and trash bags help with quick clean-ups during the transition at both old and new locations.

![High Angle View Of An Asda Delivery Truc 1](/images/blog/2029/08/8-supplies-that-make-moving-day-easier/high-angle-view-of-an-asda-delivery-truc-1.webp)


## 6. First-Night Box

Toiletries, towels, bedding, change of clothes, and anything you'll need to sleep comfortably night one without unpacking everything.

## 7. Snacks and Water

Keep water bottles and easy snacks accessible. Moving is exhausting, and you don't want to stop for food runs during the busiest part of the day. Pro tip: freeze some water bottles the night before. They'll thaw throughout the day and stay cold longer in Miami's heat, whether you're moving to Brickell, Kendall, or Aventura.

![U Haul Moving Truck Parked On Roadside W 2](/images/blog/2029/08/8-supplies-that-make-moving-day-easier/u-haul-moving-truck-parked-on-roadside-w-2.webp)


## 8. Furniture Sliders and Moving Blankets

Protect your floors and furniture during the chaos. These simple tools prevent scratches and make heavy items easier to maneuver. Many Coral Gables and Coconut Grove homes have hardwood floors, and condo buildings in Doral and Miami Beach often require floor protection during moves.
