Some items are simply too heavy, fragile, or complex for DIY moving. Attempting to move these yourself risks injury, property damage, or ruined belongings. After handling thousands of specialty moves across Miami-Dade County, here are the 10 items our crews see homeowners struggle with most.

## 1. Grand Pianos

A grand piano can weigh between 500 and 1,200 pounds, with delicate internal mechanisms and a finish that scratches easily. Miami's humidity adds another layer of complexity since temperature changes during transport can affect tuning stability. Our crews use piano boards, specialized dollies, and interior padding to protect both the instrument and your Coral Gables hardwood floors.

## 2. Wine Collections

Miami's heat can destroy a wine collection in hours. Bottles need temperature-controlled transport, proper positioning to keep corks moist, and custom crating to prevent breakage. Many Brickell and Coconut Grove residents have invested thousands in their collections, so professional handling with climate-controlled vehicles is worth every penny.

## 3. Hot Tubs and Spas

Draining, disconnecting electrical components, and moving a 400-600 pound spa shell requires professionals who understand the process. In Florida's humid climate, improper storage during the move can lead to mold growth inside jets and plumbing. Our team disconnects, transports, and reconnects your spa correctly.

## 4. Large Aquariums

Fish need separate transport in insulated containers with battery-powered aerators. The tank itself must be completely drained, carefully padded, and moved perfectly level to prevent glass cracking from uneven stress. Miami's summer heat means fish can overheat quickly during transport, so timing and climate control matter.

## 5. Pool Tables

Slate tops on quality pool tables can weigh 450 pounds or more and require professional disassembly. Attempting to move a pool table assembled will result in warped felt, damaged pockets, and a table that never plays level again. Reassembly requires precise leveling, something our specialists handle for homes throughout Kendall, Doral, and Pinecrest.

## 6. Fine Art and Sculptures

Custom crating, climate control, and white-glove handling protect valuable artwork during transit. Miami has a thriving art scene, and we regularly move pieces from collectors in Wynwood and the Design District. Each piece gets individual attention, with custom-built crates and temperature-controlled transport when needed.

## 7. Full Gym Equipment

Treadmills, ellipticals, and multi-station weight machines are heavy, awkward, and easy to damage. Professional movers have the equipment to handle 400+ pound machines safely and the expertise to reassemble them correctly. Many Aventura and Sunny Isles high-rise residents have home gyms that require elevator reservations and building coordination.

## 8. Classic Cars

Valuable vehicles need enclosed transport and special handling. Regular moving trucks can expose cars to road debris, weather, and improper loading. For Miami car collectors, enclosed auto transport with soft tie-downs and climate control keeps your investment safe during relocation.

## 9. Antique Furniture

Delicate wood, intricate carvings, and aged joints require expert handling. Miami's humidity can cause wood to swell, so antiques need proper wrapping that allows airflow. Improper moving techniques can cause irreversible damage to heirloom pieces that have survived generations.

## 10. Safes and Gun Cabinets

These extremely heavy items often exceed 500 pounds. Professional movers have specialized equipment like safe dollies and stair climbing gear. Attempting to move a safe yourself risks serious injury and floor damage. Our crews know how to handle heavy safes in multi-story homes and condos throughout Miami-Dade.
