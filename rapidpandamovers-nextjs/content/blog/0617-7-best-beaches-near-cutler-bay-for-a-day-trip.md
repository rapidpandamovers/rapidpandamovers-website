Living near Cutler Bay means easy access to some of Florida's best beaches—perfect for spring outings. Whether you're looking for family-friendly shores, water sports, or quiet relaxation, here are seven coastal destinations worth your time.

## 1. Homestead Bayfront Park

Just 15 minutes south of Cutler Bay, this hidden gem offers calm waters perfect for families, a marina, and stunning views of Biscayne Bay. The beach area is less crowded than most Miami beaches.

![Beach Ocean Day Trip Guide](/images/blog/2028/04/7-best-beaches-near-cutler-bay-for-a-day-trip/beach-ocean-day-trip-guide.webp)


## 2. Matheson Hammock Park

A 20-minute drive north brings you to this unique atoll pool beach. The man-made lagoon provides calm, warm waters ideal for kids. The park also features excellent picnic areas and a waterfront restaurant.

## 3. Crandon Park Beach (Key Biscayne)

About 30 minutes away, this consistently top-rated beach offers over two miles of shoreline, calm waters, and abundant amenities including restrooms, picnic areas, and lifeguards.

## 4. Bill Baggs Cape Florida State Park

At the southern tip of Key Biscayne, this beach features the historic Cape Florida Lighthouse, excellent swimming conditions, and beautiful natural surroundings. Perfect for a full-day adventure.

## 5. Virginia Key Beach

This restored historic beach is about 25 minutes from Cutler Bay. It offers a quieter alternative to South Beach with good facilities, kayak rentals, and a mountain biking trail nearby.

## 6. Haulover Beach Park

Head north for about 40 minutes to find this popular beach with excellent swimming, fishing pier, and food concessions. The northern section is clothing-optional, so choose your spot accordingly.

## 7. Key Largo Beaches

Take a scenic 45-minute drive south into the Keys for crystal-clear waters and excellent snorkeling at John Pennekamp Coral Reef State Park. The underwater scenery is unmatched in South Florida.
