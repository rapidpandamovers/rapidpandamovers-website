## About Bay Harbor Islands

Bay Harbor Islands is a prestigious town comprising two islands - East Island and West Island - nestled along Biscayne Bay in northeastern Miami-Dade County. With approximately 6,000 residents, this intimate community offers a relaxed lifestyle in the heart of South Florida's most desirable area.

## Neighborhoods and Island Character

### West Island
Exclusively single-family residences on quiet, tree-lined streets. Many homes feature private docks with direct bay access, perfect for boat owners who want to skip marina fees. The streets stay quiet, with minimal through traffic, giving it a suburban feel despite being minutes from Miami Beach. Expect to see families walking dogs, kids riding bikes, and neighbors who wave hello.

### East Island
A mix of multifamily properties, commercial areas, and the community's K-8 school. New luxury developments like La Baia and Onda Residences are transforming the waterfront with modern amenities and stunning bay views. Kane Concourse runs through East Island with restaurants, cafes, and boutiques within walking distance. The island has a more urban energy compared to West Island's residential calm.

## Lifestyle and Recreation

Bay Harbor Islands residents enjoy a water-focused lifestyle year-round. The bay stays calm enough for paddleboarding most mornings, and you can kayak to nearby islands for a change of scenery. Direct access to Biscayne Bay and the Intracoastal Waterway means boaters can reach open ocean in under 30 minutes.

**Popular activities include:**
- Morning paddleboard sessions on the calm bay waters
- Sunset boat rides through the Intracoastal
- Walking to Bal Harbour Shops for designer shopping and fine dining
- Beach days at nearby Surfside or Haulover Beach
- Dining at waterfront restaurants along Kane Concourse

The weather stays warm year-round, though summer afternoons bring brief thunderstorms. Most residents plan outdoor activities for mornings May through October.

### How much does it cost to move to Bay Harbor Islands?

### What are the condo move-in requirements in Bay Harbor Islands?

Most Bay Harbor Islands condos require 48-72 hour advance notice, a Certificate of Insurance (COI) from your moving company, and elevator reservation. Some buildings restrict move-in hours to weekdays only. We handle all paperwork and coordinate directly with building management.

### Is Bay Harbor Islands safe?

Bay Harbor Islands maintains its own police department and consistently ranks as one of the safest communities in Miami-Dade County. The small-town atmosphere means residents know their neighbors, and the island layout limits through traffic.

### How long does it take to drive from Bay Harbor Islands to downtown Miami?

Expect 25-30 minutes to Brickell or downtown Miami in normal traffic. Rush hour can extend this to 45 minutes or more. Many residents use Collins Avenue or I-95 depending on their destination.
