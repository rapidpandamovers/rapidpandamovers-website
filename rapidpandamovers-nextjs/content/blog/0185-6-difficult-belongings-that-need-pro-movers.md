Some belongings just can't go on a regular moving truck. They're too heavy, too fragile, too oddly shaped, or too valuable to risk with standard handling. Here are six items that Miami homeowners often underestimate until moving day arrives.

## 1. Grand Pianos

A grand piano weighs between 500 and 1,200 pounds, with delicate internal mechanisms that can shift out of tune from a single bump. Moving one requires piano boards, specialized straps, and a crew that knows how to handle doorways and stairs without scratching hardwood floors. In Miami's humid climate, pianos also need climate-controlled transport to prevent soundboard damage.

## 2. Wine Collections

Miami's summer heat can ruin a wine collection in hours. Bottles need to remain horizontal, at consistent temperatures between 55-65 degrees. Professional movers use climate-controlled trucks and custom crating to protect your investment. If you're moving from a Coral Gables wine cellar to a new home in Pinecrest, temperature control during the short drive is just as important as during long-distance hauls.

## 3. Hot Tubs and Spas

That 800-pound spa in your Kendall backyard needs to be drained, disconnected from electrical, and transported without cracking the shell. Most hot tubs won't fit through standard gates or doorways, requiring crane lifts or partial disassembly. Our crews work with local electricians to ensure safe disconnection and reconnection at your new Miami home.

## 4. Large Aquariums

Fish stress easily during moves, and a 100-gallon tank can weigh over 1,000 pounds when filled. The tank must be completely drained, fish transported separately in temperature-controlled containers, and the tank moved perfectly level to prevent glass stress fractures. Many Miami homeowners with saltwater setups near Biscayne Bay work with specialized aquarium movers to protect exotic fish collections.

## 5. Pool Tables

A standard pool table weighs 700-1,000 pounds, with a three-piece slate top that must be disassembled by specialists. Each slate piece weighs around 150 pounds and requires perfect re-leveling at your new home. Improperly moved pool tables warp, develop dead spots, and never roll true again. Miami's many game rooms and entertainment spaces make pool table moving a common request for our crews.

## 6. Fine Art and Sculptures

From Wynwood gallery pieces to family heirlooms, artwork requires museum-quality handling. Custom wooden crates, acid-free packing materials, and climate control protect against Miami's humidity and heat. Large sculptures need rigging equipment and padded truck interiors. Our white-glove service includes detailed condition reports before and after transport.

## Why Professional Moving Matters for Specialty Items

These six items share common challenges: extreme weight, fragile components, specialized disassembly, or climate sensitivity. Attempting DIY moves often results in property damage, personal injury, or destroyed belongings. Professional movers carry the right insurance, equipment, and experience to handle these challenges safely.
