## Key Tips for Military Moving in May

### Plan Ahead for Peak Times

May can be a busy time for moving companies, especially near military bases like Homestead Air Reserve Base. With Mother's Day activities and seasonal transitions, scheduling your military moving early ensures you get your preferred date and time.

### Know Your Miami Area Options

If you're PCSing to South Florida, popular neighborhoods for military families include Kendall for its affordability, Doral for quick access to major highways, and Palmetto Bay for excellent schools. Those stationed at Homestead often choose nearby Florida City or Cutler Bay for shorter commutes.

### Keep all documentation organized for reimbursement purposes

This single step can save hours of stress and potential problems on moving day. Our experienced crews have seen what happens when this step is skipped.

### Consider the Weather

South Florida's spring weather is predictable in some ways and surprising in others. May brings the start of rainy season, with afternoon thunderstorms rolling in from the Everglades almost daily by 3pm. Professional movers know how to protect your belongings from humidity, rain, and heat exposure during transport, and schedule morning loads to avoid weather delays.

### Prepare Your Belongings

Take time to inventory your items before the move. This is especially important for military moving, as proper documentation helps ensure everything arrives safely at your new location.
