Welcome to your comprehensive guide for moving to Bay Harbor Islands! This exclusive two-island community offers small-town charm, luxury living, and easy access to Miami's best amenities.

## About Bay Harbor Islands

Bay Harbor Islands is a prestigious town comprising two islands - East Island and West Island - nestled along Biscayne Bay in northeastern Miami-Dade County. With approximately 6,000 residents, this intimate community offers a relaxed lifestyle in the heart of South Florida's most desirable area.

![Bay Harbor Islands Waterfront Checklist](/images/blog/2027/03/bay-harbor-islands-neighborhood-guide-for-new-residents/bay-harbor-islands-waterfront-checklist.webp)


### Demographics and Community

- **Population**: Approximately 6,048 (2025 estimate)
- **Median Age**: 36 years
- **Diversity**: Hispanic (52.4%), White Non-Hispanic (41%)
- **Median Household Income**: $89,212
- **Foreign-Born Residents**: 48.7%
- **Housing**: 51% owner-occupied, 49% renter-occupied

## Neighborhoods and Island Character

### West Island
Exclusively single-family residences on quiet, tree-lined streets. Many homes feature private docks and direct bay access.

### East Island
A mix of multifamily properties, commercial areas, and the community's K-8 school. New luxury developments like La Baia and Onda Residences are transforming the waterfront.

## Schools and Education

### Public Schools
- **Ruth K. Broad/Bay Harbor K-8 Center**: "A" rated school on the East Island
- **Miami Beach Nautilus Middle School**: Serving older students
- **Miami Beach Senior High School**: The designated high school

![Bay Harbor Islands Waterfront Essentials](/images/blog/2027/03/bay-harbor-islands-neighborhood-guide-for-new-residents/bay-harbor-islands-waterfront-essentials.webp)


### Private Schools Nearby
Riviera Preparatory School, Blessed Trinity Catholic School, St. Lawrence Elementary School, Holy Family Catholic School

## Housing Market

### Home Prices (2025)
- **Median Listing Price**: $897,000-$958,000
- **Single-Family Homes**: $2,000,000 to $34,500,000
- **Condos**: $290,000 to $13,850,000
- **Waterfront Residences**: Starting around $3 million
- **Median Rent**: $2,348-$2,416/month

## Location and Accessibility

- **Bal Harbour Shops**: Ultra-luxury shopping (walking distance)
- **Surfside**: Charming beach community adjacent
- **Aventura Mall**: Major shopping destination nearby
- **Miami International Airport**: 25-30 minutes
- **Fort Lauderdale Airport**: 35-40 minutes

## Lifestyle and Recreation

- Direct access to Biscayne Bay and Intracoastal Waterway
- Year-round boating, kayaking, and paddleboarding
- Many homes feature private docks
- Walking distance to Bal Harbour's world-class dining
