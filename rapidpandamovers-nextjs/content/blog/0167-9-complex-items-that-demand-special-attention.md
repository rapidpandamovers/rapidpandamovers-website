Some belongings require extra care, specialized equipment, and professional expertise during a move. These complex items demand special attention during your May relocation.

## 1. Grand Pianos

Weighing 500-1200 pounds with delicate internal components, grand pianos require specialized dollies, ramps, and experienced movers to prevent damage.

![Unusual Special Care Unique Guide](/images/blog/2025/05/9-complex-items-that-demand-special-attention/unusual-special-care-unique-guide.webp)


## 2. Antique Furniture

Irreplaceable heirlooms need custom crating, climate control, and white-glove handling. Refinishing after damage can cost thousands.

## 3. Gun Safes

These 500-2000+ pound steel boxes require specialized equipment and knowledge of floor load limits at both locations.

## 4. Hot Tubs and Spas

Draining, disconnecting electrical and plumbing, and safely transporting these 400+ pound items requires professional handling.

## 5. Large Aquariums

Fish need separate transport; tanks must be drained, carefully padded, and kept level. Re-establishing the ecosystem takes weeks.

## 6. Wine Collections

Temperature-controlled transport, proper bottle positioning, and custom crating protect valuable collections from heat and vibration damage.

![Unusual Special Care Unique Overview](/images/blog/2025/05/9-complex-items-that-demand-special-attention/unusual-special-care-unique-overview.webp)


## 7. Pool Tables

Slate tops require professional disassembly. Improperly moved pool tables lose their level and never play correctly again.

## 8. Medical Equipment

Hospital beds, mobility devices, and breathing equipment need careful handling and often require same-day setup at the new location.

## 9. Fine Art and Sculptures

Custom crating, climate control, and insurance documentation protect artwork worth more than most household contents combined.
