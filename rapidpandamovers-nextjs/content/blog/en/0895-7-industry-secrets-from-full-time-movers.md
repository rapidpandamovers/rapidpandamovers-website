After thousands of moves across Miami-Dade County, our professional movers have learned what works and what causes problems. From Brickell high-rises to Coral Gables estates, here are the insider tips we wish every customer knew before moving day.

## 1. Packing Takes Longer Than You Think

A 3-bedroom house takes 3-5 days to pack properly. Not one frantic night. Start early and pace yourself. In Miami's humidity, you'll also want climate control running while you pack. Items left in a hot garage overnight can warp or melt.

## 2. Small Boxes for Heavy Items

Books, dishes, and tools go in small boxes. Large boxes are for light items like bedding and clothes. This is basic physics: a large box full of books weighs 70+ pounds and the bottom will give out. We see this mistake constantly, especially when Miami residents pack their own libraries.

## 3. Labeling Saves Everyone Time

"Kitchen" is not enough detail. Write "Kitchen - Coffee Station - Fragile" on the box AND the side so stacked boxes remain identifiable. This saves hours at your new place. In condo moves around Wynwood or Aventura, where elevator time is limited, good labeling means we can prioritize what goes up first.

## 4. Fragile Items Need Communication

If something is exceptionally fragile or valuable, tell movers before they touch it. That antique mirror from your grandmother? The signed artwork? Point it out. We handle items with care, but we cannot identify family heirlooms by sight alone.

## 5. Access Matters More Than You Think

Clear pathways, reserved elevators, and available parking make moves faster. Every obstacle adds time and cost. This is especially true for Miami high-rise moves in Brickell or Downtown. Reserve the freight elevator at both buildings, confirm move-in windows, and get parking permits sorted in advance.

## 6. Disassembled Furniture Helps

Beds, tables, and desks move faster when disassembled. Keep all hardware in labeled bags taped directly to the furniture piece. Taking apart that IKEA bed before we arrive can save 30 minutes of labor. Just keep the Allen keys handy for reassembly.

## 7. Have the New Layout Ready

Know where furniture goes before we arrive at the new place. Walk through your new home in Kendall or Coconut Grove beforehand and decide where the couch, bed, and dining table belong. Uncertainty leads to multiple rearrangements, which means more time and more chances for scuffs.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How far in advance should I start packing?

For a 3-bedroom home, start at least 2 weeks before your move date. Begin with rooms you use least, like guest bedrooms or storage areas. Save everyday items like kitchen essentials for the final days.

### Should I empty dresser drawers before moving?

It depends on the dresser. Lightweight dressers with sturdy drawers can stay loaded with soft items like clothes. Heavy wooden dressers should be emptied to prevent drawer damage and make lifting safer.

### Do movers pack items in closets?

Not unless you request full packing services. Standard moving covers furniture and boxes you have prepared. If you want closets packed, ask about our [packing services](/packing-services) when you get your quote.
