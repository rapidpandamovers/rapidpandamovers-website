Some household items require specialized expertise, equipment, and handling techniques that go beyond standard moving procedures. Here are 7 complex items that demand special attention during a move.

## 1. Grand and Baby Grand Pianos

Pianos are among the most challenging items to move. Their weight (400-1,200+ pounds), delicate internal mechanisms, and awkward shape require specialized equipment like piano boards, skids, and straps. The keys, hammers, and strings are sensitive to temperature and humidity changes.

## 2. Large Aquariums

Moving an aquarium means more than transporting glass. Fish must travel separately in temperature-controlled containers. The tank requires complete draining, careful padding, and level transport to prevent stress cracks. Substrate, decorations, and filtration systems need separate handling.

## 3. Pool Tables

Slate-top pool tables must be disassembled by specialists. The felt can't be folded, the slate sections are extremely heavy and fragile, and improper reassembly means the table will never play true again. This is not a DIY item.

## 4. Wine Collections

Fine wine is temperature-sensitive and vibration-intolerant. Large collections require climate-controlled transport, individual bottle protection, and proper positioning. The value at stake often justifies specialized wine moving services.

![Detailed View Of Piano Internal Mechanis 4](/images/blog/2026/06/7-complex-items-that-demand-special-attention/detailed-view-of-piano-internal-mechanis-4.webp)


## 5. Hot Tubs and Spas

Hot tubs must be drained, disconnected from electrical systems, and transported upright. Their bulk and weight require equipment like appliance dollies and potentially a crane for deck removal and installation.

## 6. Grandfather Clocks

These precision instruments have pendulums, weights, and delicate mechanisms that must be properly secured or removed. The tall, narrow profile makes them prone to tipping. Professional clock movers understand how to protect these heirlooms.

## 7. Large Safes

Gun safes and vault safes can weigh 500-2,500+ pounds. They require specialized dollies, stair-climbing equipment, and often multiple movers. Floor protection and structural considerations at the destination are essential.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does specialty item moving cost in Miami?

Specialty item moves typically range from $200-$800 per item depending on size, weight, and complexity. Piano moves average $350-$600, while large aquariums or hot tubs can run $500-$1,000+ due to equipment needs.

### Do I need to prepare my piano before moving?

Yes. Lock the keyboard lid, secure the music stand, and avoid tuning for at least two weeks after the move. Miami's humidity means your piano will need time to acclimate.

### Can you move a full aquarium with fish?

We transport the tank and equipment, but fish travel separately in insulated, temperature-controlled bags. We recommend finding a local aquarium shop that can board fish overnight for longer moves.
