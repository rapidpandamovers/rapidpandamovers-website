## Why Choose Homestead?

Homestead sits at the southern tip of Miami-Dade County, where suburban convenience meets rural Florida charm. The city offers something rare in South Florida: space. Half-acre lots are common here, and you can still find single-family homes under $400K, a price point that disappeared from Kendall years ago.

![Loc Beautiful White Houses With A Tropical S 2](/images/blog/2027/11/moving-to-homestead-what-you-need-to-know/loc-beautiful-white-houses-with-a-tropical-s-2.webp)


The Everglades National Park sits at your doorstep, just 10 minutes from most Homestead neighborhoods. Biscayne National Park is equally close. Families move here for the space, the affordability, and the slower pace of life that urban Miami cannot offer.

### Location and Accessibility

Homestead connects to the rest of Miami-Dade via US-1 and the Florida Turnpike Extension. Downtown Miami is about 35 miles north, roughly a 45-minute drive outside rush hour. The Homestead Station offers Metrorail bus connections for those who prefer public transit.

Homestead-Miami Speedway brings NASCAR and IndyCar events, drawing visitors from across the state. The Homestead Air Reserve Base provides military employment, and the agricultural industry offers seasonal work throughout the Redland farming district.

### Community and Lifestyle

Homestead maintains a small-town feel despite its growth. The historic downtown district along Krome Avenue features local shops, restaurants like the Royal Palm Grill, and community events year-round. The Homestead Rodeo, running since 1948, happens every January. The Redland Tropical Trail hosts farm tours and fruit festivals throughout the year.

## Neighborhoods to Consider

Homestead breaks into distinct areas, each with its own character:

- **Downtown Homestead**: Walking distance to shops and restaurants along Krome Avenue. Older homes with character, some needing renovation. Most affordable area, with homes starting around $300K.

- **The Redland**: Agricultural district north of downtown. Large lots (1-5 acres common), tropical fruit groves, and genuine farm living. Privacy and space, but limited walkability. Homes range from $450K to over $1M depending on acreage.

- **Waterstone/Keys Gate**: Master-planned communities on the east side near US-1. Newer construction, HOA-maintained amenities, community pools and clubhouses. Family-oriented with homes typically $400K-$600K.

- **Leisure City**: Unincorporated area between Homestead and Florida City. Budget-friendly options, diverse community, but fewer amenities. Consider proximity to schools carefully.

### Choosing Your Perfect Spot

Your daily commute matters most. If you work in Kendall or points north, consider neighborhoods closer to the Turnpike entrance. Working locally or remotely? The Redland offers space you will not find elsewhere in Miami-Dade. Families should check school zones, as Homestead Senior High and South Dade Senior High serve different neighborhoods.

## Moving to Homestead: Timing Matters

November through April offers the best moving weather. Summer months bring afternoon thunderstorms almost daily, and humidity makes physical work exhausting. Hurricane season runs June through November, so avoid scheduling moves during storm forecasts.

### Timing Your Move

Plan around these Homestead-specific factors:

- **Best months**: November through March. Dry season, mild temperatures, and manageable humidity.
- **Avoid if possible**: August and September (peak hurricane season) and NASCAR race weekends when traffic on Speedway Boulevard backs up for miles.
- **Best days**: Tuesday through Thursday for better mover availability and rates.
- **Morning moves**: Schedule loading before noon. Afternoon storms pop up quickly May through October.

## Essential Services to Locate

As a new Homestead resident, you will want these addresses saved:

- **Healthcare**: Homestead Hospital (975 Baptist Way) handles emergencies. Baptist Health and Cleveland Clinic have satellite offices in the area for specialty care.
- **Schools**: Miami-Dade Public Schools serves the area. Homestead Senior High, South Dade Senior High, and several charter options including Keys Gate Charter High School.
- **Shopping**: The Homestead Pavilion and Southland Mall cover basics. Publix and Sedano's have multiple locations. For bigger shopping trips, The Falls in Kendall is 20 minutes north.
- **Recreation**: Harris Field Park and Homestead Bayfront Park offer sports facilities. Fruit & Spice Park showcases tropical agriculture. Everglades Alligator Farm provides family entertainment.
