## Key Tips for Last Minute Moving in September

### Plan Ahead for Peak Times

September can be a busy time for moving companies. With football season activities and seasonal transitions, scheduling your last minute moving early ensures you get your preferred date and time.

### Prioritize must-move items and accept help with packing to save time

This single step can save hours of stress and potential problems on moving day. Our experienced crews have seen what happens when this step is skipped.

### Consider the Weather

South Florida's fall weather is predictable in some ways and surprising in others. September in Miami means hurricane season is still active, with afternoon thunderstorms common from Kendall to North Miami Beach. Professional movers know how to protect your belongings from humidity, rain, and heat exposure during transport.

### Prepare Your Belongings

Take time to inventory your items before the move. This is especially important for last minute moving, as proper documentation helps ensure everything arrives safely at your new location.
