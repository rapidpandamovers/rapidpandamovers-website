You found it: the one Miami-Dade community where paparazzi get turned away at the gate. Golden Beach sits between Sunny Isles Beach and Hallandale, home to just 900 residents across 357 single-family homes. No condos, no high-rises, no commercial buildings. Just private oceanfront estates behind a 24-hour police patrol.

Six months from now, you could be walking your dog on a private beach without dodging tourists or fighting for parking. Here's what our crew has learned from moving families into this exclusive enclave.

## Why Golden Beach Stands Apart

Golden Beach is not just another Miami Beach neighborhood. This is a self-governing town of 0.85 square miles where homes average $10 million and privacy is the primary amenity. The town employs its own police force (one of the highest officer-to-resident ratios in Florida), and the single access point at Collins Avenue and 195th Street means no through traffic.

![Loc Serene Beach Scene At Sunset With Calm W 1](/images/blog/2029/07/golden-beach-moving-tips-local-insights-for-relocators/loc-serene-beach-scene-at-sunset-with-calm-w-1.webp)


The community attracts executives, celebrities, and families seeking the ultimate in South Florida privacy. Your neighbors might include international business figures and entertainment industry professionals who chose Golden Beach specifically because it offers what nearby Bal Harbour, Aventura, and Miami Beach cannot: genuine seclusion.

### Location and Getting Around

Golden Beach sits on the barrier island between the Atlantic Ocean and the Intracoastal Waterway. Collins Avenue (A1A) runs through town, connecting you to:

- **Aventura Mall** (10 minutes south) for shopping and dining
- **Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport** (20 minutes north)
- **Miami International Airport** (40 minutes south)
- **Brickell and Downtown Miami** (30 minutes via I-95)
- **Bal Harbour Shops** (5 minutes south) for luxury retail

Most residents drive, though some boat directly to waterfront restaurants in North Miami Beach or dock at Haulover Marina.

### Community Character

With only 357 homes, everyone knows everyone. The Town Hall hosts community events, and the private beach becomes the social hub for families. Unlike the bustle of nearby Sunny Isles Beach high-rises, Golden Beach maintains a residential, almost suburban feel despite its oceanfront location.

## Understanding Golden Beach Properties

Unlike Coconut Grove or Coral Gables where you might choose between neighborhoods, Golden Beach is small enough that location differences are minimal. The real decisions involve:

### Oceanfront vs. Intracoastal

- **Oceanfront homes** (east side of Collins): Direct beach access, sunrise views, typically $15M+. You'll hear the waves and walk to the sand in seconds.
- **Intracoastal homes** (west side): Deeper lots, boat docks, sunset views over the water, typically $8-12M. Better for families with boats or those who prefer calmer water for paddleboarding.

### What You'll Find

Every home in Golden Beach is single-family. No condos, no apartments, no commercial properties. Most properties are:

- **Lot sizes**: 10,000 to 40,000+ square feet
- **Home sizes**: 4,000 to 15,000+ square feet
- **Style**: Modern new construction dominates, though some mid-century estates remain
- **Features**: Most include pools, many have direct water access (ocean or Intracoastal)

## Moving to Golden Beach in July

July moves require planning around South Florida's summer weather. Temperatures hit 90°F by 10am, humidity hovers near 80%, and afternoon thunderstorms roll in like clockwork between 3-5pm. Our crews schedule Golden Beach moves for early morning starts, typically wrapping loading before the storms arrive.

### Timing Your Move

When planning your relocation, consider:

- **Best moving days**: Weekdays work best since Golden Beach only allows moving trucks Monday through Saturday
- **Morning starts**: Schedule an 8am arrival to beat the afternoon storms and heat
- **Town coordination**: Contact Golden Beach Town Hall at least 48 hours ahead; they track all moving vehicles entering town

## Essential Services Near Golden Beach

As a new Golden Beach resident, you'll find most services in nearby Aventura and Sunny Isles Beach:

- **Healthcare**: Aventura Hospital (15 minutes), Mount Sinai Medical Center in Miami Beach (25 minutes), and Memorial Regional Hospital in Hollywood (20 minutes)
- **Schools**: Ojus Elementary, Aventura Waterways K-8 Center, or private options like Cushman School in Miami and Pine Crest in Fort Lauderdale
- **Shopping**: Aventura Mall (10 minutes) for major retail, Publix at Ojus or Whole Foods in Aventura for groceries
- **Dining**: Bal Harbour Shops restaurants (5 minutes), Gulfstream Park in Hallandale (15 minutes), or waterfront spots in North Miami Beach
