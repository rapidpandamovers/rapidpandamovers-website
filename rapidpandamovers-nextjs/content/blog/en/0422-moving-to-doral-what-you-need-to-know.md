Thinking about moving to Doral? You're considering one of Miami-Dade's fastest-growing and most family-friendly cities. This guide provides essential information about Doral to help you plan your relocation.

## About Doral

Doral is a thriving city of approximately 83,600 residents, known for its excellent schools, safe neighborhoods, and strong business community. The city has experienced remarkable growth, with population increasing over 300% since 2000, making it one of the fastest-growing cities in Florida.

![A Joyful Child With Socks On Feet Playin 2](/images/blog/2026/03/moving-to-doral-what-you-need-to-know/a-joyful-child-with-socks-on-feet-playin-2.webp)


### Demographics at a Glance

- **Population**: 83,625 (2024)
- **Median Age**: 37.2 years
- **Median Household Income**: $88,474
- **Population Density**: 6,353 people per square mile
- **Hispanic Population**: 85.3% (primarily Venezuelan, Colombian, and Cuban)
- **Foreign-Born Residents**: 66.5%

### Languages
Doral is a truly bilingual community where Spanish is widely spoken alongside English. Many businesses and services operate fluently in both languages.

## Real Estate Market

Doral offers diverse housing options from condos to luxury estates.

### Current Market (2024-2025)
- **Median Sale Price**: $588,000
- **Median Price per Square Foot**: $366
- **Average Days on Market**: 102 days
- **Market Trend**: Up 5.9% year-over-year

### Housing Types
- Gated single-family home communities
- Luxury condominiums
- Townhouse developments
- Golf course properties around Trump National Doral

### Buyer Demographics
- 81% are primary residence buyers
- 19% are investors
- 61% use conventional financing
- 35% purchase with cash
- Strong international buyer interest from Latin America

## Schools and Education

Doral is renowned for its excellent schools, a major draw for families.

### Top-Rated Schools
- **School For Advanced Studies**: 10/10 GreatSchools rating
- **Divine Savior Lutheran Academy**: A+ Niche rating
- **Doral Academy Charter Middle School**: Ranked #28 of 1,205 Florida middle schools
- **Ronald W. Reagan/Doral Senior High School**: Highly rated public high school

### Academic Performance
- 82-94% of students proficient in various assessments
- 89.7% four-year graduation rate
- Average test scores of 76% proficiency
- Multiple International Baccalaureate and Advanced Placement programs

### School Options
Doral offers 34 school options including public, charter, and private institutions.

## Best Neighborhoods for Families

### Downtown Doral
A vibrant mixed-use area with residential towers, shopping, dining, and the Downtown Doral Charter Upper School. Perfect for those who want walkability.

### Doral Isles
A gated community known for security and family-friendly atmosphere. Features lakes, parks, and a strong sense of community.

### The Reserve at Doral
Spacious townhomes with clubhouse and community pool. Close proximity to Ronald W. Reagan/Doral Senior High School.

### Grand Bay Estates
Upscale neighborhood with large homes and luxurious amenities for families seeking more space and privacy.

## Parks and Recreation

## Moving Tips for Doral

### Gated Community Access
Many Doral neighborhoods are gated. Coordinate with HOA management for:
- Moving truck access codes
- Designated moving hours
- Elevator reservations (for condos)

### Best Moving Times
- **Early mornings** before school traffic
- **Weekdays** when business traffic is lighter
- **Avoid rush hours** on major expressways

### HOA Considerations
Most Doral communities have HOA rules regarding:
- Moving hours (typically 8 AM - 6 PM)
- Truck parking locations
- Damage deposits
