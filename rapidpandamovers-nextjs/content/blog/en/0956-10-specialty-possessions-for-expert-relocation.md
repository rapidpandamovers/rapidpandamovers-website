Whether you're a long-time Miami resident or just making your June move to South Florida, there's always something new to discover. Here's our curated list to help you make the most of life in the Sunshine State this summer.

## 1. Grand Pianos

These massive instruments require specialized equipment, multiple people, and expertise to avoid damage to the piano and property. Many Coral Gables and Pinecrest homes feature dedicated music rooms, and moving a grand piano through Miami's humid climate requires extra precautions to protect the soundboard and finish.

![A Standout Red Tipped Match Among Burnt  3](/images/blog/2030/06/10-specialty-possessions-for-expert-relocation/a-standout-red-tipped-match-among-burnt--3.webp)


## 2. Wine Collections

Temperature sensitivity, fragile bottles, and proper positioning require climate-controlled handling and custom crating. South Florida's heat makes this especially critical. When moving wine cellars from Coconut Grove or Brickell high-rises, climate-controlled trucks are essential to prevent spoilage.

## 3. Hot Tubs and Spas

Draining, disconnecting, and moving these heavy items requires professionals familiar with the process. Miami Beach and Aventura homeowners often have rooftop or patio spas that require crane service or specialized rigging to relocate.

## 4. Large Aquariums

Fish need separate transport; tanks must be drained, carefully padded, and moved level to prevent cracking.

## 5. Pool Tables

Slate tops require disassembly by specialists. Improperly moved pool tables never play the same.

## 6. Fine Art and Sculptures

Custom crating, climate control, and white-glove handling protect valuable artwork during transit. The Wynwood arts district and Design District attract serious collectors, and we've moved everything from gallery installations to private collections throughout Miami-Dade.

## 7. Full Gym Equipment

Treadmills, weight machines, and home gyms are heavy and awkward. Professional movers have the equipment to handle them.

## 8. Classic Cars

Valuable vehicles need enclosed transport and special handling. Regular moving trucks aren't appropriate. From vintage convertibles in Doral to collector Ferraris in Golden Beach, South Florida's car culture means we handle classic and exotic vehicles regularly.

## 9. Antique Furniture

Heirloom pieces require special padding, climate control, and careful handling to prevent damage to delicate finishes and joints. Kendall and Homestead estate sales often feature antique pieces that need expert care during transport to their new homes.

## 10. Medical Equipment

Hospital beds, oxygen concentrators, and mobility devices need proper disassembly, transport, and setup by trained professionals.
