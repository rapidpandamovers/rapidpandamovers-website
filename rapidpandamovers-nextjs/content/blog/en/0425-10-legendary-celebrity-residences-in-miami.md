Miami attracts the rich and famous, and their homes are as impressive as you'd expect. From Star Island estates to historic South Beach mansions, here are 10 legendary celebrity residences that have made Miami famous.

## 1. Gloria Estefan's Nena's Villa - 1 Star Island Drive

The Queen of Latin Pop and her husband Emilio purchased this waterfront compound at 1 Star Island Drive, naming it Nena's Villa. The estate spans multiple acres and features a private dock, tennis court, and stunning views of Biscayne Bay. Gloria has been a Star Island resident since the 1990s and remains one of the island's most beloved long-term residents.

## 2. Gianni Versace's Casa Casuarina - 1116 Ocean Drive, Miami Beach

Perhaps the most famous celebrity residence in Miami, this Mediterranean-style mansion was built in 1930 and purchased by fashion designer Gianni Versace in 1992. Versace spent $33 million renovating the 10-bedroom property, adding a 54-foot mosaic pool lined with 24-karat gold. Now operating as The Villa Casa Casuarina boutique hotel, the property remains an architectural landmark on Ocean Drive.

## 3. Sean "Diddy" Combs Estate - 39 Star Island Drive

The music mogul purchased his Star Island estate in 2021 for a reported $35 million. The 1.3-acre waterfront property features 8 bedrooms, 11 bathrooms, and 109 feet of water frontage. Previously owned by Gloria Estefan, the estate includes a private dock, pool house, and expansive outdoor entertaining areas with direct bay access.

## 4. Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck's Former Home - Star Island

J.Lo and then-fiance Alex Rodriguez purchased a waterfront Star Island mansion for approximately $40 million. The classic Florida-style estate features sweeping views of Biscayne Bay, a dock for boats, and the privacy that makes Star Island so desirable for A-list celebrities. The property exemplified the island's appeal to Hollywood royalty.

## 5. Phil Collins' Historic Mansion - 5800 North Bay Road, Miami Beach

The legendary musician purchased this 10,500-square-foot mansion on North Bay Road for $33 million. Originally built in 1929 and previously owned by Jennifer Lopez, the property features 7 bedrooms, a home theater, and 165 feet of waterfront. Collins reportedly spent millions on renovations to restore the historic estate to its original grandeur.

## 6. Vladislav Doronin's Star Island Estate

Russian billionaire and real estate mogul Vladislav Doronin owns one of the most striking modern estates on Star Island. The property features contemporary architecture, tennis courts, fountains, and landscaping that sets it apart from the island's more traditional mansions. The estate reportedly cost over $50 million.

## 7. Lisa Hochstein's Modern Mansion - Star Island

The Real Housewives of Miami star and her husband Dr. Leonard Hochstein (known as the "Boob God" of Miami plastic surgery) made headlines when they demolished their original Star Island villa to build a massive contemporary mansion. The new estate became famous on the reality show and represents the modern wave of Star Island development.

## 8. Ken Griffin's Record-Breaking Purchase - Star Island

Billionaire hedge fund manager Ken Griffin made waves when he purchased the most expensive home ever sold in Miami. His Star Island compound, assembled from multiple properties, represents over $100 million in real estate. Griffin has been acquiring adjacent properties to create one of the island's largest private estates.

## 9. Philip Frost's Pharmaceutical Palace - 21 Star Island Drive

Billionaire pharmaceutical magnate Philip Frost owns the estate at 21 Star Island Drive. Frost made his fortune in the healthcare industry and his Star Island property reflects his success with expansive grounds and waterfront access. The Frost family has been prominent Miami residents and philanthropists for decades.

## 10. Rick Ross' Former Mansion - 3 Star Island Drive

Rapper and entrepreneur Rick Ross purchased a Star Island estate that perfectly matched his larger-than-life persona. The property featured multiple pools, a recording studio, and enough space for his car collection. Though he later sold the property, it remains one of the most recognizable celebrity homes from its appearances in music videos and social media.
