Indian Creek Island is not a typical Miami neighborhood. With only 41 homes, a private police force, and its own golf course, this 300-acre island in Biscayne Bay is one of the most exclusive residential enclaves in America. If you are moving here, congratulations. Here is what to expect.

## About Indian Creek Island

Known as "Billionaire's Bunker," Indian Creek has housed some of the world's wealthiest residents including Jared Kushner and Ivanka Trump, Tom Brady and Gisele Bundchen, Carl Icahn, and Julio Iglesias. The island sits between Miami Beach and the mainland, accessible only by a single bridge with 24/7 security.

### By the Numbers
- **Population**: Approximately 86 full-time residents
- **Homes**: 41 single-family estates
- **Median Home Price**: $30 million to $75 million
- **Property Sizes**: 1 to 4+ acres each
- **Private Police**: 13 officers for 41 homes

## Security and Access

### Entry Protocols
Moving to Indian Creek means working with security from day one. The guard gate requires advance notice for all service providers, including moving trucks. Expect to provide:

- Driver identification for all crew members
- Vehicle registration and license plates
- Detailed schedule of arrival and departure times
- Insurance documentation from your moving company

### Coordinating with Island Security
Contact the Indian Creek Village office at least 2 weeks before your move. They will issue temporary access passes and coordinate with security for truck entry. Large moving trucks may need to arrive during specific hours to avoid disrupting the single access road.

## Moving Logistics Unique to Indian Creek

### Bridge and Road Considerations
The Indian Creek Bridge connects the island to Miami Beach. Wide-load trucks need prior approval. Standard 26-foot moving trucks typically pass without issue, but 53-foot semi-trailers require special coordination.

![Elegant Waterfront Mansion In Fort Laude 1](/images/blog/2028/12/moving-to-indian-creek-island-ultimate-guide/elegant-waterfront-mansion-in-fort-laude-1.webp)


### Timing Your Move
Early morning arrivals work best. The single access road means any delays affect the entire island. Most residents prefer moves to begin at 7 AM before the golf course becomes active.

### Estate Move Complexity
Indian Creek homes average 15,000 to 30,000 square feet. Moves of this scale require:
- Multiple days of loading and unloading
- Specialized equipment for artwork, wine cellars, and safes
- Climate-controlled transport for sensitive items
- White glove service for antiques and high-value furniture

## Nearby Amenities

Indian Creek Island is exclusively residential. All shopping, dining, and services require leaving the island.

### Minutes Away
- **Bal Harbour Shops**: Ultra-luxury shopping, 5 minutes
- **Aventura Mall**: General shopping, 10 minutes
- **Mount Sinai Medical Center**: Miami Beach, 15 minutes
- **Private Schools**: Ransom Everglades (25 min), Miami Country Day (20 min)

### Airports
- **Miami International (MIA)**: 25 minutes
- **Fort Lauderdale (FLL)**: 35 minutes
- **Opa-locka Executive Airport**: Private aviation, 15 minutes

## The Indian Creek Country Club

The 18-hole golf course covers a third of the island. Membership is automatic with property ownership but comes with annual dues exceeding $150,000. The club includes tennis courts, a marina, and dining facilities. Many residents host private events at the clubhouse.
