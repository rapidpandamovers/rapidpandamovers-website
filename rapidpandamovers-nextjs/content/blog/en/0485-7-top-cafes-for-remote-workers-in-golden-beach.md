Living near Golden Beach means access to some of Miami's best coffee spots, perfect for getting work done. This exclusive barrier island community sits between Sunny Isles Beach and Hallandale Beach, offering a quiet residential vibe with easy access to great cafes just minutes away on Collins Avenue. Whether you're looking for strong WiFi, great espresso, or a quiet atmosphere to escape the South Florida heat, here are the top cafes for remote workers in and around Golden Beach.

## 1. Wolsen Caffe

**Address:** 17100 Collins Ave, Ste 105, Sunny Isles Beach, FL 33160

![A Thoughtful Woman Using A Laptop At An  1](/images/blog/2027/06/7-top-cafes-for-remote-workers-in-golden-beach/a-thoughtful-woman-using-a-laptop-at-an--1.webp)


Open daily 9:30am-9pm, Wolsen Caffe offers fresh, healthy cuisine alongside excellent coffee and tea. A cozy atmosphere with high-speed WiFi makes it perfect for remote work. The AC is strong here, which you'll appreciate during Miami's humid summer months.

## 2. The Coffee Store

**Address:** 18060 Collins Ave, Sunny Isles Beach, FL

An Argentinian bakery established in 1998 offering high-quality coffee, medialunas, empanadas, and pastries. European-style cafe perfect for breakfast, brunch, or a working lunch. The Latin American influence is strong here, fitting right into Miami's diverse food scene.

## 3. Filomena's Bean Coffee

**Address:** Sunny Isles Beach, FL

A perfect day-to-day go-to spot with good music, excellent energy, and delicious drinks. Designed as a place to work, meet friends, and enjoy great coffee. Popular with the local beach crowd heading in after morning walks along the Sunny Isles coastline.

## 4. Naughty Coffee

**Address:** Collins Ave, Sunny Isles Beach, FL

One of the top-rated coffee spots in the area, known for creative drinks and a trendy atmosphere. Great for a change of scenery from home office.

## 5. Neverland Coffee Bar

**Address:** Sunny Isles Beach, FL

![Positive African American Female In Casu 2](/images/blog/2027/06/7-top-cafes-for-remote-workers-in-golden-beach/positive-african-american-female-in-casu-2.webp)


A specialty coffee shop with a whimsical atmosphere. Features quality espresso drinks and light bites in a comfortable setting for working remotely.

## 6. Ralph's Coffee

**Address:** Sunny Isles Beach, FL

Elegant coffee shop offering premium brews in a sophisticated setting. Perfect for important video calls or focused work sessions.

## 7. Granier Bakery

**Address:** Sunny Isles Beach, FL

A European-style bakery with excellent coffee and fresh pastries. Offers a quiet atmosphere ideal for morning work sessions.

## Tips for Working Remotely in Miami

A few things to keep in mind when working from cafes in the Golden Beach area:

- **Arrive early** - Most spots fill up by mid-morning, especially on weekends
- **Check hurricane season schedules** - Some cafes adjust hours during storms (June through November)
- **Bring a light jacket** - Miami AC can be cold when you're sitting for hours
- **Parking** - Most spots along Collins Ave have metered parking or nearby garages
