Moving specialty items requires more than just a truck and some muscle. From luxury condos in Brickell to waterfront homes in Coral Gables, Miami residents often own unique possessions that need expert handling. Here are 8 specialty items that call for professional movers.

## 1. Grand Pianos

These massive instruments require specialized equipment, multiple people, and expertise to avoid damage to the piano and property.

![Close Up Of A Gift Wrapped Hamburger Hel 1](/images/blog/2029/02/8-specialty-possessions-for-expert-relocation/close-up-of-a-gift-wrapped-hamburger-hel-1.webp)


## 2. Wine Collections

Temperature sensitivity, fragile bottles, and proper positioning require climate-controlled handling and custom crating. Miami's heat makes this especially important - wines can spoil quickly in South Florida temperatures without proper protection.

## 3. Hot Tubs and Spas

Draining, disconnecting, and moving these heavy items requires professionals familiar with the process. Popular in Kendall and Doral homes with outdoor living spaces, hot tubs need careful handling to avoid damage.

## 4. Large Aquariums

Fish need separate transport; tanks must be drained, carefully padded, and moved level to prevent cracking. Saltwater aquariums are common in Aventura and Coconut Grove waterfront properties.

![Funny Outdoor Haircut Session By The Lak 4](/images/blog/2029/02/8-specialty-possessions-for-expert-relocation/funny-outdoor-haircut-session-by-the-lak-4.webp)


## 5. Pool Tables

Slate tops require disassembly by specialists. Improperly moved pool tables never play the same.

## 6. Fine Art and Sculptures

Custom crating, climate control, and white-glove handling protect valuable artwork during transit. Wynwood collectors and gallery owners know the importance of proper art transportation.

## 7. Full Gym Equipment

Treadmills, weight machines, and home gyms are heavy and awkward. Professional movers have the equipment to handle them.

![Unemotional Ethnic Female With Bare Shou 2](/images/blog/2029/02/8-specialty-possessions-for-expert-relocation/unemotional-ethnic-female-with-bare-shou-2.webp)


## 8. Classic Cars

Valuable vehicles need enclosed transport and special handling. Regular moving trucks aren't appropriate.
