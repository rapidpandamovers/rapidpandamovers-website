Before your move, know what professional movers cannot legally or safely transport. These items require special handling or must stay with you personally.

## 1. Hazardous Materials

Gasoline, propane, paint thinner, aerosols, and chemicals are fire hazards and illegal to transport on moving trucks. In South Florida's heat, these items become even more dangerous. Visit your local Miami-Dade Household Hazardous Waste facility to dispose of them safely before your move.

![A Blue Volvo Truck Parked Outside A Ware 1](/images/blog/2027/12/7-things-movers-arent-allowed-to-transport/a-blue-volvo-truck-parked-outside-a-ware-1.webp)


## 2. Explosives and Ammunition

Fireworks, ammunition, and anything explosive must be disposed of or transported personally with proper safety measures.

## 3. Perishable Foods

Refrigerated and frozen items won't survive most moves, especially in Miami's tropical climate where truck temperatures can spike quickly. Consume, donate to Feeding South Florida, or dispose of perishables before moving day.

![A Classic Hong Kong Tram Traverses Moder 3](/images/blog/2027/12/7-things-movers-arent-allowed-to-transport/a-classic-hong-kong-tram-traverses-moder-3.webp)


## 4. Plants (for Long-Distance Moves)

Agricultural regulations often prohibit transporting plants across state lines due to pest concerns. Florida has strict inspection requirements to prevent citrus diseases and invasive species from spreading. For local moves within Miami-Dade, Broward, or Palm Beach counties, plants can typically come along.

## 5. Pets and Living Creatures

Fish, pets, and any living animals cannot travel on moving trucks. In Miami's heat, an enclosed truck is dangerous for animals. Arrange separate, air-conditioned transportation for your pets, and consider boarding them at a local facility in Coral Gables or Kendall on moving day to reduce their stress.

## 6. Irreplaceable Documents

Passports, birth certificates, financial records, and legal documents should stay with you personally during the move.

![Pink Moving Trucks Parked In A Chicago A 2](/images/blog/2027/12/7-things-movers-arent-allowed-to-transport/pink-moving-trucks-parked-in-a-chicago-a-2.webp)


## 7. Jewelry and Cash

High-value, easily stolen items belong in your personal vehicle, not on a truck full of boxes.
