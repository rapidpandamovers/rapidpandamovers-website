## Key Tips for Hourly Moving in December

### Book Early to Avoid the Rush

December rates stay competitive if you schedule ahead. Wait until the last week of the month, and you'll compete with everyone trying to move before their lease ends on January 1st. Doral, Kendall, and Homestead see especially high demand as families settle before school resumes.

### Have Everything Packed and Ready

This single step can cut your bill by 30% or more. Our crews charge from arrival to departure. Every minute spent waiting for boxes to be taped adds up. Stack boxes near your front door, disassemble furniture the night before, and clear walkways so movers can work efficiently.

### Consider the Weather Window

South Florida's December mornings are cooler and drier, perfect for moving. Schedule your crew for 8am to avoid afternoon humidity and holiday traffic building up on US-1 and I-95. If you're moving between Miami Beach and the mainland, factor in causeway congestion during peak hours.

### Document Your Items

Create a simple inventory before the crew arrives. Snap photos of valuable items and note their condition. Hourly movers work fast, so having this documentation ready protects both you and the moving team if questions come up later.
