The January move is done, but the work isn't over. How you approach unpacking determines whether you're living out of boxes for weeks or settling in efficiently. Whether you just moved to a Brickell high-rise or a Coral Gables family home, here's how to get organized fast.

## 1. Start with Your Essentials Box

Open your essentials box first: toiletries, phone chargers, coffee maker, paper towels, and in Miami, your portable fan or AC remote. These get you through day one.

![A Young Couple Unpacks Boxes In Their Ne 2](/images/blog/2028/01/8-ways-to-speed-up-the-unpacking-process/a-young-couple-unpacks-boxes-in-their-ne-2.webp)


## 2. Set Up Kitchen Basics Immediately

A functional kitchen means normal life can resume. Prioritize dishes, utensils, and basic cooking equipment. In Miami's heat, having your blender ready for smoothies or cafecito supplies organized is a sanity-saver.

## 3. Make Beds Early

Exhausted from unpacking? Having made beds ready means you can stop whenever you need rest.

## 4. Work Room by Room

Finish one room before starting another. Scattered, partial progress everywhere creates overwhelming chaos.

## 5. Break Down Boxes Immediately

Collapsed boxes take up less space and make rooms feel more settled. Don't let empty boxes pile up. Many Miami-Dade neighborhoods like Kendall and Doral have curbside recycling pickup for cardboard, making disposal easy.

![Couple Sitting On The Floor Unpacking Bo 1](/images/blog/2028/01/8-ways-to-speed-up-the-unpacking-process/couple-sitting-on-the-floor-unpacking-bo-1.webp)


## 6. Place Furniture First

Position large furniture before unpacking boxes. Moving furniture around boxes is frustrating and inefficient.

## 7. Organize as You Unpack

Don't just shove things in cabinets planning to organize later. You won't. Organize as you go.

## 8. Schedule Donation Pickup

Set a deadline for unpacking by scheduling a donation pickup for items you realize you don't need. Goodwill and The Salvation Army have multiple locations throughout Miami-Dade, from Aventura to Homestead, and many offer free pickup for larger donations.
