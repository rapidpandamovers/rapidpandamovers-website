Just moved to Kendall or planning your fall relocation? Food is one of the best ways to experience your new community. We've compiled this October guide to help you discover the culinary gems that make Kendall such a delicious place to call home.

## 1. Start with the Ventanitas

Those walk-up coffee windows outside Kendall restaurants serve more than cafecito. Strike up conversation with regulars—they'll tell you exactly where to eat.

![Restaurant Food Dining Guide](/images/blog/2029/10/10-best-restaurants-to-try-in-kendall-after-your-move/restaurant-food-dining-guide.webp)


## 2. Check Yelp for 'Hidden Gem' Reviews

Filter Kendall restaurant reviews for phrases like 'locals only' or 'hidden gem.' These tend to surface the places that survive on quality, not location.

## 3. Time Your Visits to Beat the Crowds

Miami eats late. Hit popular Kendall spots at 6pm instead of 8pm, or try lunch service when kitchens are less slammed and more attentive.

## 4. Follow Local Food Instagram Accounts

Search '#kendallfood' or '#kendalleats' for real-time recommendations from people who actually live there.

## 5. Don't Sleep on Strip Mall Restaurants

Some of Kendall's best restaurants hide in strip malls. Packed parking lots and no English on the menu are usually good signs.

## 6. Ask Your Server What THEY Eat

Once you find a restaurant you like in Kendall, ask staff where they eat on their days off. Restaurant workers know the best spots.

![Restaurant Food Dining Steps](/images/blog/2029/10/10-best-restaurants-to-try-in-kendall-after-your-move/restaurant-food-dining-steps.webp)


## 7. Hit Happy Hour for Budget Exploration

Many Kendall restaurants offer substantial happy hour food deals. It's a low-risk way to sample higher-end places without the full price tag.

## 8. Join a Local Supper Club or Food Group

Search Facebook for 'Kendall foodies' or 'Miami food lovers.' Group dinners help you discover restaurants AND meet your new neighbors.

## 9. Save Special Occasion Spots for Later

Once you've settled in Kendall, you'll have better context for which upscale restaurants are worth the splurge for birthdays and anniversaries.

## 10. Explore the Food Trucks

Kendall has a vibrant food truck scene. Check local social media for weekly gatherings and rotating locations featuring diverse cuisines.
