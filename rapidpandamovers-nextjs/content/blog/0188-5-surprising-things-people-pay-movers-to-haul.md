Whether you're a long-time Miami resident or just making your July move to South Florida, there's always something new to discover. Here's our curated list to help you make the most of life in the Sunshine State this summer.

## 1. Taxidermy Collections

From mounted fish to full-size bears, taxidermy requires careful handling. One Miami client had us move an entire trophy room—antlers and all.

![Funny Unusual Stories Checklist](/images/blog/2025/07/5-surprising-things-people-pay-movers-to-haul/funny-unusual-stories-checklist.webp)


## 2. Life-Size Statues

Garden statues, Buddha figures, and even custom mannequins. We've moved a 6-foot bronze dolphin that weighed over 500 pounds.

![Funny Unusual Stories Guide](/images/blog/2025/07/5-surprising-things-people-pay-movers-to-haul/funny-unusual-stories-guide.webp)


## 3. Vintage Arcade Machines

Pinball machines and classic arcade cabinets are surprisingly heavy and incredibly fragile. The glass, electronics, and wooden cabinets all need special care.

## 4. Professional Kitchen Equipment

Commercial pizza ovens, industrial mixers, and walk-in freezer components. When restaurant owners move home, their professional gear comes too.

![Funny Unusual Stories Overview](/images/blog/2025/07/5-surprising-things-people-pay-movers-to-haul/funny-unusual-stories-overview.webp)


## 5. Motorized Vehicles (Indoors)

Golf carts kept in garages, mobility scooters, and even a client's vintage motorcycle displayed in their living room. Yes, we've moved them all.
