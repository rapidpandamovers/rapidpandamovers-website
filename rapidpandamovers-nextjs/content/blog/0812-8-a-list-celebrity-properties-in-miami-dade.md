Miami attracts the rich and famous, and their homes are as impressive as you'd expect. This July, take a look at some of the most notable celebrity properties in the area—from Star Island mansions to South Beach penthouses.

## 1. Star Island Estates

This exclusive island has housed Madonna, Diddy, and Gloria Estefan—requiring discrete, high-security moving services.

![Celebrity Mansion Luxury Checklist](/images/blog/2029/07/8-a-list-celebrity-properties-in-miami-dade/celebrity-mansion-luxury-checklist.webp)


## 2. Versace Mansion (Casa Casuarina)

Gianni Versace's famous South Beach residence has seen multiple transformations and celebrity occupants.

## 3. Fisher Island Properties

Accessible only by boat, this ultra-exclusive community requires specialized logistics for any move.

## 4. Coral Gables Mansions

The historic Mediterranean-style estates in Coral Gables have housed numerous celebrities over the decades.

## 5. Miami Beach Penthouses

Luxury high-rise penthouses require coordinated elevator access and building management approval for moves.

![Celebrity Mansion Luxury Tips](/images/blog/2029/07/8-a-list-celebrity-properties-in-miami-dade/celebrity-mansion-luxury-tips.webp)


## 6. Indian Creek Island

Known as "Billionaire Bunker," this ultra-private island has housed Ivanka Trump, Tom Brady, and Jeff Bezos—with its own police force.

## 7. La Gorce Island

This exclusive Miami Beach enclave features waterfront estates that have attracted A-listers seeking privacy and luxury.

## 8. Coconut Grove Estates

The leafy, bohemian neighborhood has long attracted celebrities seeking a more laid-back Miami lifestyle with historic charm.
