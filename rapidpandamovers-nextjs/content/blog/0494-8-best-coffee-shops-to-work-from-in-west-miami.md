Finding your perfect coffee spot is one of the first steps to feeling at home in a new neighborhood. West Miami sits at the intersection of several great coffee cultures, with traditional Cuban ventanitas on Calle Ocho and specialty roasters in neighboring Coral Gables all within a 10-minute drive.

Here are eight spots where West Miami residents grab their coffee and settle in for remote work.

## 1. Latin American Bakery & Cafe

**Address:** 890 SW 57th Ave, West Miami, FL 33144

![Laptop Coffee And Notebook Setup For Rem 3](/images/blog/2027/06/8-best-coffee-shops-to-work-from-in-west-miami/laptop-coffee-and-notebook-setup-for-rem-3.webp)


Right in the heart of West Miami on 57th Avenue, this family-owned bakery serves Cuban coffee, pastries, and sandwiches. The cortadito is strong and the pastelitos are fresh from the oven. Open early mornings through late evening, making it perfect for early risers and night owls alike. Limited seating, but great for a quick caffeine boost between tasks.

## 2. Versailles Restaurant & Bakery

**Address:** 3555 SW 8th St, Miami, FL 33135

The iconic Cuban restaurant and bakery on Calle Ocho, just 5 minutes from West Miami. Their ventanita (walk-up window) serves what many consider the best cafecito in Miami. The bakery side has pastries, sandwiches, and seating. While the main dining room gets loud, the bakery area works for quick laptop sessions with legendary Cuban coffee.

## 3. Threefold Cafe

**Address:** 141 Giralda Ave, Coral Gables, FL 33134

A 7-minute drive into Coral Gables brings you to this Australian-style cafe opened in 2014 by Nick and Teresa, bringing Melbourne coffee culture to Miami. Specializes in breakfast and lunch with locally sourced ingredients. Their flat whites are excellent, and the vibe welcomes remote workers. Outdoor seating on Giralda Avenue when the weather cooperates.

## 4. The Cafe at Books & Books

**Address:** 265 Aragon Ave, Coral Gables, FL 33134

![Professional Man Drinking Coffee And Wor 4](/images/blog/2027/06/8-best-coffee-shops-to-work-from-in-west-miami/professional-man-drinking-coffee-and-wor-4.webp)


Inside the beloved independent bookstore, this cafe offers the rare combination of quality coffee, comfortable seating, and an atmosphere that practically demands productivity. Browse the shelves during breaks, grab an espresso, and settle into creative work. The literary ambiance draws writers, students, and professionals alike.

## 5. Pasion del Cielo Coffee

**Address:** 3301 Ponce de Leon Blvd, Coral Gables, FL 33134

A local roaster with multiple Miami locations, this Coral Gables spot offers carefully crafted pour-overs and espresso drinks. The beans are roasted locally, and the staff takes their craft seriously. Good WiFi, ample seating, and a steady stream of regulars who treat this as their second office.

## 6. La Colada Gourmet

**Address:** 1643 SW 8th St, Miami, FL 33135

A modern take on Cuban coffee culture along Calle Ocho, serving specialty coffee drinks alongside traditional cafecito. Better seating than most ventanitas, with WiFi and a comfortable interior. The modern aesthetic appeals to remote workers who want Cuban coffee roots with a contemporary twist.

## 7. Cafe Grumpy

**Address:** 300 Aragon Ave, Coral Gables, FL 33134

![Young Woman Working On A Laptop In An Ou 2](/images/blog/2027/06/8-best-coffee-shops-to-work-from-in-west-miami/young-woman-working-on-a-laptop-in-an-ou-2.webp)


This certified woman-owned specialty coffee shop opened its first location outside New York in Coral Gables. Beans are roasted in-house in Brooklyn and shipped fresh weekly. The minimalist space attracts a creative crowd, and the baristas know their craft. Strong WiFi and plenty of outlets.

## 8. Panther Coffee

**Address:** 2390 NW 2nd Ave, Miami, FL 33127 (Wynwood)

Worth the 15-minute drive to Wynwood for West Miami coffee lovers who want something different. Panther roasts their own beans and serves them in a converted warehouse with industrial vibes. The outdoor courtyard fills with laptops by mid-morning. Their cold brew and pour-overs draw serious coffee enthusiasts from across the county.
