Finding your perfect coffee spot is one of the first steps to feeling at home in a new neighborhood. West Miami has a diverse cafe scene perfect for summer days, from traditional Cuban ventanitas to modern specialty coffee shops.

## 1. Cacao Cafe

**Address:** 1713 W Flagler St, Miami, FL

![Coffee Shop Cafe Remote Work Overview](/images/blog/2027/06/8-best-coffee-shops-to-work-from-in-west-miami/coffee-shop-cafe-remote-work-overview.webp)


A cozy coffee and tea spot on Flagler Street serving Cuban coffee and light bites. A neighborhood favorite with authentic local flavor.

## 2. Threefold Cafe

**Address:** Coral Gables (near West Miami)

Opened in 2014 by Australians Nick and Teresa, bringing Melbourne cafe culture to Miami. Specializes in breakfast and lunch with locally sourced ingredients and excellent coffee.

## 3. Roma Bakery & Cafe

**Address:** Coral Terrace/Flagami area

An Italian bakery offering cakes, Cuban coffee, sandwiches, and wines. Famous for their Cortadito, Espresso, Cappuccino, and fresh pastries.

## 4. The Gables Coffee House

**Address:** Near Coral Gables city center

A locals' favorite serving salads, fresh-squeezed juices, sandwiches, pastries, and excellent Cuban coffee. Perfect for a working lunch.

## 5. Versailles Restaurant & Bakery

**Address:** 3555 SW 8th St, Miami, FL 33135

![Coffee Shop Cafe Remote Work Steps](/images/blog/2027/06/8-best-coffee-shops-to-work-from-in-west-miami/coffee-shop-cafe-remote-work-steps.webp)


The iconic Cuban restaurant and bakery on Calle Ocho. Their ventanita (walk-up window) serves the best cafecito in Miami, perfect for a quick coffee break.

## 6. La Colada Gourmet

**Address:** SW 8th St area, Miami

A modern take on Cuban coffee culture, serving specialty coffee drinks alongside traditional cafecito. Great WiFi and comfortable seating.

## 7. Pasion del Cielo Coffee

**Address:** Coral Way area, Miami

A local roaster with multiple locations serving carefully crafted coffee drinks. Known for their pour-overs and espresso drinks.

## 8. Cafe Grumpy

**Address:** 300 Aragon Ave, Coral Gables, FL

A certified woman-owned specialty coffee shop that opened its first location outside New York in Coral Gables. Beans roasted in-house in Brooklyn and shipped fresh weekly.
