## Key Tips for Celebrity Moving in May

### Plan Ahead for Peak Times

May can be a busy time for moving companies in Miami-Dade County. With Memorial Day activities, Art Basel preparations beginning, and seasonal transitions as snowbirds head north, scheduling your celebrity moving early ensures you get your preferred date and time. Many high-profile clients book 4-6 weeks in advance during this peak season.

### Request the same trusted team for both locations

This single step can save hours of stress and potential problems on moving day. Our experienced crews have seen what happens when this step is skipped.

### Consider the Weather

South Florida's spring weather is predictable in some ways and surprising in others. May brings daily afternoon thunderstorms, high humidity levels (often above 80%), and temperatures pushing into the 90s. Professional movers know how to protect your belongings from humidity, rain, and heat exposure during transport. For waterfront properties in Aventura, Key Biscayne, or along Biscayne Bay, additional precautions may be needed for salt air and coastal conditions.

### Prepare Your Belongings

Take time to inventory your items before the move. This is especially important for celebrity moving, as proper documentation helps ensure everything arrives safely at your new location.
