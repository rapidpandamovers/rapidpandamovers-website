Welcome to your guide for moving to Pinecrest. Whether you're relocating from Coral Gables, Kendall, or making a bigger move this fall, knowing your new community makes settling in easier.

## Why Choose Pinecrest?

Pinecrest stands out as one of Miami-Dade County's most desirable areas. The community offers an upscale residential atmosphere and is particularly known for large estate homes and lush tropical gardens.

![Loc A Scenic Aerial View Of Lake Mary Fl Sho 1](/images/blog/2026/11/pinecrest-area-guide-for-new-movers/loc-a-scenic-aerial-view-of-lake-mary-fl-sho-1.webp)


The area attracts families, professionals, and retirees alike. Top-rated schools like Palmetto Senior High draw families from across South Florida. The village maintains a strict residential zoning code, meaning no commercial development clutters the tree-lined streets.

### Location and Accessibility

Pinecrest sits along US-1 between Kendall and South Miami, giving residents direct access to both areas. Dadeland Mall is a 10-minute drive north. Downtown Miami and Brickell are about 25 minutes via US-1 or the Palmetto Expressway. For beach trips, Key Biscayne and South Beach are both within 30 minutes.

The Dadeland South and Dadeland North Metrorail stations connect Pinecrest residents to the rest of Miami-Dade without dealing with traffic.

### Community and Lifestyle

Pinecrest residents enjoy a strong sense of community. Pinecrest Gardens, the 20-acre botanical garden and former Parrot Jungle, hosts concerts, farmers markets, and family events year-round. Local parks like Coral Pine Park and Evelyn Greer Park offer tennis courts, playgrounds, and sports fields.

The fall season brings the annual Pinecrest Gardens Farmers Market every Sunday, along with holiday events through November and December.

## Neighborhoods to Consider

Pinecrest is primarily a single-family home community, but different pockets offer distinct feels.

### Eastern Pinecrest (Near US-1)

Homes here provide easier access to shopping and dining along US-1. You're minutes from The Falls shopping center and closer to Metrorail stations. Properties tend to be on smaller lots compared to western Pinecrest.

### Western Pinecrest (Near Old Cutler Road)

This area features the largest estate homes, often on half-acre to full-acre lots. Old Cutler Road runs along Fairchild Tropical Botanic Garden and offers a more secluded, heavily landscaped feel. Expect higher price points but more privacy.

### Near Pinecrest Gardens

The area surrounding Pinecrest Gardens on Red Road offers walkable access to the gardens and community events. Homes range from mid-century ranch styles to newer Mediterranean construction.

### Choosing Your Perfect Spot

Consider these factors:

- **School zones**: Pinecrest Elementary, Palmetto Middle, and Palmetto Senior High are among the most sought-after in Miami-Dade
- **Lot size preferences**: Half-acre lots in western Pinecrest vs. quarter-acre near US-1
- **Commute direction**: US-1 access for downtown, Palmetto Expressway for airport and western suburbs
- **Price range**: Homes typically start around $800K and can exceed $5M for larger estates

## Moving to Pinecrest in November

November is an excellent time for your move. Temperatures drop into the comfortable 70s and 80s, and afternoon thunderstorms become rare after the summer rainy season ends.

### Timing Your Move

When planning your relocation, consider:

- **Best moving days**: Weekdays typically have better availability and rates than weekends
- **Weather advantage**: November through April offers Miami's dry season with mild temperatures
- **Holiday schedule**: Plan around Thanksgiving if you need utilities connected or permits processed
- **HOA coordination**: Many Pinecrest communities require advance notice for moving trucks

## Essential Services to Locate

As a new Pinecrest resident, you'll want to find:

- **Healthcare**: Baptist Health South Miami Hospital is the closest major facility, about 10 minutes north on US-1
- **Schools**: Miami-Dade public schools plus private options like Westminster Christian, Gulliver, and Palmer Trinity
- **Shopping**: The Falls mall, Dadeland Mall, and the shops along US-1 through South Miami
- **Groceries**: Publix on US-1, Whole Foods at Dadeland, and specialty stores in South Miami

### What are Pinecrest's HOA rules for moving?

Most Pinecrest communities require 48-72 hours advance notice for moving trucks. Some restrict moving to weekday business hours. Check with your specific HOA before scheduling.

### Is Pinecrest good for families?

Yes. Pinecrest consistently ranks among Miami-Dade's best areas for families due to top-rated public schools, low crime rates, and family-oriented amenities like Pinecrest Gardens.
