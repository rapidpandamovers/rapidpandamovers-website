Just moved to Palmetto Bay or planning your relocation? Finding the right cafe for remote work can make all the difference in your productivity. Here are six spots in and around Palmetto Bay where you can settle in with your laptop, grab great coffee, and get work done.

## 1. Starbucks - Falls Shopping Center

**Address:** 7290 SW 168th St, Palmetto Bay, FL 33157

![A Cozy Workspace Featuring An Iced Coffe 4](/images/blog/2028/07/6-top-cafes-for-remote-workers-in-palmetto-bay/a-cozy-workspace-featuring-an-iced-coffe-4.webp)


This Starbucks location near The Falls offers reliable wifi, plenty of outlets along the walls, and comfortable seating. Open daily from 5am to 9pm, it's ideal for early morning sessions before the afternoon crowds arrive. The location has both indoor seating and covered outdoor tables for those days when Miami weather cooperates.

## 2. The Glasshouse Cafe by Le Basque

**Address:** 10901 Old Cutler Rd, Coral Gables, FL 33156 (inside Fairchild Tropical Botanic Garden)

Located just north of Palmetto Bay inside Fairchild Tropical Botanic Garden, this cafe offers a unique work environment surrounded by tropical plants. Open Tuesday through Sunday from 10:30am to 4pm. The garden setting provides natural light and a calm atmosphere that helps with focus. Note: garden admission may be required.

## 3. First Watch

**Address:** 14851 S Dixie Hwy, Palmetto Bay, FL 33176

![Full Length Joyful African American Fema 1](/images/blog/2028/07/6-top-cafes-for-remote-workers-in-palmetto-bay/full-length-joyful-african-american-fema-1.webp)


While primarily a breakfast and brunch spot, First Watch welcomes laptop workers during off-peak hours. The spacious booths and free wifi make it comfortable for morning work sessions. Arrive after the 9am breakfast rush or during the mid-afternoon lull for the best experience. Their fresh juice options and healthy breakfast menu fuel productive mornings.

## 4. Brewing Buddha Cafe & Arthouse

**Address:** 20315 Old Cutler Rd, Cutler Bay, FL 33189

Just south of Palmetto Bay in Cutler Bay, this local favorite combines a coffee shop with an art gallery atmosphere. The eclectic vibe, specialty coffee drinks, and community feel make it a standout for creative professionals. They offer good wifi and comfortable indoor and outdoor seating. Check their hours as they sometimes host evening events.

## 5. Starbucks - US-1 Location

**Address:** 14795 S Dixie Hwy, Palmetto Bay, FL 33176

Another reliable option on the US-1 corridor, this Starbucks offers the standard amenities remote workers need: wifi, outlets, mobile ordering, and consistent hours. The drive-through means the inside stays quieter than some locations. It's convenient if you're running errands between work sessions.

## 6. Pinecrest Bakery

**Address:** Multiple locations near Palmetto Bay, including 12101 S Dixie Hwy, Pinecrest, FL 33156

![Refreshing Iced Coffee Placed On A Woode 2](/images/blog/2028/07/6-top-cafes-for-remote-workers-in-palmetto-bay/refreshing-iced-coffee-placed-on-a-woode-2.webp)


A Miami institution, Pinecrest Bakery offers Cuban coffee, fresh pastries, and breakfast combos at reasonable prices. While not specifically designed for remote work, the casual atmosphere and quick service work well for shorter work sessions. Grab a cafe con leche and a pastelito de guayaba while answering emails.

## Tips for Working from Cafes in Palmetto Bay

- **Arrive early**: Beat the lunch crowds by settling in before 10am
- **Respect the space**: Order something every couple of hours if you're staying long
- **Bring a backup**: Mobile hotspot helps when cafe wifi gets crowded
- **Scout outlets**: Arrive with a charged laptop and identify power sources early
