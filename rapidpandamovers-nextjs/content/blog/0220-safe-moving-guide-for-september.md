## Key Tips for Safe Moving in September

### Plan Ahead for Peak Times

September can be a busy time for moving companies. With fall moving season activities and seasonal transitions, scheduling your safe moving early ensures you get your preferred date and time.

### Know the exact weight of your safe before moving day for proper equipment

This single step can save hours of stress and potential problems on moving day. Our experienced crews have seen what happens when this step is skipped.

### Consider the Weather

South Florida's September weather means hurricane season is still active, with afternoon thunderstorms rolling in from the Everglades almost daily. From Brickell condos to Coral Gables estates, professional movers know how to protect your belongings from humidity, rain, and heat exposure during transport. Take time to inventory your items before the move. This is especially important for safe moving, as proper documentation helps ensure everything arrives safely at your new location.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How heavy are typical gun safes?**
Gun safes range from 200 pounds for smaller models to over 2,000 pounds for large vault-style safes. We need to know the weight in advance to bring the right equipment and crew size.

**Can you move a safe upstairs or into a basement?**
Yes. Our crews use specialized stair-climbing dollies and rigging equipment for multi-level moves. We assess the path during the quote process to ensure safe access.

**Do I need to empty my safe before moving?**
Yes. Remove all contents before moving day. This reduces weight, prevents damage to items inside, and allows movers to focus on transporting the safe itself.
