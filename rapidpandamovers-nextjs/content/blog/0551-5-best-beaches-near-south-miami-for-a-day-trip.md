Living near South Miami means easy access to some of Florida's best beaches, perfect for fall outings. Whether you're looking for family-friendly shores, water sports, or quiet relaxation, here are the coastal destinations worth your time.

## 1. Matheson Hammock Park Beach

**Address:** 9610 Old Cutler Rd, Miami, FL 33156

![Beach Ocean Day Trip Checklist](/images/blog/2027/11/5-best-beaches-near-south-miami-for-a-day-trip/beach-ocean-day-trip-checklist.webp)


Just minutes from South Miami, this 630-acre park features a man-made atoll pool naturally flushed by Biscayne Bay tides. Safe for swimmers of all ages. Rent kayaks, paddleboards, or canoes at the marina.

## 2. Crandon Park Beach

**Address:** 6747 Crandon Blvd, Key Biscayne, FL 33149

A locals' favorite with postcard-perfect views. Features umbrella rentals, a carousel, skatepark, and beachside restaurants. Calm, shallow waters ideal for families with children.

## 3. Bill Baggs Cape Florida State Park

**Address:** 1200 Crandon Blvd, Key Biscayne, FL 33149

Home to the historic Cape Florida Lighthouse from 1825. Climb 109 steps for panoramic bay views. Wide beaches consistently ranked among the nation's best. Kayaking and cycling available.

## 4. Virginia Key Beach Park

**Address:** 4020 Virginia Beach Dr, Miami, FL 33149

![Beach Ocean Day Trip Steps](/images/blog/2027/11/5-best-beaches-near-south-miami-for-a-day-trip/beach-ocean-day-trip-steps.webp)


A historic beach with calm waters and a vintage carousel. Mountain bike trails wind through the park. A quieter alternative to crowded South Beach, perfect for families.

## 5. Hobie Island Beach Park

**Address:** Rickenbacker Causeway, Key Biscayne, FL

Miami's premier spot for water sports with shallow waters ideal for windsurfing, paddleboarding, and kayaking. One of the few dog-friendly beaches in the area.
