After years of October moves in Miami, we've seen it all. Some items present unique challenges that require specialized knowledge and equipment. Here are the strangest things professional movers have been asked to transport.

## 1. Grand Pianos

These massive instruments require specialized equipment, multiple people, and expertise to avoid damage to the piano and property.

![Funny Unusual Stories Checklist](/images/blog/2029/10/6-weird-things-people-have-asked-movers-to-move/funny-unusual-stories-checklist.webp)


## 2. Wine Collections

Temperature sensitivity, fragile bottles, and proper positioning require climate-controlled handling and custom crating.

## 3. Hot Tubs and Spas

Draining, disconnecting, and moving these heavy items requires professionals familiar with the process.

![Funny Unusual Stories Planning](/images/blog/2029/10/6-weird-things-people-have-asked-movers-to-move/funny-unusual-stories-planning.webp)


## 4. Large Aquariums

Fish need separate transport; tanks must be drained, carefully padded, and moved level to prevent cracking.

## 5. Pool Tables

Slate tops require disassembly by specialists. Improperly moved pool tables never play the same.

![Funny Unusual Stories Steps](/images/blog/2029/10/6-weird-things-people-have-asked-movers-to-move/funny-unusual-stories-steps.webp)


## 6. Fine Art and Sculptures

Custom crating, climate control, and white-glove handling protect valuable artwork during transit.
