Welcome to your guide for moving to Golden Beach! As one of South Florida's most exclusive residential communities, Golden Beach offers unparalleled privacy and luxury oceanfront living.

## About Golden Beach

Golden Beach is an ultra-exclusive town of approximately 977 residents, making it one of the smallest incorporated municipalities in Miami-Dade County. Situated on 1.8 miles along State Road A1A at the Miami-Dade/Broward County line, this prestigious community has maintained its character since incorporation in 1929.

![Elegant Villa On A Spanish Hillside Surr 2](/images/blog/2026/02/welcome-to-golden-beach-a-moving-guide/elegant-villa-on-a-spanish-hillside-surr-2.webp)


### What Makes Golden Beach Special

- **No Commercial Development**: The Town Charter forbids commercial activity and high-rise construction
- **Single-Family Only**: Approximately 400 single-family homes, no condominiums
- **Celebrity Residents**: Past and present residents include Paul Newman, Ricky Martin, Eric Clapton, Tommy Hilfiger, Bill Gates, and Grant Cardone
- **Private Beach**: 1.5 miles of private oceanfront beach for residents only

### Demographics at a Glance

- **Population**: Approximately 977 residents
- **Median Age**: 46.2 years
- **Average Household Income**: $406,247
- **Median Household Income**: $250,001+
- **Foreign-Born Residents**: 48% of population

## Real Estate Market

Golden Beach is one of the most expensive residential markets in the United States.

- **Entry-Level Pricing**: Starting around $3.9 million
- **Median Sale Price**: $6-18 million depending on the period
- **Top-End Properties**: Up to $68 million
- **Average Days on Market**: 160-292 days
- **Total Homes**: Approximately 400 single-family residences

Properties feature direct oceanfront access, private docks on the Intracoastal Waterway, and substantial lot sizes uncommon in South Florida.

## Location and Boundaries

Golden Beach is bordered by:
- **East**: Atlantic Ocean
- **West**: Intracoastal Waterway
- **North**: Hallandale Beach (Broward County)
- **South**: Sunny Isles Beach

![Idyllic Luxury Beachfront Resort With Pa 1](/images/blog/2026/02/welcome-to-golden-beach-a-moving-guide/idyllic-luxury-beachfront-resort-with-pa-1.webp)


This location provides easy access to both Miami-Dade and Broward County amenities while maintaining complete residential privacy.

## Schools and Education

While Golden Beach has no schools within its boundaries, residents have access to:
- Miami-Dade County Public Schools to the south
- Broward County Public Schools to the north
- Numerous prestigious private schools in the area

The town's high-income demographics mean most families choose private education options.

## Amenities and Lifestyle

### Within Golden Beach
- Private beach access

### Nearby Conveniences
Since Golden Beach prohibits commercial development, residents access shopping and dining in:

- **Bal Harbour Shops**: Luxury shopping (5 minutes)
- **Sunny Isles Beach**: Restaurants and services (adjacent)
- **Hallandale Beach**: Gulfstream Park, dining options (adjacent)

## Moving to Golden Beach: Special Considerations

### Security and Access
Golden Beach has 24-hour police patrol and controlled access points. Moving companies must be pre-approved and scheduled with town administration.

![Top Down View Of A Beachfront Mansion Wi 4](/images/blog/2026/02/welcome-to-golden-beach-a-moving-guide/top-down-view-of-a-beachfront-mansion-wi-4.webp)


### Moving Truck Regulations
Contact the Golden Beach Police Department to coordinate moving dates and obtain necessary permits for large vehicles.

Most Golden Beach moves involve substantial estates requiring:
- Multiple moving trucks
- Specialized handling for artwork and antiques
- White-glove service for luxury furnishings
- Climate-controlled transport for sensitive items
